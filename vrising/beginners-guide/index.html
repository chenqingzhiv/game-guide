    🩸 V Rising: Complete Beginner's Guide — First 50 Hours

    Developer: Stunlock Studios | Publisher: Stunlock Studios
    Steam Price: $29.99 | Current Version: 1.0+ (Post-Early Access)
    Estimated Playtime: 30–50 hours to defeat all 4 Gear Score (GS) bosses
    Player Types: Solo PvE / Clan PvE / PvP Servers

    Overview

    V Rising is a vampire survival action RPG that blends the base-building depth of ARK with the boss-progression system of Valheim and the action combat of Diablo. You awaken as a weakened vampire in the gothic realm of Vardoran, trapped in a coffin with nothing but your bare hands and a fading blood curse.

    The learning curve is steep. You need to manage blood quality, castle heart levels, sun exposure, and gear score progression — all while hunting increasingly powerful bosses. Where do you start? What should you craft first? Which bosses should you fight and in what order?

    This guide covers the first 50 hours of V Rising, broken into clear phases with exact gear requirements, boss order, resource priorities, and time estimates.

    Phase 1: Awakening — First 2 Hours

    Goal: Survive your first night, establish a basic shelter, and craft your first weapon.

    Step 1: The Coffin Moment

    You spawn in a wooden coffin in the Farbane Woods. When you break out:

    1. It's daytime. You'll take sun damage rapidly. Find shade under trees immediately.
    2. You have no gear. Your basic attacks deal minimal damage.
    3. Your blood quality is 0%. You're essentially a level 1 character.

    Immediate priorities (first 30 minutes): - Find wood (hit trees with bare hands — 20–30 hits per tree) - Find iron ore (grey rock nodes scattered in Farbane Woods) - Build a Workbench (20 wood) — this is your first crafting station - Build a Smelter (20 wood + 20 iron ore) — to process iron

    Step 2: Your First Gear Set

    At the Workbench, craft in this order:

    Item Materials Purpose
    Wooden Club 10 wood Basic melee weapon (20 DPS)
    Iron Sword 20 iron ingots First real weapon (80 DPS)
    Iron Armor Set 60 iron ingots total First armor set (30% damage reduction)
    Leather Boots 10 leather Movement speed +10%

    💡 Blood drinking: Kill wolves and bandits in Farbane Woods. Drink their blood to raise your blood quality. Target 10–20% blood quality before attempting anything harder. Each blood type gives different buffs: - Wolf blood — increased damage - Bandit blood — increased stamina recovery - Drover blood — increased movement speed

    Step 3: The First Night

    When sunset approaches (the sky turns orange-red), you have two options:

    Option Pros Cons
    Stay in a wooden shelter Safe, no travel risk Vulnerable to night creatures
    Travel to a cave Natural shade, safe from sun Limited resources, may contain enemies
    Build a cloak Lets you travel at night Requires crafting materials you may not have yet

    Recommendation: Build a simple 3×3 wooden shelter with a door. Sleep in it at night (right-click bed). This is your safest option for the first 3 nights.

    ⚠️ Sunlight kills you. There is no workaround until you craft a Cloak of the Bat (requires GS100 boss drops). If you're caught in daylight without shade, you have ~15 seconds before you die.

    🎯 Milestone: You have iron gear, 10–20% blood quality, and a basic shelter. Total playtime: 1–2 hours.

    Phase 2: Castle Heart & Resource Gathering (2–10 Hours)

    Goal: Establish your first castle, unlock the Castle Heart system, and begin targeting your first boss.

    The Castle Heart — Your Base's Core

    The Castle Heart is the most important building in V Rising. It serves three functions:

    1. Defines your castle boundary — other players/creatures can't damage structures outside it
    2. Stores your inventory — access items from anywhere inside your castle
    3. Tracks your gear score (GS) — determines which bosses you can fight

    How to build it: 1. Defeat Renowned Arthur (GS100 boss in Farbane Woods) — he drops the Castle Heart Blueprint 2. Craft the Castle Heart at a Machining Table (100 wood + 100 stone + 20 iron) 3. Place it in your base

    💡 Arthur is your first real boss. He's located near the Dawn's Isle in the eastern Farbane Woods. He's a knight in heavy armor with a sword and shield. Bring iron armor and an iron sword. Fight him at night near water (he has reduced mobility near cliffs).

    Castle Heart Levels

    Your Castle Heart levels up as you gather resources and defeat bosses:

    Castle Level Requirements Unlocks
    Level 1 Castle Heart placed Basic building, iron gear
    Level 2 500 wood + 500 stone Stone gear recipes
    Level 3 2000 wood + 2000 stone + 500 iron Blood Bank, better storage
    Level 4 5000 wood + 5000 stone + 2000 iron Castle teleport, blood moon resistance
    Level 5 15000 wood + 15000 stone + 5000 iron + GS boss Max castle level

    Resource gathering strategy: - Wood: Chop trees near your castle. A single oak tree yields 50–100 wood. - Stone: Mine stone nodes (common in Farbane Woods and Dunley Farmlands). - Iron: Smelt iron ore (found in caves and surface deposits). - Silver: Mined from silver ore nodes (unlocked at Castle Level 3).

    The Blood Bank — Your Inventory Hub

    The Blood Bank (unlocked at Castle Level 3) is essential for: - Storing blood vials (your HP pool is tied to blood quality) - Managing blood type rotation (different blood types for different situations) - Accessing stored items from anywhere in your castle

    Blood type rotation strategy: - Day hunting: Use Drover blood (speed) or Bandit blood (stamina) - Boss fights: Use Wolf blood (damage) or Bat blood (lifesteal) - PvP: Use Ghoul blood (life steal on hit) or Minotaur blood (rage mode)

    🎯 Milestone: Castle Heart built, Castle Level 2+, and you're grinding resources for your first GS200 boss. Total playtime: 5–10 hours.

    Phase 3: First Bosses & Gear Progression (10–20 Hours)

    Goal: Defeat the first 4 bosses, reach GS400, and unlock endgame-tier resources.

    Boss Progression Order

    V Rising uses a Gear Score (GS) system. Each boss has a minimum GS requirement. Fight them in this exact order:

    Boss Location GS Requirement Key Drop Blood Type
    Renowned Arthur Dawn's Isle, Farbane Woods GS100 Castle Heart Blueprint —
    King Marko Dunley Farmlands GS200 Iron Pickaxe upgrade Bat Blood
    Therese, the Rotting Dunley Farmlands (swamp area) GS250 Silver ore access Hag Blood
    Sir Orvan Silverlight Hills GS300 Castle Heart Level 3 recipe Moonstone Blood
    Bloodmoon King Cursed Forest GS350 Cloak of the Bat Bloodmoon Blood
    Quinlan Silverlight Hills (mountain) GS400 Castle Heart Level 4 recipe Golem Blood

    💡 Fight timing: Always fight bosses at night. Daylight gives you sun damage on top of boss damage. Bring healing potions and lifesteal gear.

    Gear Tiers and Crafting

    Each boss defeat unlocks a new gear tier:

    Gear Tier Materials Damage Reduction Where to Craft
    Iron Iron ingots 30% Workbench
    Silver Silver ingots 40% Machining Table
    Moonstone Moonstone + Silver 50% Moonstone Forge
    Bloodstone Bloodstone + Moonstone 60% Bloodstone Crucible
    Nightfall Nightfall Ore + Bloodstone 70% Nightfall Anvil

    Crafting priority: Always craft weapons before armor. A higher DPS weapon lets you kill bosses faster, which means faster gear progression.

    Resource Farming Between Bosses

    While preparing for your next boss, farm these resources:

    Resource Best Farming Location Yield per Node
    Silver Ore Dunley Farmlands (cave entrances) 10–20 per node
    Moonstone Silverlight Hills (surface deposits) 5–15 per node
    Bloodstone Cursed Forest (boss drops + rare nodes) 3–10 per node
    Blood Vials Kill enemies near your castle 1–5 per enemy

    ⚠️ Don't skip the Cursed Forest. It's where you get Bloodmoon Blood (the best offensive blood type) and where the Bloodmoon King drops the Cloak of the Bat (lets you walk in daylight). This is a critical milestone.

    The Cloak of the Bat — Game Changer

    The Cloak of the Bat (dropped by Bloodmoon King, GS350) is arguably the most important item in V Rising:

    • Lets you walk in daylight without sun damage
    • Unlocks day-time boss hunting — significantly faster resource gathering
    • Required for Castle Heart Level 5

    Without this cloak, you're limited to nighttime gameplay. With it, the entire world opens up.

    🎯 Milestone: Castle Level 3+, Cloak of the Bat equipped, and you can now hunt bosses during the day. Total playtime: 15–20 hours.

    Phase 4: Endgame Preparation (20–35 Hours)

    Goal: Reach GS600, defeat the remaining bosses, and prepare for endgame content.

    The Second Half of Boss Progression

    After defeating Quinlan (GS400), the difficulty spikes. These bosses require significant preparation:

    Boss Location GS Requirement Special Mechanics
    Ghoul King Cursed Forest GS450 Summons ghouls, lifesteal aura
    Cain, the Undying Farbane Woods (ruins) GS500 Regenerates HP, poison clouds
    Alucard Castle Moravia GS550 Teleports, summons bats
    Dracula Castle Dracula GS600 Multiple phases, area denial

    Endgame Resource Preparation

    Before tackling GS450+ bosses, you need:

    1. Full Nightfall Gear — 70% damage reduction, essential for surviving boss mechanics
    2. Bloodmoon Blood at 100% — maximum offensive buff
    3. Ghoul Blood at 100% — maximum lifesteal for sustained boss fights
    4. Castle Heart Level 5 — unlocks endgame crafting stations

    Castle Heart Level 5 Requirements

    Requirement Details
    Resources 50,000 wood + 50,000 stone + 20,000 iron + 5,000 silver + 1,000 bloodstone
    Boss drops Cloak of the Bat + 2 GS300+ boss drops
    Crafting stations Nightfall Anvil + Bloodstone Crucible + Moonstone Forge

    💡 Resource farming tip: Build a dedicated iron farm. Place 10+ smelters around iron ore clusters and use PLS (Planetary Logistics Stations) to auto-collect. One person can produce 500+ iron ingots/hour with proper setup.

    Blood Quality Optimization

    By this stage, your blood quality should be optimized for different scenarios:

    Scenario Best Blood Type Target Quality
    Boss fighting Bloodmoon 100%
    PvP Ghoul 100%
    Resource gathering Drover 50–70% (speed over power)
    Exploration Bat 50% (lifesteal for safety)

    Switch blood types using the Blood Bank before each activity. Don't fight bosses with low-quality blood — it's the #1 reason new players fail at GS500+.

    🎯 Milestone: Nightfall gear, Castle Heart Level 5, and all blood types at 80%+. Total playtime: 25–35 hours.

    Phase 5: Endgame & Castle Building Mastery (35–50 Hours)

    Goal: Defeat Dracula, unlock the full endgame tech tree, and build a fortress.

    Defeating Dracula — The Final Boss

    Castle Dracula is located in the Dread Sea (the easternmost region of Vardoran). It's a massive castle complex with multiple floors, traps, and elite enemies.

    Preparation checklist before entering: - [ ] Full Nightfall armor set (70% damage reduction) - [ ] Nightfall weapon (minimum 800 DPS) - [ ] Bloodmoon blood at 100% - [ ] At least 50 healing potions - [ ] Castle Heart Level 5 - [ ] Night cloak (allows night travel without sun penalty)

    Dracula fight phases: 1. Phase 1: Standard melee combat — use lifesteal attacks 2. Phase 2: Summons minions — kite them while focusing Dracula 3. Phase 3: Area denial (fire pools, teleportation) — stay mobile

    Estimated fight time: 5–15 minutes depending on gear quality.

    Endgame Content

    After defeating Dracula:

    Activity Description Time Investment
    PvP Servers Raid other players' castles Unlimited
    Bloodmoon Ritual Weekly event with special rewards 1–2 hours/week
    Castle Customization Decorate with 200+ furniture pieces Hours
    Bloodline Perks Unlock passive bonuses for your blood type 5–10 hours
    Runic Magic Ancient vampire spells with devastating effects 10–15 hours

    Castle Building Tips

    V Rising's building system is deep. Here are the most impactful tips:

    1. Build your Castle Heart first — everything radiates outward from it
    2. Create separate rooms — bedroom (rest), blood bank (storage), workshop (crafting), armory (gear)
    3. Use decorative elements — torches, banners, and statues boost castle mood (indirectly improves blood quality gain)
    4. Build walls with gates — control access points. Enemy raids can breach unguarded walls.
    5. Place your castle near resources — silver mines, bloodstone deposits, and boss spawn points

    💡 Pro builder tip: Build your castle on a hill or cliff edge. Elevated positions give you range advantage in PvP and make raiding harder for enemies.

    🎯 Completion: You've defeated Dracula and unlocked the full endgame. Total playtime: 40–50 hours.

    Player Type Strategies

    Zero-Spend (Free Player)

    Aspect Approach
    Gear Stick to iron → silver → moonstone. Skip bloodstone and nightfall.
    Bosses Focus on the first 4 bosses (Arthur through Quinlan).
    Time investment 15–20 hours to reach meaningful endgame.
    Limitation Cannot access the full boss roster without paid DLC.

    Standard Player ($29.99)

    Aspect Approach
    Gear Full progression to Nightfall tier.
    Bosses All bosses including Dracula.
    Time investment 30–50 hours for complete progression.
    Advantage All gear tiers, all blood types, all castles.

    Reclusive PvE Player

    Aspect Approach
    Server type Solo or private PvE server
    Focus Castle building, blood quality optimization, boss hunting
    PvP Avoid entirely. Focus on PvE progression.
    Time investment 50–80 hours (slower due to no PvP resource rushes)

    Clan PvP Player

    Aspect Approach
    Server type Public PvP server
    Focus Raiding, territory control, resource monopolization
    Strategy Hit weaker players, steal their resources, expand castle boundaries
    Time investment 20–40 hours to reach competitive gear (rushed progression)

    Common Beginner Mistakes

    Mistake Impact Fix
    Hunting in daylight without a cloak Instant death Always check sky color before leaving shelter
    Fighting bosses below recommended GS Wasted resources, repeated deaths Wait until you meet GS requirement + 20% buffer
    Spending blood quality on one type Vulnerable to different situations Rotate blood types based on activity
    Building castle too far from resources Hours of inefficient travel Place castle near silver/bloodstone deposits
    Neglecting healing potions Boss fights become impossible Craft 20+ potions before every boss fight
    Not upgrading Castle Heart Locked out of new gear tiers Prioritize Castle Heart leveling over weapon crafting

    Quick Reference: Gear Score Progression

    GS0–100  → Bare hands + wooden gear    (Farbane Woods)
GS100    → Arthur defeated             (Castle Heart unlocked)
GS200    → King Marko defeated         (Iron → Silver transition)
GS250    → Therese defeated            (Silver ore access)
GS300    → Sir Orvan defeated          (Moonstone gear)
GS350    → Bloodmoon King defeated     (Cloak of the Bat — DAYLIGHT!)
GS400    → Quinlan defeated            (Bloodstone gear)
GS450    → Ghoul King defeated         (Nightfall materials)
GS500    → Cain defeated               (Endgame preparation)
GS550    → Alucard defeated            (Final boss approach)
GS600    → Dracula defeated            (Endgame unlocked)
