    🏰 V Rising — Castle Building Guide

    Last updated: July 2026 | Game version: 1.1 Gloomrot

    Castle building is at the heart of V Rising. Your castle is more than just a shelter — it's your base of operations, defense network, resource hub, and status symbol. This guide covers everything from your first stone walls to impenetrable fortress design, servant management, and the most strategic build locations on the map.

    Castle System Overview

    Feature Description
    Castle Heart Core of your territory — determines size and upgrades
    Building Materials Wood → Stone → Iron → Dark Silver tiers
    Rooms & Functions Each room type serves a specific purpose
    Servants Captured humans defend and gather for you
    Garden System Grow blood roses, herbs, and cotton
    Defenses Walls, gates, traps, and castle siege mechanics
    Fast Travel Teleporters and castle portals
    Decorations 200+ furniture items for aesthetics and mood

    💖 Castle Heart — The Core

    The Castle Heart is your most important structure. It defines your buildable territory, stores your items, and determines your castle level.

    How to Unlock

    Step Requirement
    1 Defeat Renowned Arthur (GS100, Farbane Woods)
    2 Craft Castle Heart at Machining Table (100 Wood + 100 Stone + 20 Iron Ingot)
    3 Place it in your desired location

    Castle Heart Levels

    Level Radius Unlock Cost Unlocks Servant Capacity
    1 8 tiles 100 Wood + 100 Stone Basic building (Wood), Simple Floor/Wall 0
    2 12 tiles 500 Wood + 500 Stone + 100 Planks Stone building, Stone Floor/Wall 2
    3 18 tiles 2000 Wood + 2000 Stone + 500 Iron Dark Silver building, Servant Coffins 4
    4 24 tiles 5000 Wood + 5000 Stone + 2000 Iron + 500 Reinforced Planks Imperial building, Castle Teleporter 6
    5 32 tiles 15000 Wood + 15000 Stone + 5000 Iron + 1000 Dark Silver + 500 Gold Max level, all building types 8

    Castle Heart Placement Guide

    Location Advantage Disadvantage
    Flat ground Easy building, symmetrical layout Exposed to raids
    Cliff edge Natural defense on one side Limited expansion direction
    Hilltop Good visibility, hard to siege Awkward building angles
    Cave Natural protection, hidden Dark, limited space
    Near water Easy blood rose farming Flood risk (cosmetic only)
    Forest clearing Abundant wood nearby Enemies approach from all directions

    💡 Tip: You can relocate your Castle Heart later, but the cost scales with level. Pick your main base location carefully — Level 5 relocation costs 10,000+ resources.

    Multi-Floor Design

    Floor Purpose Rooms
    Ground Work + defense Workshop, Alchemy, Storage, Blood Bank, Prison
    Second Living + servants Servant Quarters, Library, Kitchen, Garden (rooftop)
    Third Prestige Throne Room, Trophy Hall, Blood Altar
    Basement Secret/secure Emergency storage, escape tunnel, backup Castle Heart

    Room Efficiency Tips

    1. Adjacent bonuses: Place the Blood Bank next to the Prison — easier blood extraction
    2. Crafting clusters: Workshop + Alchemy + Kitchen should be adjacent (common material flow)
    3. Servant paths: Servants walk between their coffin and the entrance — keep paths clear
    4. Vertical integration: Storage on ground floor, crafting on first floor, living above
    5. Garden placement: South-facing (in-game) gets more light for rose growth

    Farming Tips

    1. Blood Rose is your #1 crop — you need it for every healing potion
    2. Harvest with Worker blood — increases yield by 15–75%

    4. Automated watering — place Garden Soil next to a water source (no manual watering needed)
    5. Expand gradually — start with 10 Blood Rose plants, scale to 30+

    🛡️ Castle Defense

    Wall & Gate Defense

    Defensive Structure HP Upgrade Cost Effectiveness
    Wooden Wall 200 4 Planks Low — tier 1, easily broken
    Stone Wall 500 4 Stone Bricks Medium — standard defense
    Iron-reinforced Wall 800 4 Stone Bricks + 2 Iron Good — requires siege equipment
    Imperial Wall 1200 4 Stone Bricks + 4 Dark Silver Excellent — endgame defense
    Wooden Gate 300 8 Planks + 4 Iron Low — castle entrance
    Stone Gate 750 8 Stone Bricks + 6 Iron Good — main gate
    Imperial Gate 2000 8 Stone Bricks + 10 Dark Silver + 4 Gold Best — nearly unbreakable

    Defense Strategies by Server Type

    PvE Server

    Defense Purpose Notes
    Walls Keep wandering enemies out 1-tile-thick stone is sufficient
    Gates Control access One main gate, one emergency exit
    Outer perimeter Mark territory Decorative palisade or hedge
    Recommended: Stone walls + stone gate. No traps needed.

    PvP Server

    Defense Purpose Notes
    Double walls Time-delay raiders 2-tile-thick Imperial walls
    Multiple gates Bypass bottleneck Build 2–3 gates, not 1
    Honeycomb rooms Waste siege explosives Small rooms with internal walls
    False doors Confuse raiders Decorative doors leading to walls
    Fallback shelter Last stand Small reinforced room with Castle Heart
    Traps (explosive) Area denial Place behind doors, on stairs
    Recommended: Imperial double walls, 3 gates, honeycomb interior.

    Siege Mechanics

    Siege Weapon Purpose HP Damage Charge Time Counter
    Ram Breach gates 50/sec 3s charge Attack the operators
    Catapult Destroy walls 200 per hit 8s reload Destroy it, or repair walls
    Explosive Charge Blow through walls 500 5s fuse Defuse (if you reach it in time)
    Trebuchet Long-range siege 300 per hit 12s reload Hard to counter (roof helps)

    Anti-Siege Tips

    1. Build roofs on everything — catapults and trebuchets can't target covered areas
    2. Keep backup materials — 500+ stone bricks for emergency repair mid-raid
    3. Multiple Castle Hearts — place a secondary heart in a hidden location as a fallback
    4. Alarm system — place bells near each entrance to alert you of breaches
    5. Perimeter walls should have crenellations — you can shoot over them; enemies can't

    🧛 Servant System

    Capturing Servants

    Step Action Requirements
    1 Find a human NPC Villagers, bandits, soldiers
    2 Weaken them (below 25% HP) Any weapon
    3 Use Charm ability Unlocked from V Blood carrier
    4 Transport them to your castle Must be done before they wake up
    5 Place them in a Servant Coffin Craft: 20 Planks + 5 Iron
    6 Assign them a role From Castle Heart menu

    Converted Servant Stats by Original NPC Type

    NPC Type Combat Rating Resource Gathering Speed Best Role
    Villager 10 30% Normal Farming, gathering
    Bandit 30 15% Fast Combat, defense
    Soldier 50 10% Slow Defense, PvP
    Militia 40 20% Medium All-rounder
    Rogue 35 25% Very Fast Scouting, resource runs
    Mage 60 5% Slow Defense (AOE damage)
    Priest 45 15% Slow Healing, support
    V Blood NPC 80+ 30% Variable Best-in-slot (very rare)

    Servant Roles

    Role Function Stat Priority
    Guard Patrols castle, attacks intruders Combat
    Resource Gatherer Collects specified resources Gathering
    Defender Stationary guard at a specific spot Combat
    Scavenger Collects random materials outside Gathering + Speed

    Servant Equipment

    Equipment Slot Item Types Stat Effects
    Weapon Sword, Axe, Mace, Staff +Damage
    Armor Cloth, Leather, Plate +HP, +Defense
    Accessory Ring, Amulet +Speed, +Gathering
    Special Blood Rose (consumable) +Healing in combat

    Servant Leveling

    Level XP Required Sources
    1 → 10 1000 Patrol kills, gathering missions
    10 → 20 5000 Combat, rare resource runs
    20 → 30 15000 V Blood raids, PvP defense
    30 → Max 50000 Endgame content, raids

    Servant Tips

    1. Rotate servants — send 2 on missions while 2 rest (cooldown: 2 hours real time)
    2. Equip your best gear on servants — a well-equipped servant is worth 3 ungeared ones
    3. Mages make the best defenders — their AOE attacks hit multiple intruders
    4. Villagers are underrated — they gather 30% faster than soldiers
    5. Don't neglect prison happiness — unhappy servants have lower stats

    🚪 Fast Travel & Teleporters

    Castle Teleporter

    Unlocked at Castle Heart Level 4. Allows instant travel between your castles:

    Feature Detail
    Unlock Castle Heart Lv. 4 + 100 Dark Silver + 50 Gold
    Recipe Machining Table: 50 Stone Bricks + 20 Dark Silver + 10 Gears
    Range Unlimited (any castle you own)
    Cooldown 30 seconds
    Max connections 3 teleporters per castle

    Waygate Network

    Waygates are public fast travel points found throughout Vardoran:

    Waygate Location Region Nearest Castle Spot
    Farbane Woods Crossroads Farbane Woods Central — good for first castle
    Dunley Farmlands Crossroads Dunley Farmlands Near iron deposits
    Silverlight Hills Gate Silverlight Hills Near silver nodes
    Cursed Forest Entrance Cursed Forest Protected by trees
    Gloomrot South Waygate Gloomrot South Tech resources

    Fast Travel Rules (PvP Server)

    Rule Detail
    Can teleport to any owned castle Always allowed
    Can teleport from owned castle to waygate Always allowed
    Can teleport with items Not allowed on most PvP servers
    Teleporter cooldown 30s after use
    Enemy can destroy teleporter Yes (if they breach your castle)

Cons: Far from starting area, limited horizontal space
Build strategy: Vertical tower design, 5 floors

    Common Castle Building Mistakes

    Mistake Impact Fix
    Building too large too fast Waste of resources, half-finished castle Start with essential rooms, expand outward
    Castle Heart in exposed location Easy to destroy in raids Place it in the center, on the second floor
    No servant quarters early Miss free resource gathering Build servant quarters at Castle Heart Lv. 2
    Ignoring garden Expensive potion costs Plant 10+ Blood Roses immediately
    Single entrance Easy to camp/bottleneck Build 2–3 gates, multiple exits
    Wood walls past early game Easily destroyed Upgrade to Stone at Castle Heart Lv. 2, Iron at Lv. 3
    No roof Catapults destroy interior Always roof your castle
    Building in a valley Enemies attack from above Build on high ground or flat plateau
