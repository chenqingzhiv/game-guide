    ⛏️ V Rising — Materials & Resources Database

    Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 1.1 Gloomrot

    V Rising's progression is driven by materials. Every gear tier, castle upgrade, and consumable requires specific resources gathered from the world. This database covers every material — from basic wood and stone to rare endgame components — with acquisition methods, crafting uses, and regional availability.

    🪵 Basic Resources

    Wood Types

    Name Type Tier Acquisition Primary Uses Region
    Wood Planks Refined 1 Sawmill: 1 Log → 4 Planks Basic buildings, torches, early gear All regions
    Stone Dust Refined 1 Grinder: 1 Stone → 4 Dust Castle upgrades, bricks All regions
    Sulphur Refined 1 Sulphur veins (Farbane) Explosives, bullets Farbane Woods
    Gears Refined 1 Furnace: 1 Iron + 1 Stone Workstations, advanced crafting Crafted
    Reinforced Planks Refined 2 Sawmill: 8 Planks + 4 Iron Castle heart T2+, high-tier gear Crafted
    Stone Bricks Refined 2 Grinder: 4 Stone Dust + 2 Sulphur Stone castle floors, walls Crafted
    Copper Ingot Refined 1 Furnace: 3 Copper + 1 Sulphur Copper weapons, Simple gear Crafted — Copper node
    Iron Ingot Refined 2 Furnace: 3 Iron + 1 Sulphur Iron weapons, Hollowfang gear Crafted — Iron node
    Dark Silver Refined 3 Furnace: 3 Silver + 1 Sulphur Dark Silver weapons, PvP gear Crafted — Silver node
    Gold Ingot Refined 4 Furnace: 3 Gold + 1 Sulphur Sanguine gear, high-end castle Crafted — Gold vein

    Blood Crystal Crystal deposit Dark Silver pickaxe 1–3 per hit Cursed Forest (rare)
    Primal Blood Heart nodes Endgame tool 1 per node Dracula's Castle
