    ⚔️ V Rising — Equipment & Upgrade Path

    Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 1.1 Gloomrot

    Gear in V Rising is tied to V Blood progression. Each tier unlocks at the Smithy after defeating specific bosses. Your gear level determines which V Blood carriers you can challenge — being undergeared means dealing reduced damage and taking increased damage. This guide maps every equipment tier from start to endgame.

    Gear Score System

    Your total Gear Score (GS) is the average of your weapon and armor item levels. Each gear tier has a specific GS range:

    Tier Item Level Range Playable Zone Minimum Boss Level
    Copper / Simple 8–13 Farbane Woods 16–20
    Bronze / Hollowfang 15–20 Farbane Woods (hard) 22–27
    Iron / Merciless 22–28 Dunley Farmlands 30–40
    Dark Silver 30–38 Silverlight Hills 40–52
    Shadow / Gloomrot 40–48 Cursed Forest / Gloomrot 55–70
    Sanguine / Legendary 50–60 Gloomrot North / Castle 74–86+

    🗡️ Weapon Progression

    Tier 1: Copper Weapons

    Unlocked by defeating Keely the Frost Archer (Lv. 16).

    Weapon Damage Attack Speed Special Materials
    Copper Sword 14–18 1.2s None 8 Copper + 4 Planks
    Copper Axe 16–20 1.3s +10% vs wood 10 Copper + 4 Planks
    Copper Mace 12–16 1.4s +15% Stagger 10 Copper + 4 Planks
    Copper Spear 15–19 1.1s +5m range 8 Copper + 6 Planks
    Copper Crossbow 20–24 1.8s +% Headshot 12 Copper + 6 Planks
    Copper Reaper 10–14 1.5s +50% vs plants 12 Copper + 8 Planks

    Total Materials for Full Loadout: ~60 Copper Ingots, 30 Planks, 8 Leather

    Tier 2: Iron Weapons

    Unlocked by defeating Vincent the Frost Bringer (Lv. 37).

    Weapon Damage Attack Speed Special Materials
    Iron Sword 22–28 1.1s None 12 Iron + 6 Planks + 2 Leather
    Iron Axe 24–30 1.2s +15% vs wood 14 Iron + 6 Planks + 2 Leather
    Iron Mace 20–26 1.3s +20% Stagger 14 Iron + 6 Planks + 2 Leather
    Iron Spear 24–30 1.0s +5m range 12 Iron + 8 Planks + 2 Leather
    Iron Crossbow 30–36 1.7s +% Headshot 16 Iron + 8 Planks + 4 Leather
    Iron Reaper 18–22 1.4s +50% vs plants 16 Iron + 10 Planks + 2 Leather
    Iron Scythe 20–26 1.2s +25% vs undead 14 Iron + 6 Planks + 4 Bone

    Total Materials for Full Loadout: ~90 Iron Ingots, 50 Planks, 20 Leather, 10 Bone

    Tier 3: Dark Silver Weapons

    Unlocked by defeating Terah the Geomancer (Lv. 52).

    Weapon Damage Attack Speed Special Materials
    Dark Silver Sword 32–40 1.0s +15% Holy damage vs undead 14 Dark Silver + 8 Planks + 4 Leather
    Dark Silver Axe 34–42 1.1s +20% vs wood, +Holy 16 Dark Silver + 8 Planks + 4 Leather
    Dark Silver Mace 30–38 1.2s +25% Stagger, +Holy 16 Dark Silver + 8 Planks + 4 Leather
    Dark Silver Spear 34–42 0.9s +6m range, +Holy 14 Dark Silver + 10 Planks + 4 Leather
    Dark Silver Crossbow 40–48 1.6s +15% Headshot, +Holy 18 Dark Silver + 10 Planks + 6 Leather
    Dark Silver Reaper 28–34 1.3s +50% vs plants, +Holy 18 Dark Silver + 12 Planks + 4 Leather

    Total Materials for Full Loadout: ~100 Dark Silver Ingots, 60 Planks, 30 Leather

    Tier 4: Shadow / Gloomrot Weapons

    Unlocked by defeating Mordrim the Diseased (Lv. 65) or Voltatia (Lv. 62).

    Weapon Damage Attack Speed Special Materials
    Shadow Sword 40–50 0.9s +20% Shadow damage, Life drain 18 Shadow Essence + 12 Iron + 6 Gears
    Shadow Axe 42–52 1.0s +25% vs wood, +Shadow 20 Shadow Essence + 14 Iron + 6 Gears
    Shadow Mace 38–48 1.1s +30% Stagger, +Shadow 20 Shadow Essence + 14 Iron + 6 Gears
    Shadow Spear 42–52 0.9s +6m range, +Shadow 18 Shadow Essence + 12 Iron + 8 Gears
    Shadow Crossbow 48–58 1.5s +20% Headshot, +Shadow 22 Shadow Essence + 16 Iron + 10 Gears

    Total Materials for Full Loadout: ~120 Shadow Essence, 80 Iron Ingots, 40 Gears

    Tier 5: Sanguine / Legendary Weapons

    Unlocked by defeating Raziel the Soul Reaver (Lv. 80).

    Weapon Damage Attack Speed Special Materials
    Sanguine Sword 52–64 0.8s +25% Blood damage, Lifesteal 18 Primal Blood + 12 Spectral Silk + 6 Iron of Glory
    Sanguine Axe 54–68 0.9s +30% vs wood, +Blood 20 Primal Blood + 14 Spectral Silk + 6 Iron of Glory
    Sanguine Mace 50–62 1.0s +35% Stagger, Lifesteal 20 Primal Blood + 14 Spectral Silk + 6 Iron of Glory
    Sanguine Spear 54–68 0.8s +7m range, Bleed 18 Primal Blood + 12 Spectral Silk + 8 Iron of Glory
    Sanguine Crossbow 60–74 1.4s +25% Headshot, Bleed 22 Primal Blood + 16 Spectral Silk + 10 Iron of Glory

    Total Materials for Full Loadout: ~120 Primal Blood, 80 Spectral Silk, 40 Iron of Glory

    📈 Gear Progression Roadmap

    LEVEL 1–20: Farbane Woods
  → Copper Weapon (Keely → Lv.16)
  → Simple Armor → Hollowfang Armor
  → Clear: Keely → Rufus → Feron → Errol → Grayson → Lidia
  Gear Score Target: 15–18

LEVEL 20–45: Dunley Farmlands
  → Iron Weapon (Vincent → Lv.37)
  → Iron / Merciless Armor
  → Clear: Vincent → Christina → Leandra → Goreswine → Matka → Maja
  Gear Score Target: 25–30

LEVEL 45–60: Silverlight Hills
  → Dark Silver Weapon (Terah → Lv.52)
  → Dark Silver Armor
  → Clear: Terah → Azariel → Solarus
  Gear Score Target: 35–40

LEVEL 55–75: Cursed Forest → Gloomrot
  → Shadow/Gloomrot Weapon (Mordrim → Lv.65)
  → Shadow Armor
  → Clear: Mordrim → Bloodbringer Matriarch → Voltatia → General Marcus
  Gear Score Target: 45–50

LEVEL 75–86+: Gloomrot North → Castle
  → Sanguine Weapon (Raziel → Lv.80)
  → Sanguine Armor
  → Clear: Raziel → Dracula
  Gear Score Target: 55–60
