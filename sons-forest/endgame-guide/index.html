    🏆 Endgame Guide

    Sons of the Forest Endgame Guide

    The endgame of Sons of the Forest takes you deep beneath the island into a massive underground research facility. This guide covers everything you need — the gear, the route, the boss fights, and the multiple endings.

    Prerequisites — What to Bring

    Essential Items

    Item Location Purpose
    Golden Armor Bunker Residential (Guest Keycard) Required to enter final chamber. See route below.
    Shovel Bunker Maintenance Dig up artifact spots, open specific doors
    Rebreather Caves (early game) Underwater sections in the lab
    Rope Gun Caves Cross gaps, create ziplines
    Glider Caves / Crafted Traverse the massive lab open areas
    Keycard A Bunker Residential Lab floor 1 locked doors
    Keycard B Bunker Entertainment Lab floor 2 access
    Keycard C Bunker Food & Storage Lab floor 3 reactor access

    Recommended Supplies

    Item Quantity Why
    Medical Mix 10+ 60% heal, fast animation
    Energy Mix 10+ Sprint endurance, combat mobility
    Tech Mesh Armor Full set Best armor-to-weight ratio
    Katana (upgraded) 1 Fastest melee DPS
    Pistol + Shotgun + Rifle All Each has a use — pistol for singles, shotty for groups
    Slingshot 1 Stun lock mutants
    Grenades 3+ Boss damage burst
    Time Bomb 3+ Group clear for mutant gauntlets
    Flashlight attachment 1 Dark lab sections
    Night Vision Goggles 1 Optional (found in Entertainment Bunker)

    Golden Armor — Complete Route

    The Golden Armor is the single most important endgame item. Without it, you cannot enter the Artifact Chamber and cannot complete the game.

    Step-by-Step Path

    Step Location Action
    1 Bunker Residential Entrance Find the bunker entrance (green circle on map, near the large lake)
    2 Guest Keycard Inside the bunker — it's on a desk in the first office area
    3 Maintenance Keycard From the Shovel bunker (southwest of the island)
    4 Golden Armor Room Requires Guest Keycard. Located in the lower level of Bunker Residential
    5 Equip armor It goes in the armor/accessory slot. Once equipped, it protects you from the artifact room's lethal radiation

    Climbing gear note: You need the Climbing Rope (#2 knot shortcut) to access some vertical sections of the bunker. Craft it from 3 ropes.

    Final Cave — The Laboratory Descent

    The final cave is a massive 4-level laboratory beneath the island's central mountain. Enter through the giant metal door near the helicopter crash site (requires Keycard C).

    Level 1: The Descent

    Section Enemies Hazards Strategy
    Initial drop None 50m fall (use rope) Repel down carefully
    Corridor A Armsy (mutant arms) Tight spaces Shotgun + dodge backwards
    Holding cells Virginia mutants (×4-6) Open area, multiple entry points Grenade the group, pick off survivors
    Elevator shaft Fingers mutants Drop attack from ceiling Listen for growling — look up before entering
    Level 1 exit None Keycard A door Use Keycard A

    Level 2: The Laboratory

    Section Enemies Hazards Strategy
    Flooded corridor Drowned mutants Underwater rebreather sections Use rebreather, swim FAST
    Containment breach Mutant dogs + flying squid mutants Darkness, no lights Night vision or flashlight
    Central atrium 2x Armsy + Fingers Open 3-story atrium Use the high ground. Snipe from the catwalk
    Cryo chambers Frozen mutants (don't break glass!) Breaking glass awakens everything Walk carefully around cryo tubes
    Lab exit None Keycard B door Use Keycard B

    Level 3: The Reactor

    Section Enemies Hazards Strategy
    Reactor core entrance None Radiation (wear Tech Mesh) Move fast through contaminated areas
    Power coupling puzzle 3x Fingers mutants 3 couplings must be removed simultaneously Use time bombs to sync the destruction
    Cooling tunnels Mega-Fingers boss Tight tunnels, boss chases you Kite backwards, shoot weak point (glowing sac on its belly)
    Boss arena Mega-Fingers + adds Large chamber with pillars Use pillars for cover. Aim for the glowing belly sac

    Mega-Fingers Boss Strategy: - Phase 1: Boss charges in straight lines. Side-step and shoot the glowing belly - Phase 2 (below 50% HP): Boss spawns smaller Fingers adds. Grenade them immediately - Phase 3 (below 25%): Boss enrages — faster charges and wider hitbox. Use the environment pillars - Kill reward: Drops the Artifact (3 modes — see Endings below)

    Level 4: The Artifact Chamber

    Section Requirements Enemies Reward
    Entry door Golden Armor equipped 0 (radiation kills without armor) Chamber access
    Artifact pedestal Artifact from Mega-Fingers 0 Place artifact to choose ending

    Multiple Endings

    After defeating Mega-Fingers and collecting the Artifact, descend to Level 4 with your Golden Armor. The Artifact has 3 modes that produce 3 different endings:

    Ending A: Escape (Sky Mode)

    Mode Activation Cutscene Outcome
    Place Artifact in Sky position (upward) Helicopter arrives within 60 seconds You and Kelvin/Virginia escape on a helicopter. The island is sealed.

    Requirements: Must have kept both Kelvin and Virginia alive through the game.

    Good ending cutscene: The helicopter flies over the island as the facility collapses. End credits show your character back in civilization.

    Ending B: Destroy (Fire Mode)

    Mode Activation Cutscene Outcome
    Place Artifact in Fire position (red/downward) Explosion sequence triggers The entire facility and island are destroyed. You presumably die in the explosion.

    Requirements: None — can do alone.

    Bad ending cutscene: Massive explosion from the center of the island. The screen fades to white.

    Ending C: Power (Cube Mode)

    Mode Activation Cutscene Outcome
    Place Artifact in Cube position (neutral/center) Defense grid activates You become the island's new guardian. The facility's automated defenses activate, purging all mutants. You rule the island.

    Secret ending cutscene: You sit on a throne in the control room as the defense grid fires. The island becomes a fortress under your command. Post-credits scene shows a new mutant species emerging from the ocean (sequel setup).

    Post-Game Content

    After completing any ending, the game continues in free-play mode:

    Max base defense Build an impenetrable fortress Survivor bragging rights
    Weapon collection Find all weapon types (9 total) Combat versatility
    Virginia companion max Max her trust level (4-stages) Machine gun Virginia

    Pro Endgame Tips

    1. Golden Armor first: Make the Golden Armor your primary objective once you have Keycard A. It opens the entire final sequence. Don't explore every bunker first — the armor is priority #1.
    2. Time bombs for bosses: Craft 5-10 time bombs before entering the final cave. The Mega-Fingers boss takes 3x damage from explosives. A well-placed time bomb does 200+ damage.
    3. Virginia's trust: Max her trust before the final cave. At trust level 4, she gives you a unique weapon (machine gun pistols). She also provides covering fire during the final boss.
    4. Kelvin's notebook: Kelvin takes notes throughout the game. Read his notebook after each bunker to piece together the lore. His final entry changes based on your ending choice.
    5. Save prep: Create a hard save BEFORE entering the artifact chamber. This lets you replay all 3 endings without re-fighting Mega-Fingers.
    6. The "secret" purple bunker: There's a hidden bunker accessible only by breaking through a weak floor in Cave C. It contains the Golden Mask cosmetic and extra lore.
    7. Night Vision vs Flashlight: Night Vision uses batteries but doesn't attract enemies. Flashlight draws cannibal attention. For stealth in the final lab, use Night Vision.
    8. Multiple playthroughs: Endings A and C each unlock unique cosmetic items for future playthroughs (save file bound). Ending A gives a pilot outfit; Ending C gives a security guard uniform.
