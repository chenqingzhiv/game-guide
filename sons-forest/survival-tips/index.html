    🆕 Survival Tips

    Sons of the Forest Survival Guide

    Sons of the Forest is a brutal survival horror game. This guide covers everything from your first 10 minutes on the island to building a fortified base that can survive endgame mutant waves.

    Day 1: Crash Landing Survival

    Immediate Priority List

    Order Action Materials Needed Why
    1 Find a water source — Thirst kills in ~2 days
    2 Collect 10 sticks Ground pickup Shelter + fire
    3 Collect 5 rocks Ground pickup Stone axe + fire ring
    4 Build a temporary shelter 10 sticks Saves the game
    5 Craft a Stone Axe 5 sticks + 2 rocks Chop trees efficiently
    6 Craft a Bow 5 sticks + 3 cloth + 1 rope Ranged hunting
    7 Kill 1-2 rabbits Bow or spear Meat + hide
    8 Build a fire 5 sticks + 3 rocks Cook meat, warmth, light

    What NOT to Do on Day 1

    Mistake Consequence
    Attack cannibals They remember and bring more. Plus you have no armor — one hit = 50% HP
    Sleep without fire Wake up freezing, lose HP throughout the night
    Enter caves unprepared Caves are late-day content. No weapons = death
    Build a permanent base immediately Day 1 is about survival, not architecture. Wait until you've scouted
    Eat raw meat Raw rabbit = 5 food, 5 health damage. Cooked = 10 food, 0 damage

    Companion Management: Kelvin

    Kelvin's Follow Mode Commands

    Kelvin is your silent, injured companion. He takes written notes and follows verbal commands via the command book (press 'C' on keyboard, or open the radial menu).

    Command Interaction What Kelvin Does
    Fetch Logs, Rocks, Fish, Stick, Small Rocks Gathers the specified resource within ~50m radius
    Build Shelter, Wall, Bed, Fire Constructs the specified structure
    Repair Repair Fixes the nearest damaged structure
    Attack To specific enemy type Kills wildlife ONLY (rabbits, deer, squirrels)
    Rest No fighting Sits near base, stays out of combat
    Follow — Stays by your side
    Wait — Stays at current position until recalled

    Kelvin's best uses: - Fetch Logs: Set him to gather wood while you explore. 10 logs = 1 wall segment. He can gather 20+ logs in the time it takes you to build - Build a fire: Always have him start a fire while you prepare food - Build walls: He auto-constructs defensive walls around your base marker - Fish gathering: Set him to fetch fish near water = passive food income

    Kelvin Health Management

    • Kelvin takes damage but CANNOT die from cannibal attacks below a certain AI threshold (he'll crawl and recover)
    • He CAN die from fall damage, drowning, or friendly fire
    • Heal Kelvin: Give him a Meds item (right-click in backpack while near him)
    • If Kelvin gets knocked down: Wait ~30 seconds, he recovers. Don't shoot near him

    Companion Management: Virginia

    Virginia is a three-legged mutant who starts as a hostile NPC. She becomes a companion through a trust system.

    Trust Progression

    Stage Trust Level Behavior Unlocks
    0 0-25% Flees from you Nothing
    1 25-50% Watches from distance She approaches the edge of your base
    2 50-75% Approaches your base Takes food from your hand
    3 75-100% Follows you Fights alongside you, gives gifts
    4 100% Best friend Gives you machine gun pistols, dances, plays

    How to Gain Trust Fast

    Action Points Gained Repeatable?
    Give her food (berries, meat) +5 Yes, 2x per day
    Let her close without attacking +2 Passive
    Have her near you during combat +3 per fight Yes
    Give her a weapon +10 One-time (pistol, shotgun)
    Complete story events near her +15 Per location
    Attack her -25 Avoid at all costs

    Weapons for Virginia: Once she reaches trust level 2, drop a pistol (with ammo) on the ground near her. She'll pick it up and holster it. Later, upgrade her to shotgun. At max trust, she uses twin machine pistols.

    Base Building: Fortification Strategies

    Base Location Considerations

    Location Pros Cons
    Beach near crash site Flat, easy access to water, logs nearby Cannibal patrols frequent in late game
    Lake island Natural moat, easy defense Limited building space, must swim/logs
    Cliff plateau Defensible, single approach Difficult to expand
    Forest interior Abundant trees, cover Hard to see approaching threats

    Best beginner base location: The beach peninsula east of the crashed helicopter. Flat ground, ocean on 3 sides, dense forest behind. Build walls along the forest side only.

    Defense Layers (Top to Bottom Priority)

    Layer Materials Purpose
    Deadfall traps Logs + rope (5 per trap) First wave defense — kills cannibals outright
    Spike barricade Part logs ×15 per segment Slows and damages enemies
    Stone wall Stones (30 per segment) Fireproof, high HP — essential for night 15+
    Wood wall backup Logs (10 per segment) Cheaper, burns — place inside stone walls
    Second wall layer Any Bunker-style defense
    Interior firing platform Logs + planks Elevated shooting position

    Base Building Checklist

    1. [ ] 4x4 foundation (logs)
    2. [ ] 1-wall height perimeter (stone preferred)
    3. [ ] Single entrance with spike traps
    4. [ ] Interior: fire pit + drying rack + storage/sled
    5. [ ] Defensive spikes on all walls
    6. [ ] Deadfall traps at choke points
    7. [ ] 2-3 zipline exits (escape routes)
    8. [ ] Trap chokepoint corridor before the entrance
    9. [ ] Upgraded to stone walls by Day 10
    10. [ ] Second wall perimeter by Day 20

    Combat Guide

    Weapon Tier List

    Weapon Damage Speed Ammo Best Against
    Katana 80 Very Fast None Everything (melee)
    Shotgun 120×5 Slow Shells Groups, mutants
    Rifle 80 Medium Rifle rounds Sniping cannibals from base walls
    Pistol (Silenced) 40 Fast 9mm Stealth kills
    Crossbow 90 Slow Bolts Silent 1-shot on cannibals
    Stun Baton 60 Fast Batteries Lockdown single mutant
    Slingshot 30 Fast Stones Stun lock, ammo everywhere

    Enemy Weaknesses

    Enemy Weakness Strategy
    Regular Cannibal Headshot (bow/gun) 1-shot with crossbow. 2-shot with pistol
    Painted Cannibal Fire Molotov = panic + burn damage
    Mutant Baby Avoid melee Shoot from range. They explode
    Armsy (many arms) Kite, shoot from behind Back side has no armor
    Virginia Mutant Fire + explosives Burns quickly, prioritize
    Fingers Glowing belly Dodge charge, shoot the belly sac
    Mega-Fingers Explosives Boss arena tactics

    Story Items — Key Locations

    Item Location Hint Purpose
    Shovel Bunker Maintenance (southwest of island) Dig up artifact locations, unlock specific doors
    Rebreather Cave C (central mountain, underwater entrance) Breathe underwater
    Rope Gun Cave A (northwest, near river) Cross gaps, create ziplines
    Glider Cave B (east side, high cliff) Traverse large gaps, escape from mutants
    Maintenance Keycard Bunker Maintenance (same as shovel) Opens maintenance doors
    Guest Keycard Bunker Residential (near lake) Open residential bunker doors
    VIP Keycard Bunker Entertainment (northeast) Open luxury bunker doors
    Golden Armor Bunker Residential (Guest Keycard area) Required for final ending chamber

    Pro Survival Tips

    1. Warmth management: Always have a fire going near your base during rain/snow. Wetness meter = hypothermia risk. Dry off at a fire.
    2. Food preservation: Build a drying rack (6 sticks + 2 rope). Dried meat lasts forever and restores 15 food. 5 drying racks = infinite food supply.
    3. Traps for patrol routes: Cannibals use the same patrol paths. Place deadfall traps on their trails between camps. Check them daily for free loot.
    4. Zipline network: Build ziplines between your base and key locations (caves, shoreline, bunkers). Mutants can follow ziplines but it takes them time — gives you a head start.
    5. Sticks are the best resource: Sticks make arrows, fire, shelter, traps, and crafting upgrades. Always carry a full stack (30+).
    6. Cave prep: Before entering any cave, save. Bring: flashlight, weapon, 3+ meds, 3+ energy mix, flare gun (scare mutants). Don't go underground at night.
    7. Mutant raid schedule: The game escalates raids every 5 days. Night 5: first cannibal raid. Night 10: mutant appears. Night 15: full mutant + cannibal group. Night 20+: you need a stone base.
    8. Creative building exploits: You can build a zipline INTO your base roof for a rooftop escape. Place a log hanging from a rope over your cooking fire for automatic meat smoking.
