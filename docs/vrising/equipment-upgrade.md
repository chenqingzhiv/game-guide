# ⚔️ V Rising — Equipment & Upgrade Path

*Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 1.1 Gloomrot*

---

Gear in V Rising is tied to **V Blood progression**. Each tier unlocks at the Smithy after defeating specific bosses. Your gear level determines which V Blood carriers you can challenge — being undergeared means dealing reduced damage and taking increased damage. This guide maps every equipment tier from start to endgame.

---

## Gear Score System

Your total **Gear Score (GS)** is the average of your weapon and armor item levels. Each gear tier has a specific GS range:

| Tier | Item Level Range | Playable Zone | Minimum Boss Level |
|:-----|:-----------------|:--------------|:-------------------|
| Copper / Simple | 8–13 | Farbane Woods | 16–20 |
| Bronze / Hollowfang | 15–20 | Farbane Woods (hard) | 22–27 |
| Iron / Merciless | 22–28 | Dunley Farmlands | 30–40 |
| Dark Silver | 30–38 | Silverlight Hills | 40–52 |
| Shadow / Gloomrot | 40–48 | Cursed Forest / Gloomrot | 55–70 |
| Sanguine / Legendary | 50–60 | Gloomrot North / Castle | 74–86+ |

## 📈 Gear Progression Roadmap

```text
LEVEL 1–20: Farbane Woods
  → Copper Weapon (Keely → Lv.16)
  → Simple Armor → Hollowfang Armor
  → Clear: Keely → Rufus → Feron → Errol → Grayson → Lidia
  Gear Score Target: 15–18

LEVEL 20–45: Dunley Farmlands
  → Iron Weapon (Vincent → Lv.37)
  → Iron / Merciless Armor
  → Clear: Vincent → Christina → Leandra → Goreswine → Matka → Maja
  Gear Score Target: 25–30

LEVEL 45–60: Silverlight Hills
  → Dark Silver Weapon (Terah → Lv.52)
  → Dark Silver Armor
  → Clear: Terah → Azariel → Solarus
  Gear Score Target: 35–40

LEVEL 55–75: Cursed Forest → Gloomrot
  → Shadow/Gloomrot Weapon (Mordrim → Lv.65)
  → Shadow Armor
  → Clear: Mordrim → Bloodbringer Matriarch → Voltatia → General Marcus
  Gear Score Target: 45–50

LEVEL 75–86+: Gloomrot North → Castle
  → Sanguine Weapon (Raziel → Lv.80)
  → Sanguine Armor
  → Clear: Raziel → Dracula
  Gear Score Target: 55–60
```
