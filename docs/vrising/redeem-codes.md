## How to Redeem Codes in V Rising

1. Launch V Rising and enter your game world
2. Press **ESC** to open the pause menu
3. Click **"Redeem Code"** in the menu
4. Enter the code exactly as shown (codes are **case-sensitive**)
5. Click **Redeem** — rewards will appear in your inventory

> 💡 **Tip:** V Rising codes are rare and usually tied to **major updates**, **anniversaries**, or **Twitch Drops**. Grab them while they're active!
