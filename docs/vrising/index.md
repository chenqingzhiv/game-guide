# V Rising 🩸

**Awaken as a vampire.** Rise from your crypt, rebuild your castle, and feast on the blood of the living. Explore a sprawling open world, dominate the mortal kingdoms, and challenge other vampires in this gothic survival RPG.

## What is V Rising?

V Rising is an open-world survival action RPG where you play as a weakened vampire awakened from centuries of slumber. Hunt for blood to regain your power, craft dark arcane technology, build a gothic castle, and conquer the realm of Vardoran. Play solo or in clans on PvE/PvP servers.

## Getting Started Guide

### 1. First Steps

1. **Build a coffin** — your respawn point
2. **Extract blood** from deer, wolves, and humans
3. **Build a basic castle** — workbench, blood press, and storage
4. **Stay out of the sun** — travel through forests or use the bat form
5. **Defeat V Blood carriers** — bosses that unlock new powers and tech

### 2. Gear Progression

```
Tier 1: Bone weapons + Simple gear
  → Defeat Keely the Frost Archer (V Blood Lv.16)
    → Tier 2: Copper/Bronze weapons
      → Defeat Quincey the Bandit King (V Blood Lv.27)
        → Tier 3: Iron weapons + Dark Silver gear
          → Defeat Leandra the Shadow Priestess (V Blood Lv.37)
            → Tier 4: Sanguine gear + Legendary weapons
```

### 3. Blood Types

| Blood Type | Effect | Best Source |
|:-----------|:-------|:------------|
| 🩸 **Creature** | Movement speed, mount bonuses | Deer, wolves, bears |
| 🩸 **Brute** | Physical damage, siege damage | Bandits, soldiers |
| 🩸 **Rogue** | Crit chance, resource yield | Rogues, thieves |
| 🩸 **Warrior** | Weapon skill cooldown, parry | Militia, guards |
| 🩸 **Scholar** | Spell power, spell cooldown | Mages, priests |
| 🩸 **Draculin** | Drain power, life leech | Endgame enemies |
| 🩸 **Mutant** | Unique buffs, rare | Mutated creatures |

## Base Building Tips

- **Place your castle heart first** — determines territory radius
- **Upgrade castle heart** to expand build area
- **Servants** (charmed humans) defend your castle and gather resources
- **Keep crafting stations close** — workshops and forges in the same room
- **Build near a water source** for blood press and fishing
- **Gardens** grow plant fiber, cotton, and herbs

---

## Combat Tips

- **Dodge roll** has a cooldown — time it carefully
- **Blood rage** (ability trigger) varies by blood type
- **Crossbows** are safe for PvE farming
- **Swords and axes** have different special moves — experiment
- **Shapeshifting** (bat, wolf, human form) has combat applications
