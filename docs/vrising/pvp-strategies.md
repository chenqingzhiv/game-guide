## Best Builds

Axe (burst DPS): Slasher offhand + Axes main. Chaos Volley + Ward of the Damned.
Reaper (control): Reaper + Pistols. Ice Block + Frost Bat.
Sword & Shield (sustain): Longsword + Crossbow. Blood Rage + Sanguine Coil.

## Raid Defense

Multi-layer honeycomb: 3+ wall layers.
False rooms waste raider resources.
Servants near castle heart.
Castle heart in deepest room behind reinforced walls.
Hollow walls: 2-layer with empty space = more HP to break.

## Combat Mechanics

Dodge Roll: 0.5s invulnerability. Time with enemy projectiles.
Counter: Reflect melee attacks. Use after enemy commits to swing.
Ultimate: Full bar = ult. Hit 3+ targets for max value.
