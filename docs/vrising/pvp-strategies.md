## V Rising PvP Guide

PvP in V Rising is a brutal battle of gear score, ability management, and castle design. Whether you're defending your castle from vampire raiders or planning a full-scale assault, this guide covers the strategies that separate winners from ash piles.

---

## PVP Combat Mechanics

### Core Combat System

| Mechanic | Details | Counterplay |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Dodge Roll | 0.5s invulnerability window | Time attacks after roll ends |
| Counter Ability | Reflects the next melee hit | Bait the counter, wait it out |
| Spell Projectiles | Travel speed varies by spell | Learn trajectory + dodge pattern |
| Ultimate Abilities | Full meter = unleash ult | Use when enemy has no dodge |
| Blood Types | 12% → 100% quality | Higher quality = stronger buffs |

### Blood Type Buffs

| Blood Type | Effect at 100% Quality | Best For |
|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| Warrior | +30% Physical Power +30% Crit Chance | Melee DPS builds |
| Creature | +30% HP regen, -15% damage taken | Tank/Axe builds |
| Rogue | +30% Movement Speed, -20% cooldown | Hit-and-run, crossbow builds |
| Brute | +25% Primary Attack Life Leech | Sustain/survival builds |
| Scholar | +30% Spell Power, -30% Cooldown | Pure mage/caster builds |
| Worker | +30% resource yield | PvE only (not PvP viable) |

## Castle Defense Architecture

### Honeycomb Layout

The honeycomb is the gold standard of castle defense. It forces raiders to break through multiple walls to reach your castle heart.

```
[ Outside ]
    |
[ Wall 1 ]——[ Trap Room 1 ]
    |              |
[ Wall 2 ]——[ Trap Room 2 ]
    |              |
[ Wall 3 ]——[ Trap Room 3 ]
    |
[ Castle Heart ]
```

### Layer Requirements

| Defense Layer | Material | HP | Time to Break (Golem) |
|--------------|----------|-----|---------------------|
| Tier 1 Walls | Stone | 5,000 | ~30 seconds |
| Tier 2 Walls | Stone + Iron | 7,500 | ~60 seconds |
| Tier 3 Walls | Dark Silver | 12,000 | ~120 seconds |
| Tier 4 Walls | Gemstone | 20,000 | ~180 seconds |

### Room Types

| Room Type | Purpose | Best Placement |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| False Room | Empty room with locked door | Layers 2-3 — waste raider time/resources |
| Trap Room | Floor spikes, explosion traps | Layer 1 — damage enemies immediately |
| Servant Room | 4+ servant coffins | Layers 2-3 — defenders attack raiders |
| Storage Room | All valuable loot | Beyond layer 3 — protected |
| Hollow Wall | 2 walls with empty space | Any layer — double the HP to break |

### Key Defense Principles

1. **Castle heart position**: Place the castle heart in the deepest room, surrounded by at least 3 wall layers in every direction. Never put it on an outer wall.
2. **Servant proximity**: Servant coffins should be within 1 room of the castle heart. Servants have a patrol range — keep them near the core.
3. **No windows**: Windows let enemies see inside your base and plan their breach. Solid walls only.
4. **Multiple floors**: If your castle tier allows, build vertically. 2+ floors force raiders to climb stairs (slow, exposed).
5. **Servant patrol routes**: Place waypoints in your hollow walls. Servants patrol through them and engage raiders who are mid-breach.
6. **The "spider" layout**: Central heart with 4+ corridors radiating outward. Each corridor has 3-4 rooms. Raiders must guess which corridor leads to the heart.

### Raid Strategy

| Phase | Action | Key Details |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1. Reconnaissance | Scout the base at night | Note wall material, entrance count, servant level |
| 2. Siege setup | Place golem at weakest wall | Look for corners (less support) |
| 3. Breach | Slam walls in sequence | Focus on one corridor — don't spread damage |
| 4. Interior combat | Kill servants + defenders | Use Chaos Volley for tight corridors |
| 5. Castle heart | Destroy it in under 60 seconds | Use all remaining charges/abilities |

### Raid Window

- **V Blood Raid Hours**: 17:00-21:00 (event-based on most servers)
- **Full Loot PvP**: Always open
- **Offline raid protection**: Check your server settings. Some servers have 24h protection after logout

## Pro PvP Tips

1. **Potion economy**: Always carry 2+ Elixir of Holy Resistance + 2+ Blood Rose Potion before engaging in PvP. Buff food lasts 30 minutes — eat before hunting.
2. **Dodge discipline**: Do NOT spam dodge. A wasted dodge = death against good players. Wait for enemy abilities to commit before rolling.
3. **Ground telegraphs**: Every ability has a 0.3-0.5s ground telegraph. Learn to read them. Blood Rage (red aura) = incoming charge.
4. **Servant blood quality**: Only recruit servants with 75%+ blood quality. Low-quality servants die in one hit to geared raiders.
5. **Jewel crafting**: Farm Sunken Monastery for jewel crafting recipes. Speed Jewels (-cooldown) are universally useful. (Weaving) Jewels are build-specific.
6. **Gear PVP scaling**: Maximum gear gap matters. If you're GS 70 and enemy is GS 80, you deal ~30% less damage. Keep your gear within 2 tiers of the server max.
7. **Escape routes**: Every room should have an exit path. Use bat form (PvP servers) or Rat Form (unlock from V Blood) to escape through small gaps. Keep a teleporter room near the heart.
8. **Clan coordination**: In clan PvP (solo players are at a disadvantage), assign roles: 1 tank (Brute + Sword), 2 DPS (Warrior + Axes), 1 support (Scholar + Curse spells). Stun + focus fire = kill confirm.
9. **Base reputation**: On merciless PvP servers, your castle's rep matters. If you're known for camping outside newbie bases, the server will gang up on you. Play smart, not toxic.
10. **Server settings check**: Before building a castle, verify: raid hours, offline raiding rules, castle destruction decay timer, and PvP damage modifier. Settings vary wildly between official and community servers.
