# Subnautica Biomes Guide

Planet 4546B has over 20 distinct biomes, each with unique resources, dangers, and secrets. Knowing where to go — and what to expect — determines whether you return with loot or become leviathan food. This guide covers every biome with detailed data tables.

## Biome Exploration Checklist

1. **Check your depth** — Each vehicle has a crush depth. Going even 2m below destroys it instantly.
2. **Scan for threats** — Use the Seamoth sonar upgrade to detect predators before they detect you.
3. **Mark with beacons** — Drop a beacon at every biome entrance, base location, and wreck for navigation.
4. **Check resources** — Scan for specific resources using the Scanner Room before diving.
5. **Bring power cells** — A Seamoth uses 1 power cell per 15 minutes of active driving. Carry spares.
6. **Watch your oxygen** — At 500m+, a standard O2 tank gives only 45 seconds. Upgrade to High Capacity before deep dives.
7. **Stick to cave roofs** — In caves, navigating by the roof is easier than the floor. Light glows from above.
8. **Listen for roars** — Each leviathan has a unique roar audible from 300m away. Learn the sounds.
9. **Plan exit routes** — Know which entrance you'll use to leave before you enter. Getting lost in deep biomes without oxygen is fatal.
10. **Build outposts** — Place a small base (1 room + 1 solar panel + 1 fabricator) at key biome intersections for emergency refueling.
