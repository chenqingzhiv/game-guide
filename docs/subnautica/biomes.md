## Safe Biomes

Safe Shallows: Starter biome. Plentiful resources, no predators.
Kelp Forest: Creepvines, stalkers. Good for early titanium.
Grasslands: Red grass, sand sharks. Easy resources.

## Dangerous Biomes

Blood Kelp Zone: Deep, scary. Blood oil, deep shrooms.
Lost River: Green brine, ghost leviathans. Endgame zone.
Lava Zone: Extreme depth, sea dragons. Best resources.
Dunes: Reaper leviathans. Avoid until Prawn Suit.
