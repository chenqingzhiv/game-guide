## 📊 Aggression Quick Reference

- **🟢 Passive** — ignores the player entirely unless attacked. Best candidates for scanning.
- **🟡 Territorial** — attacks only if you get too close or after being provoked.
- **🟠 Defensive** — reacts to threats with a non-lethal deterrent (gas, hypnosis, ink).
- **🔴 Aggressive** — actively hunts the player on sight. Requires situational awareness.
- **⚫ Leviathan** — the apex predators. One-hit-kill territory. Use a vehicle or avoid completely.
