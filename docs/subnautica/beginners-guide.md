## First Day

1. Escape burning lifepod
2. Gather 10 Titanium, 5 Copper, 5 Quartz
3. Craft Survival Knife > Scanner > Fins
4. Build Fabricator + Oxygen Tank
5. Create Habitat Builder
6. Build 1-room base in Safe Shallows
7. Place 2 Solar Panels

Pro tip: Build in Safe Shallows - central, safe, plenty of resources.

## Resources

Titanium: Limestone, Salvage.
Copper: Limestone.
Quartz: Safe Shallows, Kelp.
Silver: Sandstone.
Gold: Sandstone.
Lithium: Mountain Island, Deep.
Magnetite: Jellyshroom Cave.
Ruby: Deep Grand Reef.

## Vehicle Progression

Seaglide (Day 1): Fast personal travel.
Seamoth (Day 3-4): 1-person sub, fast.
Prawn Suit (Day 7+): Deep mining, combat.
Cyclops (Endgame): Mobile base, holds vehicles.
