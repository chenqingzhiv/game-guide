# Enshrouded Boss Guide

Embervale is home to deadly bosses that guard the best loot in the game. From the Fell Wyrms in the Springlands to the terrifying Fell Dragon, each boss requires specific strategies. This guide breaks down every boss with locations, weaknesses, attack patterns, and guaranteed drops.

## Fell Wyrm (Springlands) — Level 15

**Location:** Open-world boss arena in southeastern Springlands, near the Ancient Spire.

**Recommended Gear:** Level 12+ weapons, Scavenger or Soldier armor set.

### Attack Patterns

| Attack | Description | Counter |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Fire Breath** | 180-degree cone, 3-second wind-up | Dodge behind or to the side |
| **Tail Sweep** | Ground-level 360-degree spin | Jump over it |
| **Stomp** | Front paw slam, small AoE | Roll backward |
| **Charge** | Rushes forward 20 meters | Side-step at the last moment |

### Phase Breakdown

**Phase 1 (100%–60% HP):** Slow attacks with clear wind-ups. Fire breath has a 3-second tell — the Wyrm rears its head back and inhales audibly. Use this window to get behind it and land 3–4 hits.

**Phase 2 (60%–30% HP):** Adds a tail sweep and charge. The tail sweep hits hard (70+ damage) but is easy to jump over. During charge, the Wyrm stops briefly before charging — side-step don't roll backward.

**Phase 3 (30%–0% HP):** Enrages. Attack speed doubles. Fire breath now has a 1.5-second wind-up only. Pop a health potion at 30% and go aggressive. This phase is a DPS race.

### Strategy

**Melee:** Stay at the flanks (between front and back legs). This avoids both fire breath and tail sweep. Two heavy attacks then roll out.

**Ranged:** Keep 15+ meters distance. Headshots do 2x damage. Fire breath can't reach you at max range. Watch for the charge.

**Magic:** Frost wand + frost staff. Ice damage is 50% more effective against Wyrms. Frost staff's AoE blast can stagger the Wyrm out of its fire breath wind-up.

### Guaranteed Drops

| Item | Use |
|------|-----|
| Fell Wyrm Scale x2–4 | Tier 4 armor upgrade material |
| Wyrm Hide x1–2 | Leather crafting |
| Fell Wyrm Meat x2 | Cook for +30 food, +15% melee damage buff |
| Coins x50 | Vendor currency |

**Rare Drop (15%):** Wyrm Tooth Ring — +8% Critical Hit Chance.

## Hollow Lord (Springlands Vault) — Level 18

**Location:** Inside the Springlands Sun Temple Vault. Requires a Vault Stone (found at the pillar leading to the temple).

**Recommended Gear:** Level 16+, fire weapon recommended. Bring lockpicks.

### Attack Patterns

| Attack | Description | Counter |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Soul Bolt** | 3-projectile magic spread | Side-step between bolts |
| **Shield Charge** | Rushes with shield up (blocking frontal damage) | Dodge to side, attack from behind |
| **Seal Phase** | Creates 4 Seal Crystals at 75% HP | Destroy all 4 within 60 seconds |
| **Shadow Burst** | AoE around boss (75%+ mana cost for you) | Move 10m+ away |

### Phase Breakdown

**Phase 1 (100%–75% HP):** Slow melee combos and Soul Bolts. Easy. Use fire weapons for 40% extra damage.

**Phase 2 (75%–40% HP — Seal Phase):** The boss becomes invulnerable and spawns 4 Seal Crystals around the arena. You have 60 seconds to destroy all 4. Each crystal has 500 HP. If you fail, the boss heals 20% and resummons them. **Priority:** Destroy crystals immediately. Use AoE spells or explosives for quick clears.

**Phase 3 (40%–0% HP):** Boss drops the shield and goes berserk. Spams Shadow Burst and Shield Charge in rapid succession. Keep moving. Attack after dodging a charge — the boss is stunned for 2 seconds after missing.

### Guaranteed Drops

| Item | Use |
|------|-----|
| Hollow Core x1 | Tier 4 crafting material |
| Soul Crystal x1–2 | Mana potion ingredient |
| Vault Key Fragment | Combine 3 for Vault Key |
| Hollow Lord's Helm blueprint | Heavy helmet, 28 defense |

**Rare Drop (20%):** Soul Reaper Staff — fires homing soul projectiles.

## Fell Wyrm (Revelwood) — Level 25

**Location:** Deep within Revelwood forest, near the Pink Apple Grove.

**Differences from Springlands Wyrm:** Same base moveset but adds poison pools, 50% more HP, and faster attack speed. Bring anti-poot.

| New Attack | Description | Counter |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **Poison Pool** | Vomits poison puddle (lingers 30s, 15 damage/tick) | Stay mobile, never stand still |
| **Toxic Roar** | AoE poison + stagger | Block or dodge through it |
| **Add Spawn** | Summons 2 Fell Spitters every 45 seconds in Phase 2+ | Kill adds immediately |

### Strategy

**Key Difference:** Don't stand in one place for more than 3 seconds — poison pools will accumulate under you. Kite the Wyrm around the arena. When adds spawn, switch to them immediately. A single Spitter can poison you for 50% of your HP.

**Recommended Weapon:** Ice damage is best. The Fell Stinger (bow, dropped by this boss on rare proc) is ideal for ranged.

## Hollow Lord (Kindlewastes) — Level 28

**Location:** Kindlewastes Obsidian Vault. Requires Obsidian Vault Stone.

**Changes from Springlands version:** Triple seal crystals (1,200 HP each), add spawns every 30 seconds in Phase 2+, and the boss moves 40% faster.

| New Mechanic | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Triple Seals** | 3 crystals must be destroyed simultaneously (within 5 seconds of each other) or they all respawn |
| **Add Waves** | 3 Fell Warriors spawn every 30 seconds in Phase 2 |
| **Lava Floor** | Floor tiles glow red before erupting — move off them within 2 seconds |

### Strategy

**Phase 2 (Seal Phase):** This is the hardest mechanic. Damage all 3 crystals evenly until they're all at ~10% HP, then destroy them in quick succession. Use AoE to damage multiple at once. Clear adds before focusing crystals.

**Phase 3:** Kite around the arena avoiding lava tiles. The boss's Shadow Burst now covers a 15m radius — much larger than the Springlands version.

## Fell Dragon (Albaneve Summits) — Level 30+

**Location:** Dragon's Peak, the highest point in Albaneve Summits. Requires Dragon Key (drops from both Hollow Lords and both Wyrms).

**Recommended Gear:** Level 30+ with legendary weapons. Full Radiant Paladin or Fell Nightmare set. Bring 20+ bandages and 5+ health potions.

### Phase Breakdown

**Phase 1 — Ground (100%–70% HP)**
| Attack | Counter |
|--------|---------|
| Bite (frontal cone) | Dodge sideways |
| Wing Sweep (wide arc) | Block (heavy damage reduction) |
| Tail Slam (behind) | Don't stand behind — massive damage |
| Fire Breath (cone) | Run to the side, not backward |

**Phase 2 — Flight (70%–40% HP)**
The dragon takes off and rains fireballs from above. Use the grapple points around the arena edges to reach floating platforms. On platforms, you can melee the dragon's wings.

| Attack | Counter |
|--------|---------|
| Fireball Barrage (10 orbs) | Grapple to a different platform |
| Wing Gust (knockback) | Face into the gust to reduce push |
| Dive Bomb (instant kill) | Grapple hook to a platform LAST SECOND |

**Phase 3 — Enrage (40%–0% HP)**
Dragon returns to ground but moves at double speed. All cooldowns halved. This is a pure DPS check.

**Strategy:** Pop all cooldowns at 40% and go ham. Use healing potions on cooldown. The dragon's fire breath during enrage is a 360-degree AoE — you can't outrun it, only outheal it or block it.

**Save your stamina for dodging.** Don't waste it on sprint attacks.

### Guaranteed Drops

| Item | Use |
|------|-----|
| Dragon Scale x3–5 | Tier 5 upgrade material |
| Dragon Heart x1 | Legendary crafting component |
| Fell Dragonfang (weapon) | Legendary sword, 45 base damage |
| Dragon's Breath (staff) | Legendary fire staff |
| Coins x200 | Vendor currency |
| Dragon Armor blueprint | Best heavy armor in the game |

## Shroud Beast — Level 32+

**Location:** The Underworld, at the end of the Shroud Caves dungeon chain.

**Mechanics:** The boss is immune to all damage until you destroy 3 Shroud Orbs in the arena. Orbs regenerate after 45 seconds. You must deal 20% of the boss's HP per orb phase.

**Weakness:** Radiant (light) damage. Hollow weapons deal reduced damage.

**Drops:** Shroud Essence (legendary upgrade material), Beast Core (crafting), Shroud Walker boots blueprint (immunity to shroud damage while worn).

---

## The Collector — Level 35+

**Location:** The Underworld, Deep Vault. Requires all 3 Vault Keys from previous bosses.

**Mechanics:** 5-phase fight — each phase the boss summons different Undead minions and gains a new damage type.

**Drops:** Collector's Key (unlocks final treasure room), Ancient Tome (2 skill points), Collector's Amulet (+20% all damage, +10 mana).

## Boss Preparation Checklist

1. **Check elemental weakness** — equip the right weapon type before entering the arena
2. **Repair all gear** — broken armor mid-fight is a death sentence
3. **Craft potions** — 5 health, 3 mana, 1 anti-poison/anti-fire as needed
4. **Clear inventory** — you'll get valuable drops and don't want to leave them behind
5. **Set your respawn point** — place a bedroll outside the boss arena
6. **Eat a full meal** — food buffs last 25+ minutes and provide stat bonuses
7. **Bring a friend** — bosses scale for multiplayer but having a healer makes every fight easier
8. **Study the arena** — know where the grapple points, cover, and hazards are before engaging
9. **Stock arrows/potions** — running out of ammo mid-fight is a common mistake
10. **Use a Vault Stone** — marks the vault so you can return after death
