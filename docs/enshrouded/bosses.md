## Boss Overview

Enshrouded features diverse bosses across Embervale. Each requires specific strategies, gear, and preparation.

## The Fell Wyrms

Level 15 (Springlands): Fire breath, 180 degree arc. Dodge behind during wind-up. Weak to ice. Loot: Fell Wyrm Scale.

Level 25 (Revelwood): Adds poison pools. Clear adds first. Bring anti-poison.

## The Hollow Lords

Level 18 (Springlands Vault): Shield phase at 75% HP. Break 4 Seal Crystals. Weak to fire.

Level 28 (Kindlewastes): Triple seal crystals. Add spawns every 30s.

## Fell Dragon (Endgame)

Phases: Ground (bite/sweep) > Flight (fireballs, use grapple) > Enrage (double speed, pop cooldowns).

Required: Level 30+, legendary gear.
