## Biomes

Springlands (1-8): Starter, green hills, basic resources.
Revelwood (8-16): Dense forest, copper/tin.
Nomad Highlands (14-20): Arid, iron/sulfur.
Kindlewastes (20-25): Desert, obsidian.
Albaneve Summits (25-30): Snow, frost crystal.
The Underworld (30+): Endgame, best loot.

## Fast Travel

15 Ancient Spires across biomes. Climb to top, activate crystal. Free travel after activation.

## Secrets

Floating Island (Revelwood): Requires 20+ grapple stamina. Unique weapon blueprint.
Underground Lake (Springlands): Via well near Longkeep. Rare fish + hidden chest.
Abandoned Library (Kindlewastes): 3 skill point tomes.
