# Enshrouded Map & Exploration Guide

The world of Embervale spans six distinct biomes, from the gentle Springlands to the deadly Underworld. Each zone offers unique resources, enemies, and secrets. Understanding the map — and how to navigate it efficiently — is the key to progression.

---

## Biome Overview Table

| Biome | Level Range | Key Resources | Dangers | Notable POIs |
|-------|-------------|--------------|---------|-------------|
| **Springlands** | 1–8 | Stone, Wood, Resin, String, Copper Ore | Fell Spitters, Wolves, Scavengers | Longkeep, Elixir Well, Ancient Spire |
| **Revelwood** | 8–16 | Copper, Tin, Sulfur, Infused String | Poison Spitters, Wolfmasters, Fell Wasps | Pink Apple Grove, Floating Island, Hunter's Cabin |
| **Nomad Highlands** | 14–20 | Iron Ore, Sulfur, Coal, Leather Scraps | Nomad Archers, Fell Stalkers, Shroud Banshees | Nomad Fortress, Cinder Vault, Mining Camp |
| **Kindlewastes** | 20–25 | Obsidian, Palladium, Ember, Cinder | Fell Warriors, Lava Spiders, Flame Banshees | Obsidian Vault, Abandoned Library, Dragon's Gate |
| **Albaneve Summits** | 25–30 | Frost Crystal, Dragon Scale, Winter Wood | Frost Trolls, Ice Wraiths, Snow Stalkers | Dragon's Peak, Glacier Temple, Frozen Vault |
| **The Underworld** | 30+ | Shroud Essence, Beast Core, Ancient Relics | Shroud Beasts, Corrupted Knights, The Collector | Deep Vault, Shroud Caves, Ancient City |

> **Tip:** Don't rush to higher biomes too early. Enemies in Revelwood will two-shot you if you're still level 6. The game's level scaling is strict.

## The Shroud

The Shroud is a magical fog that covers parts of Embervale. Standing in it without protection deals rapid damage.

### Shroud Mechanics

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Damage per second (outside shroud) | 8 HP/s |
| Damage per second (deep shroud) | 20 HP/s |
| Maximum time in shroud (base) | 5 minutes |
| Maximum time (with Shroud Weaver potion) | 15 minutes |
| Shroud Core duration boost | +2 minutes each |

### Shroud Survival

| Item | Effect | How to Get |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Shroud Survival Flask | Base 5 min timer | Crafted at Alchemist (5 Shroud Cores) |
| Shroud Weaver Potion | +10 min timer | Alchemist (10 Shroud Cores + 3 Red Flowers) |
| Shroud Walker Boots | Immunity in shroud | Underworld boss drop |
| Elixir Well buff | +10 min timer (regional) | Activate Elixir Well |

> **Warning:** If your shroud timer reaches zero, you take 50 HP/s damage. This kills you in seconds. Always carry a backup flask.

### Shroud Cores

Shroud Cores are the most important resource in the game. They're used for:
- Upgrading your Flame Altar (levels 2–5)
- Crafting shroud survival items
- Crafting mid-to-late game weapons and armor
- Activating Ancient Spires

**How to farm Shroud Cores:**
1. Defeat shroud enemies — Fell Spitters, Shroud Stalkers, Corrupted Knights
2. Loot Elixir Well chests (guaranteed 5–8 cores each)
3. Break glowing orange shroud nodes in Shroud zones
4. Complete Vault runs (10–15 cores per run)

## Ancient Spires (Fast Travel)

There are **15 Ancient Spires** across Embervale. Each must be climbed once to activate it as a fast travel point.

### Spire Locations by Biome

| Biome | Number of Spires | Notable Spire |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| Springlands | 3 | Central Spire (starter, easiest climb) |
| Revelwood | 3 | Revelwood Spire (requires double jump) |
| Nomad Highlands | 3 | Highlands Spire (wind puzzles) |
| Kindlewastes | 2 | Desert Spire (heat resistance climb) |
| Albaneve Summits | 2 | Summit Spire (cold resistance climb) |
| The Underworld | 2 | Underworld Spire (shroud timer climb) |

### Climbing Tips

1. **Check your stamina** — Each Spire climb requires 15+ blocks of stamina for grapple points. Bring stamina food.
2. **Find the grapple points** — Each Spire has glowing orange grapple hooks at intervals. Miss one and you'll need to backtrack.
3. **Break the crystal at the top** — The Spire crystal must be struck to activate it. This also heals you and removes all status effects.
4. **Spires are level-gated** — A Level 25 Spire has Level 25 enemies. Don't attempt Summit Spire at Level 12.
5. **Use the map** — Unactivated Spires show as gray icons on the map. Fast travel between activated Spires is free.

## Elixir Wells

Elixir Wells are points of interest that provide **regional shroud protection** buffs.

### Elixir Well Mechanics

Each Elixir Well can be activated once per character. Activation:
1. Requires 20 Shroud Cores as fuel
2. Grants +10 minutes of shroud time in that region
3. Unlocks a fast travel point at the well
4. Spawns a chest with rare loot (guaranteed Hollow Core, random weapon blueprint)

### All Elixir Well Locations

| Well | Biome | Coordinates (approximate) |
|------|-------|--------------------------|
| Springlands Well | Central Springlands | Near Longkeep ruins |
| Revelwood Well | Western Revelwood | Near the river bend |
| Nomad Well | Eastern Nomad Highlands | Inside the canyon |
| Kindlewastes Well | Northern Kindlewastes | Near Oasis |
| Summit Well | Base of Albaneve Summits | Before the Glacier Temple |
| Underworld Well | Underworld entrance | After the Shroud Caves |

## Hidden Secrets & Easter Eggs

### Floating Island (Revelwood)
- **Location:** High above the eastern Revelwood forest
- **Requirement:** 20+ max stamina (for grapple chain), double jump skill
- **Loot:** Unique sword blueprint "Skyreaver" — 38 base damage, +15% airborne damage
- **Bonus:** 3 Rare Chests with guaranteed Hollow Cores

### Underground Lake (Springlands)
- **Location:** Deep well near Longkeep (look for the stone well with blue glow)
- **Entrance:** Drop into the well, swim down through the underwater tunnel (30s swim)
- **Loot:** Rare fish (Mana Fish — cook for +20 Mana buff), hidden chest with 500 coins
- **Secret:** Break the false wall at the back of the cave for an Ancient Tome (+1 skill point)

### Abandoned Library (Kindlewastes)
- **Location:** Ruined structure in the eastern desert
- **Entrance:** Clear the rubble blocking the main door (requires explosive)
- **Puzzle:** Align 3 book shelves by pulling levers in the correct order (top > bottom > middle)
- **Loot:** 3 Skill Point Tomes, Lore scroll explaining the Fell Curse
- **Boss:** Librarian Golem (Level 22, weak to pickaxe damage)

### Sunken Temple (Nomad Highlands)
- **Location:** Oasis lake in the southern highlands
- **Entrance:** Dive to the bottom, find the hidden tunnel behind the coral
- **Puzzle:** Light 4 braziers in order of the zodiac symbols
- **Loot:** Water Breathing Amulet (permanent underwater breathing), Neptune's Trident (spear, 42 damage)

### Dragon's Nest (Albaneve Summits)
- **Location:** Hidden cave behind the frozen waterfall at Dragon's Peak base
- **Requirement:** Fire resistance potion (warmth buff)
- **Loot:** Dragon Egg (cosmetic base decoration), Dragon Scale x5
- **Danger:** Sleeping dragon block — wake it and you must fight a mini-boss

## Exploration Tips & Tricks

1. **Mark your map** — Use custom map markers for unopened chests, secret entrances, and boss locations. You can place up to 50 markers.
2. **Always grapple** — The grappling hook isn't just for Spires. Use it to reach high ledges, cross chasms, and escape combat.
3. **Double jump skill** — This is the single most useful exploration skill. Unlock it at level 5.
4. **Glide everywhere** — The glider lets you cross enormous distances. Glide from Spire tops to reach otherwise inaccessible areas.
5. **Look for orange glows** — Hidden chests, Shroud Cores, and collectibles all emit a faint orange glow that's visible from 50m away.
6. **Night exploration is dangerous** — Enemy spawn rates double at night, and new enemy types (Banshees, Wraiths) only appear after dark.
7. **Sound cues** — Unique audio cues play near secrets. A faint humming means a hidden chest is nearby. A bell sound means a skill point tome.
8. **Verticality matters** — Embervale is a vertical world. Always look up — many secrets are on rooftops, cliff edges, and floating platforms.
9. **Horse is faster than glider on flat ground** — Tame a horse in Revelwood for ground travel. Use glider in mountains.
10. **The Underworld has no map** — Once you enter the Underworld, your map stops working. Bring a torch and remember landmarks.
