# 🗺️ Enshrouded — Map & Exploration Guide

*Last updated: July 2026 | Game version: 1.0+*

---

Enshrouded's world is vast, seamless, and divided into distinct biomes — from the lush Springlands to the scorching Kindlewastes. Each region offers different challenges, resources, and rewards. This guide covers every region, the Shroud system, fast travel, loot hotspots, and the optimal exploration route.

## 🌿 Springlands (Level 1–7)

The starting region. Green rolling hills, ruined villages, and the first Shroud zones.

### Key Landmarks

| Landmark | Coordinates | Significance |
|:---------|:------------|:-------------|
| Flame Sanctuary | (200, 150) | First altar location, tutorial area |
| Ancient Spire (Springlands) | (350, 200) | Fast travel hub — activate ASAP |
| Copper Mines | (400, 250) | Early copper farming (respawning nodes) |
| Fell Wytch Lair | (250, 350) | First boss (Lv. 7) |
| Hollow Halls Entrance | (500, 150) | Dungeon entrance (Lv. 10) |
| Revelwood Border | (600, 300) | Transition zone to Revelwood |
| Carpenter's Villa | (300, 200) | Carpenter NPC rescue location |
| Farmer's Homestead | (200, 300) | Farmer NPC rescue location |

### Resources

| Resource | Location | Yield per Node |
|:---------|:----------|:---------------|
| Copper Ore | Copper Mines, surface nodes | 3–6 |
| Tin Ore | Stream beds, caves | 2–4 |
| Stone | Everywhere | 4–8 |
| Clay | River banks, ponds | 3–5 |
| Plant Fiber | All grassy areas | 2–4 |
| Shroud Essence | Shroud zones, enemies | 1–3 |

### Points of Interest (POIs)

- **Fell Wytch Lair** — Southern Springlands. Ruined temple with purple vines. Boss: The Fell Wytch.
- **Underground Bunker** — Southwest corner. Hidden room with chests containing bronze bars.
- **Fell Thunderbrute Arena** — Eastern edge near Revelwood border. Open clearing with copper deposits.
- **Elixir Well** — Central Springlands. Unlocks shroud survival upgrades.

### Recommended Exploration Order

1. Awaken at Flame Sanctuary → activate Ancient Spire (north)
2. Explore northeast → find Carpenter NPC
3. Head southeast → clear Copper Mines
4. Visit Farmer's Homestead (southwest)
5. Challenge Fell Wytch Lair (south)
6. Cross eastern border into Revelwood

## 🌳 Revelwood (Level 6–12)

A dense, dark forest with towering trees, deep lakes, and underground caves. The first major difficulty spike.

### Key Landmarks

| Landmark | Coordinates | Significance |
|:---------|:------------|:-------------|
| Ancient Spire (Revelwood) | (700, 200) | Zone fast travel point |
| Hollow Halls | (650, 150) | The Hollow One boss dungeon |
| Alchemist Tower | (750, 300) | Alchemist NPC rescue |
| Sunken Temple | (800, 250) | Water-filled dungeon, iron rewards |
| Revelwood Lake | (700, 350) | Central water feature, fishing |
| Nomad Highlands Pass | (800, 500) | Northern exit to Nomad Highlands |

### Resources

| Resource | Location | Yield per Node |
|:---------|:----------|:---------------|
| Iron Ore | Revelwood caves, mine shafts | 4–8 |
| Fungi | Dark areas, cave floors | 2–4 |
| Resin | Pine trees | 1–3 |
| Water | Lakes, rivers | Unlimited (bucket) |
| Shroud Cores | Shroud enemies | 1–2 |

### Exploration Challenges

- **Dense tree cover** — easy to get lost. Use the Ancient Spire as a navigational reference.
- **Water zones** — swimming is slow. Build bridges or use the glider to cross lakes.
- **Night enemies** are stronger. Fel bats and wraiths spawn after dark.
- **Poison pools** in the eastern swamp area. Craft Poison Resistance potions.

### Hidden Areas

| Area | Entrance | Reward |
|:-----|:---------|:-------|
| Underground Crystal Cave | Behind waterfall (central Revelwood) | Rare gems, iron bars |
| Hollow Halls Secret Room | Break wall behind boss arena | Legendary weapon schematic |
| Sunken Temple Depths | Dive through underwater tunnel | Ancient Core, 3 chests |
| Alchemist's Basement | Trapdoor under rug in tower | Recipe: Elixir of Shroud Resistance |

## 🏔️ Nomad Highlands (Level 12–18)

A high-elevation mesa region with canyons, plateaus, and strong winds. Mid-game challenge zone.

### Key Landmarks

| Landmark | Coordinates | Significance |
|:---------|:------------|:-------------|
| Ancient Spire (Nomad) | (900, 600) | Zone fast travel point |
| Drowned Temple | (950, 650) | Wraith of the Deep boss |
| Hunter's Lodge | (850, 550) | Hunter NPC rescue |
| Windy Pass | (900, 700) | Connects to Kindlewastes |
| Canyons of the Lost | (1000, 600) | Maze-like canyon, rich loot |

### Resources

| Resource | Location | Yield per Node |
|:---------|:----------|:---------------|
| Limestone | Mesa cliffs | 4–8 |
| Iron Ore | Deep caves, canyon walls | 4–6 |
| Silver Ore | Rare nodes, canyons | 2–4 |
| Feathers | Bird enemies | 2–3 |
| Dye Plants | Mesa plateaus | 1–2 |

### Environmental Hazards

- **High winds** — glider is risky. Use caution when flying between mesas.
- **Long falls** — mesas are tall. One wrong step = fall damage death.
- **Limited water** — bring water bottles or locate the few springs.
- **Tornadoes** — rare weather event, deals damage and throws you off mesas.

### Recommended Route

1. Enter from Revelwood via the northern pass
2. Activate Ancient Spire immediately (central mesa)
3. Rescue Hunter at the lodge (due west)
4. Clear Canyons of the Lost (east) for loot
5. Challenge Drowned Temple (southeast) for Wraith of the Deep
6. Prepare for Kindlewastes — craft Steel gear

### Heat Mechanics

| Heat Level | Effect | Duration | Counter |
|:-----------|:-------|:---------|:--------|
| Mild | +1 thirst per minute | Constant | Water bottle |
| Moderate | -1 HP/sec in direct sun | Daytime | Shade structures, heat potion |
| Severe | -3 HP/sec | Midday (12:00–14:00) | Full shade + heat potion |
| Lethal | -10 HP/sec | Near lava | Stay away or use fire resist gear |

> 💡 **Tip:** Travel at night or during cloudy weather. The desert is much more survivable after dark. Set up a forward base at the Oasis before deep exploration.

## 🌑 Blackmire (Level 15–20)

A small, treacherous swamp sub-zone accessible from the southeastern Kindlewastes. High difficulty but great loot.

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|:--------|:------------|
| **Size** | Small (1/4 of Springlands) |
| **Visibility** | Very low — constant fog |
| **Water** | Poisonous — standing in it deals damage over time |
| **Enemies** | Swamp wraiths, mutated creatures |
| **Unique Drops** | Shadow Essence, rare alchemy ingredients |

### Survival Tips

1. **Stick to the raised path** — stepping in swamp water triggers poison damage
2. **Use torches** — the fog reduces visibility to 10m without light
3. **Bridge sections** are destructible — don't run across them mid-fight
4. **The center has the best loot** — a buried chest with legendary-quality items

## ⚡ Fast Travel System

### Ancient Spires

Ancient Spires are the primary fast travel network. Each region has one central spire.

| Spire | Region | Activation | Travel Cost |
|:------|:-------|:-----------|:------------|
| Springlands Spire | Central Springlands | Free (unlocked) | Free |
| Revelwood Spire | Central Revelwood | 10 Shroud Essence | Free after activation |
| Nomad Highlands Spire | Central mesa | 25 Shroud Essence | Free after activation |
| Kindlewastes Spire | Central desert | 50 Shroud Essence | Free after activation |

**Activation:** Pay Shroud Essence once per spire. After activation, travel between any activated spires is **free**.

### Flame Altar Fast Travel

Your Flame Altar acts as a personal fast travel point:

- **Main altar:** Always available for recall (no cost)
- **Secondary altars:** Travel to any owned altar for 5 Shroud Essence
- **Friend's altars** (co-op): Travel to group members' altars for 10 Shroud Essence

### Glider Travel

Not strictly fast travel, but the **glider** is the fastest way to cross long distances:

| Glider Type | Unlock | Speed | Distance |
|:------------|:-------|:------|:---------|
| Basic Glider | Craft: 8 Wood + 4 String | Slow | 100m |
| Improved Glider | Crafter: 10 Wood + 6 Silk | Medium | 200m |
| Advanced Glider | Blacksmith: 6 Iron + 4 Fabric | Fast | 350m |
| Master Glider | Endgame: 8 Steel + 6 Silk | Very Fast | 500m |

**Glider tip:** Launch from high ground (Ancient Spire top, mesa cliffs) for maximum distance.

## 🕳️ The Shroud — Complete Guide

The Shroud is a purple toxic mist that covers certain areas of the map. It's both a hazard and a resource source.

### Shroud Zones

| Zone Type | Danger Level | Timer | Loot Quality |
|:----------|:-------------|:------|:-------------|
| Surface Shroud (Green) | Low | None | Common |
| Surface Shroud (Purple) | Medium | 5 min | Uncommon |
| Deep Shroud (Subterranean) | High | 10 min | Rare |
| Elite Shroud (endgame) | Very High | 8 min | Legendary |

### Shroud Timer Mechanics

- **Timer starts** when you enter a Shroud zone (shown on screen)
- **Timer counts down** — when it hits zero, you take rapid damage (5 HP/sec)
- **Timer resets** when you leave the Shroud zone
- **Elixir upgrade** extends timer: +2 min per upgrade level
- **Shroud Essence** can be consumed for +30s of safe time

### Shroud Timer Upgrades

| Upgrade Level | Total Timer | Cost |
|:--------------|:------------|:-----|
| Base | 5:00 | None |
| +1 | 7:00 | 10 Shroud Essence |
| +2 | 9:00 | 25 Shroud Essence + 3 Ancient Cores |
| +3 | 12:00 | 50 Shroud Essence + 5 Ancient Cores |
| +4 | 15:00 | 100 Shroud Essence + 10 Ancient Cores |

### Shroud Loot

| Item | Source | Use |
|:-----|:-------|:-----|
| Shroud Essence | Shroud plants, enemies | Crafting, timer extension |
| Shroud Core | Shroud walkers, elite enemies | Weapon upgrades |
| Ancient Core | Deep Shroud chests | Endgame crafting |
| Shadow Bloom | Shroud flora (rare) | Alchemy ingredients |
| Corrupted Gem | Elite Shroud enemies | Jewelry crafting |

### Shroud Survival Checklist

1. Upgrade your **Shroud Timer** at the Elixir Well first
2. Always carry **20+ Shroud Essence** for emergency timer resets
3. Mark the **Shroud edge** with map markers before entering
4. **Never fight bosses** near the timer limit
5. **Partner up** — one player can reset the other's timer by dropping Shroud Essence

### Marker Types

| Marker | Icon | Use Case |

| 🟢 Resource | Pickaxe | Mark resource clusters |

| ⚪ Base | House | Your Flame Altar locations |

### Best Practices

1. **Color-code by region** — use 🔴 for Springlands, 🔵 for Revelwood, 🟣 for Kindlewastes
2. **Mark every Ancient Spire** immediately — saves hours of backtracking
3. **Mark Shroud zone edges** — know exactly where the safety boundary is
4. **Mark boss arenas before you're ready** — you'll come back later
5. **Use text labels** (enabled via map interface) for complex notes like "Iron ore, 10 nodes here"
