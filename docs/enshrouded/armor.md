# 🛡️ Enshrouded — Armor & Gear Guide

*Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 1.0+*

---

Armor in Enshrouded is more than just damage reduction — each set provides **set bonuses**, **elemental resistances**, and **stat modifiers** that define your playstyle. Armor is crafted at the **Blacksmith** using metal bars, leather, and special materials found throughout the world. This guide covers every armor set, individual piece stats, material costs, and upgrade requirements.

## Recommended Armor Progression Path

1. **Rag → Scrap** (Lv. 1–4) — Craft Scrap immediately. Rag is only for the first 30 minutes.
2. **Scrap → Bronze** (Lv. 5–8) — Upgrade to Bronze when you have 30+ Bronze bars. Skip upgrading Scrap beyond +2.
3. **Bronze → Iron** (Lv. 9–14) — Iron is the first "serious" armor set. Upgrade to +3 before attempting The Hollow One.
4. **Iron → Steel** (Lv. 15–20) — Steel is the pre-endgame set. Required for Kindlewastes. Upgrade to +4.
5. **Steel → Legendary** (Lv. 21–25) — Legendary is expensive but worth it for the Mad Redeemer. Farm Crimson Guards for materials.

**Hybrid Option:** Mix and match sets for specific resistances. For example, Steel Chest + Iron Legs + Bronze Boots gives reasonable protection with lower material cost.
