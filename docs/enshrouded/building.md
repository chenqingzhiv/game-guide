## Structural Integrity

Each material has support value and weight. Dirt: 3 floors. Wood: 5. Stone: 8. Steel: 12. Obsidian: 16.

Each floor reduces support by 50%.

## Room Bonuses

Bedroom: +20% rest. Workshop: faster crafting. Kitchen: +25% food duration. Smithy: +15% armor durability. Laboratory: +20% potion duration.

## Base Defense

2-block walls stop ground enemies. Spike traps at entries. Lighting reduces spawns. Elevated bases on cliffs avoid ground enemies.
