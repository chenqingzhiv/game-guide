# ⚔️ Enshrouded — Weapons & Equipment Data

*Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 1.0+*

---

Enshrouded offers a deep weapon system with **6 weapon classes**, each with unique movesets, special attacks, and upgrade paths. Weapons drop from bosses, loot chests, or are crafted at the **Blacksmith**. Every weapon can be upgraded to higher quality levels (Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary) using runes and metal bars.

## Weapon Stat Glossary

- **Base Damage:** Range shown for Common quality. Upgrading increases damage by ~15% per tier.
- **Attack Speed:** Lower = faster. 0.7s is the fastest melee speed in the game.
- **Stagger:** Higher stagger means enemies flinch more often, interrupting their attacks.
- **Crit Chance:** Critical hits deal 2x damage. Can stack with elemental effects.
- **Elemental Effects:** Fire (burn DOT), Frost (slow + frostbite DOT), Poison (DOT), Lightning (stun chance).
