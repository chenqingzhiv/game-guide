## Weapon Classes

Swords (balanced), Axes (high damage, slow), Bows (ranged), Wands (magic ranged), Staves (heavy magic).

## Best Weapons by Tier

Early: Rusted Sword, Hand Axe, Shortbow.
Mid: Iron Longsword, War Axe, Recurve Bow.
Late: Hollow Knight Blade, Fell Cleaver, Fell Stinger.
Endgame: Fell Dragonfang, Doom's Edge, Eternal Flame staff.

## Upgrade System

Tiers 1-4 at Blacksmith. Each tier: +25% damage + stat bonus.

Materials: Resin > Metal Bars > Hollow Core > Dragon Scale.
