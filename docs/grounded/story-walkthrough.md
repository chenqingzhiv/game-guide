## Lab Order

Hedge > Pond > Haze > Black Anthill > Mant > Undershed Lab (final).

~30 hours main story. ~60 hours with optional bosses.

## Boss Fights

The Mant: Upper yard shed. Phase 1: Normal. Phase 2: Adds (mant nymphs). Phase 3: Enrage (double speed). Phase 4: Ground pound (jump to avoid).

Broodmother (final): Full strength version. Hardest fight in game.

## Endgame

After killing Mant, its chip unlocks Undershed Lab. Solve 4 lasers + 3 keycards. Beat Broodmother. Machine repaired - can shrink/grow at will (post-game).
