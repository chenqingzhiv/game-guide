# Grounded Equipment Guide

In Grounded, your gear determines whether you feast or become a spider's lunch. With three tiers of armor and weapons, plus a robust upgrade system through science, this guide covers every piece of equipment and optimal loadouts for every stage.

---

## Equipment Tier System

| Tier | Science Cost | Materials | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Tier 1** | 100–200 Raw Science | Basic bug parts | Early exploration, first bases |
| **Tier 2** | 500–1,000 Raw Science | Advanced bug parts | Mid-game, Hedge/Pond/Haze |
| **Tier 3** | 2,000–5,000 Raw Science | Upper yard bug parts | Endgame, Mant/Broodmother |

> **Progression Tip:** Don't skip to Tier 3 immediately. Each tier's armor set has a set bonus that synergizes with specific playstyles. Mixing tiers is often optimal.

#### Set Analysis

| Set | Best For | Why |
|-----|----------|-----|
| **Clover** | Healers, early game survival | Extra healing means fewer deaths. Clover is free (craft from grass) — great starter set. |
| **Grub** | Stamina builds, two-handed weapons | The stamina bonus lets you swing heavy weapons 2–3 more times before tiring. |
| **Acorn** | Tanks, first spider fights | Highest Tier 1 defense. The +2 armor from the set bonus stacks with everything. |
| **Red Ant** | Hauling, combat | +1 hauling means you can carry a weed stem without Grass Chestplate. +1 attack is a flat damage increase. |

**Tier 1 Recommendation:** Acorn set for defense, Red Ant set for general utility. Grub set for stamina-heavy builds.

## Equipment Tips & Tricks

1. **Armor doesn't stack via multiple pieces of the same set** — The set bonus activates at 3+ pieces. You need 3 different pieces (hood, chest, legs) for the bonus.
2. **Mix Tier 2 and Tier 3** — Wearing 2 Roly Poly pieces + 1 Ladybug piece gives you most of the tank bonus plus the healing. Experiment.
3. **Weapon upgrade > new weapon** — A fully upgraded Tier 2 weapon often out-damages a base Tier 3 weapon. Upgrade before replacing.
4. **Mutations are equipment** — You can equip 3 mutations (5 endgame). Treat them as part of your loadout. Change them per fight.
5. **Durability matters** — All equipment has durability. Carry repair glue (2 Sap, 1 Insect Part) for mid-expedition repairs.
6. **Elemental matchups** — Mint > Ladybugs/Spicy Coaltana. Spicy > Spiders/Fire Ants. Salty > Salt enemies/Sour. Sour > Robots/Ox beetles.
7. **Trinkets** — You can equip 1 trinket (from bosses/labs) for additional bonuses like +HP regen or +block efficiency.
8. **Blocking > dodging** — Perfect blocks (timed right before hit) negate all damage and stagger the enemy. Practice on weevils.
9. **Web armor works underwater** — Koi armor doesn't slow swimming. Spider armor doesn't slow climbing. Read armor descriptions.
10. **Gliders stack with Moth armor** — Web glider + Moth set = you can cross the entire upper yard in one glide.
