## Tier 1 Armor

Clover (+1 heal), Grub (+2 stam), Acorn (+2 armor), Red Ant (+1 attack, hauling).

## Tier 2 Armor

Spider (+3 attack, poison), Ladybug (+3 armor, heal on block), Koi (+3 stam, perfect block), Bee (+2 attack, crit).

## Tier 3 Armor

Fire Ant (+4 attack, corrosion), Roly Poly (+5 armor, taunt), Moth (+4 stam, flight), Mant (+5 attack, execution).

## Best Weapons

Early: Red Ant Club (high stun).
Mid: Mosquito Needle (life steal), Salt Morning Star (salty).
Late: Coaltana (fire), Mint Mace (minty), Rapier of Life Steal.
