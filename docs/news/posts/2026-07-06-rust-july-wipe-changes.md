## Rust July 2026 Wipe — Full Patch Notes

Facepunch Studios' monthly forced wipe for **July 2026** is live, bringing a brand-new monument, sweeping weapon balance changes, and an Underwater Labs loot table overhaul. The update also introduces new deployables, a fishing expansion, and quality-of-life improvements to the tech tree system.

### New Monument: Abandoned Refinery

The headline addition is the **Abandoned Refinery**, a large industrial monument located in the arid biome (desert areas).

**Layout:**
- A multi-story oil processing facility with exposed pipework, cooling towers, and a central cracking tower.
- Three wings: East (administrative offices with filing cabinets), West (pump control room with red card puzzle), and North (storage tanks with blue card puzzle).
- The central tower requires a **green card** to enter and contains a puzzle that rewards a **red keycard** plus a chance at elite-tier loot.

**Loot table:**

| Tier | Containers | Items |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Common | Toolboxes, Barrels | Scrap, low-tier components |
| Uncommon | Green crates (4) | Medical supplies, ammo, gear |
| Rare | Blue crates (2) | Semi-auto rifles, SMGs, hazmat suits |
| Elite | Red crate (1) | Assault rifles, metal facemask, M39 |

**Puzzles:**
- **East Wing keypad** — 4-digit code found on a whiteboard in the admin office.
- **West Wing pump sequence** — Three valves must be turned in a specific order (displayed on a schematic near the control room).
- **Central tower timer** — After swiping the green card, players have 90 seconds to navigate a maze of storage tanks before the door locks.

### Weapon Balance Overhaul

This wipe introduces the most significant weapon rebalance since the recoil update. Facepunch has adjusted damage profiles, fire rates, and recoil patterns across 14 weapons.

**Assault Rifles:**

- **AK-47** — Damage 45 → 42. Fire rate 350 → 340 RPM. Horizontal recoil tightened by 8%. The AK still dominates but is slightly less oppressive at medium range.
- **LR-300** — Damage 38 → 40. Recoil recovery speed increased by 15%. The LR-300 now rewards tap-firing at range.
- **M39** — Damage 52 → 48. Aim-down-sights time reduced by 0.1s. M39 remains the top DMR but the nerf brings it inline with the semi-auto rifle.

**SMGs:**

- **MP5** — Damage 20 → 22. Magazine 30 → 36. The MP5 gets a welcome buff to compete with the Thompson.
- **Thompson** — Damage 23 → 21. Recoil increased slightly. Thompson sprayers will need to control their bursts.
- **Custom SMG** — Unchanged. The cheap SMG keeps its role as an early-game powerhouse.

**Shotguns:**

- **Pump Shotgun** — Reload speed increased by 0.3s per shell. Slug damage increased by 10%. Pump lovers finally get some love.
- **Double Barrel** — Damage unchanged. Spread tightened. Effective range extended from 8m to 10m.

**Longbows/Crossbows:**

- **Hunting Bow** — Draw speed increased by 15%.
- **Crossbow** — Reload time 2.5s → 2.8s. Meant to encourage choosing the crossbow only when you have a clear shot.

### Underwater Labs Rework

Underwater Labs get a significant overhaul:

- **New layout variants** — 3 new lab layout configurations have been added to the procedural generation pool. Expect different room sequences and loot paths.
- **Loot table nerf** — Blue crate scrap amounts 150–400 → 100–300. Red crate scrap 300–600 → 200–450. Elite crates unchanged.
- **New hazard: Pressure Leaks** — Some rooms now have slow gas leaks that deal damage over time. Plug the leak with a welding torch (tool) to stop it. Leaks are marked with visible steam particles.
- **Floating loot** — Crates now float gradually upward when opened underwater, reducing frantically clicking to grab everything.

### Fishing Expansion

The fishing system gets new content:

- **Tropical Fish** — New rare fish type found only in the warm-water areas near the new Refinery monument. Can be cooked for 30% more health than regular fish.
- **Legendary Rod** — A craftable fishing rod (requires 10 scrap + 50 wood + 2 metal pipes at a tier-2 workbench) that reduces reel-in time by 30%.
- **Fishing trophies** — Decorative mounted fish can be placed on walls. Three rarities: Common (trout), Rare (tropical), Legendary (void fish).
