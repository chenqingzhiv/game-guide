## Sons of the Forest July 2026 Patch — Building Expansion

Endnight Games has released a major content patch for **Sons of the Forest**, delivering over **30 new building pieces**, a substantial **creature AI pathfinding overhaul**, and **50+ bug fixes** addressing long-standing community complaints. The patch is available now on Steam and brings the game to version 1.18.

### New Building Pieces — Creative Mode Expansion

The centerpiece of this patch is the expanded building system. The construction hammer now offers:

**Structural pieces:**

- **Stone Foundation** — A more durable alternative to wood. Requires 6 stones per foundation. Provides 2x the HP of a wooden foundation.
- **Stone Wall** — As above, 2x HP. Can support heavier roof loads than wood.
- **Balcony Railing** — Decorative railing that snaps to the edge of any floor.
- **Corner Post** — Reinforced corner log that increases structural integrity.
- **Archway Frame** — A rounded doorway that allows taller characters (and Kelvin) to pass through without bumping their heads.
- **Lattice Wall** — A half-height wall with gaps. Good for lookout spots or ventilation.
- **Dome Roof** — A curved roof piece constructed from multiple interlocking logs. Provides excellent rain protection and looks impressive.

**Furniture and decorations:**

- **Bookshelf** — Functional storage with 9 inventory slots. Items placed on shelves are physics-enabled and can be knocked off by explosions.
- **Rug Kit** — Craftable from 3 hides (any animal). Multiple sizes (2x2, 2x3, 3x3). Reduces footstep noise while walking on it.
- **Wall Torch Sconce** — Wall-mounted torch holder. Burns fuel slower than a standing torch.
- **Armor Stand** — Display your crafted armor sets. Right-click to equip/unequip.
- **Fishing Rod Rack** — Holds up to 3 fishing rods.
- **Taxidermy Mount** — Place a crafted animal head on the wall using a taxidermy kit (crafted from 2 hides + 1 bone + 1 resin).

**Defensive structures:**

- **Spike Wall (Stone)** — Stone spike wall that is 3x more durable than the wood variant.
- **Trap Door** — A floor panel that opens downward. Enemies walking over it fall into a pit below.
- **Drawbridge** — A large (2-tile-wide) bridge that can be raised and lowered via a rope mechanism. Requires 20 logs and 10 ropes.
- **Palisade Gate** — A reinforced gate for your outer wall. Takes 15 logs to construct and swings open on hinges.

### Creature AI Overhaul

The patch addresses one of the most common complaints: creature pathfinding.

**Pathfinding improvements:**
- Cannibals and mutants now navigate around building foundations instead of walking through them.
- Enemies can climb ladders and stairs more reliably.
- Creatures will break down doors instead of clipping through walls to reach you.
- Fliers (Puffton) have improved aerial pathfinding and won't get stuck in tree canopies.

**Combat AI changes:**
- Cannibals now use flanking tactics when attacking a fortified base.
- Mutants prioritize breaking defensive structures over chasing the player.
- Enemies retreat more intelligently when injured (move behind cover, use terrain).
- New "Alarm" behavior — a cannibal that spots you may blow a horn, alerting nearby patrols within 200 meters.

### Bug Fixes (Selected)

The patch fixes over 50 bugs. Highlights:

- Fixed Kelvin getting permanently stuck on rock formations (the most-reported bug on Steam).
- Fixed Virginia's pathfinding near water — she no longer drowns in knee-deep streams.
- Fixed multiplayer desync involving building destruction.
- Fixed log sleds despawning when loaded across save-game versions.
- Fixed flashlight flickering after reloading a save.
- Fixed GPS locator map markers disappearing after relaunching.
- Fixed gold mask not appearing in cutscenes.
- Fixed hanging meat not preserving correctly in high humidity areas.

### Quality of Life

- **Quick-build mode** — Hold the build hotkey to place blueprints without opening the book.
- **Increased log storage** — Log sled capacity increased from 8 to 12 logs.
- **Inventory sorting** — Press Middle Mouse to auto-sort your inventory by category.
- **Flashlight zoom** — Flashlight now has a zoom toggle (hold RMB) that narrows the beam for distance spotting.
