## Palworld Sakurajima DLC — Full Breakdown

Pocketpair has launched the **Sakurajima DLC**, the first major paid expansion for Palworld. Set on a cherry-blossom-covered archipelago, the DLC adds **16 new Pals**, a new **dragon element type**, Japanese-inspired building sets, and a comprehensive **combat balance overhaul** that affects the entire game.

### The Sakurajima Archipelago

The new island, roughly **40% the size of the original map**, is located to the northeast and accessible via flight Pal or fast-travel from any beacon once DLC owners activate the quest.

**Biome zones:**

- **Cherry Blossom Groves** — Rolling hills covered in pink petal trees. Passive resource nodes drop Sakura Wood and Petal Fibers.
- **Bamboo Thickets** — Dense bamboo forests with hidden cave entrances. Home to the new bamboo-themed Pals.
- **Hot Spring Valleys** — Geothermal areas with healing hot springs that slowly restore HP while standing in them. Thermal vents provide free energy via the new Geothermal Generator.
- **Oni Mountain** — A volcanic peak at the island's center. The highest-level area, home to the DLC's endgame boss.

### Dragon Element

The **Dragon element** is super-effective against Dark and Ghost types, weak to Ice. Dragon Pals have a unique synergy bonus — when two Dragon-type Pals are deployed at the same base, all damage-dealing abilities from both Pals get a 15% boost.

### Japanese Building Set

The DLC includes a full **Japanese architecture building set**:

- Wooden walls with paper screen variants
- Curved tile roofing in multiple colors (red, blue, black)
- Shoji doors and sliding screens
- Lantern light fixtures (decorative and functional)
- Zen garden decoration kit (sand, rocks, moss)
- Bamboo fence and gate
- Torii gate (large buildable structure)

### Combat Balance Overhaul

Alongside the DLC, Pocketpair released a **free patch** that reworks combat across the entire game:

**Stat rebalancing:**

- Player HP scaling adjusted — lower base HP but higher per-level gain (total HP at level 50 unchanged, but early-game is slightly harder).
- Pal defensive stats increased by 10% across the board.
- Elemental effectiveness multipliers changed from 2x to 1.75x (reducing one-shot potential while keeping type advantage meaningful).

**New mechanics:**

- **Guard Break** — Heavy attacks from large Pals now stagger enemies and temporarily break their guard.
- **Elemental Synergy** — Deploying two Pals of matching elements in combat activates a passive aura effect (e.g., two Fire Pals: nearby enemies take chip burn damage).
- **Stamina for Pals** — Pals now have a stamina bar that drains when using charged attacks. Running out of stamina causes a brief stun.

### Pricing and Availability

The Sakurajima DLC is priced at:

- **$14.99** — Standalone DLC
- **$24.99** — DLC + soundtrack bundle
- **$39.99** — Deluxe edition (DLC + soundtrack + digital artbook + exclusive Tanuking in-game hat)

Available on **Steam, Xbox Series X|S, and Game Pass** (DLC purchase required for Game Pass users — the base game is included in the subscription).
