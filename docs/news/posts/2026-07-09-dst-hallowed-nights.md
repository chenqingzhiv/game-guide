## Don't Starve Together: Hallowed Nights 2026

Klei Entertainment's annual **Hallowed Nights** event has returned to the Constant, and this year's iteration is the most content-rich yet. Running from now through **November 10**, the event introduces a new boss, over 30 character skins, exclusive recipes, and a time-limited lore questline that expands the game's cryptic narrative.

### The Umbral Hound — New Boss Encounter

The headline addition is **The Umbral Hound**, a shadow-infused variant of the classic Varg that prowls the Constant during Hallowed Nights.

**Boss mechanics:**

- **Phase 1** — The Umbral Hound moves like a standard Varg but summons shadow-hounds that deal sanity damage on hit. Players must kite carefully and manage their sanity levels.
- **Phase 2** — At 50% health, the boss's animations become erratic. It gains a teleportation attack, vanishing into shadow and reappearing behind the nearest player. A ground-slam AoE follows.
- **Phase 3** — Below 20% health, the boss enrages. Movement speed increases by 40%, attack speed doubles, and it begins draining sanity from all players within line-of-sight in a pulsing cone.

**Drops:**

- **Umbral Fang** — Used to craft the Nightmare Cleaver, a shadow-aligned weapon that deals bonus damage to nightmare creatures.
- **Shadow Husk** — A chest armor that reduces sanity loss from darkness and monster auras.
- **Sketch: Spooky Effigy** — A buildable decoration that whispers lore tidbits at dusk.

### 30+ New Skins

The Hallowed Nights 2026 skin pack includes themed outfits for nearly every character:

- **Wilson** — "Mad Alchemist" suit with bubbling flask back-slot and goggles.
- **Willow** — "Wicked Witch" dress with a broom-back accessory that leaves smoke trails.
- **Wendy** — "Phantom Bride" veil that becomes translucent at night.
- **WX-78** — "Scrap Golem" chassis with glowing orange eye-slits.

Skins are obtainable through **Hallowed Nights loot chests** (earned via gameplay) or direct purchase from the in-game store.

### Exclusive Recipes and Crafting

The event adds 12 new craftable items:

- **Spooky Lantern** — A light source that cycles through three colors (orange, purple, green). Slightly brighter than a standard lantern.
- **Candy Basket** — Deployable basket that spawns trick-or-treat candy over time. Candy restores 5 HP and 10 hunger, with a small chance of a buff or debuff.
- **Effigy of Terror** — A structure that passively scares away shadow creatures within a small radius.
- **Nightmare Brew** — A crock pot recipe combining 2 berries, 1 honey, and 1 nightmare fuel. Restores 75 sanity but drains 20 HP.

### Limited-Time Lore Questline

For the first time, Hallowed Nights includes a **story-driven quest chain** that explores the origins of the Constant's shadow realm. Players must collect **Fragments of Memory** scattered across all biomes, then assemble them at a special altar found in the Ruins.

Completing the questline rewards:

- The **Shadow Walker** character title (cosmetic only).
- A **Permanent Nightmare Fuel Converter** blueprint that turns living logs into nightmare fuel.
- An exclusive **loading screen illustration** by Klei concept artist Jeff Agala.

### Tips for New Players

If you're jumping into Hallowed Nights for the first time:

1. **Stockpile nightmare fuel** — Several new recipes require it. Haunt pig heads or harvest from shadow creatures.
2. **Prepare a kiting arena** — The Umbral Hound fight demands space. Clear a flat area near your base and place campfires to manage sanity.
3. **Cook early** — The Nightmare Brew crock pot recipe is excellent for sanity management in the Ruins.
4. **Group up** — The Umbral Hound scales per-player but is much easier with a dedicated tank and a sanity-support player.
