## Stardew Valley 1.7 Update — Tropical Farm and Dedicated Servers

Eric Barone (ConcernedApe) has released **Stardew Valley 1.7**, the surprise update that adds a brand-new **Tropical Farm map**, proper **dedicated server support** for multiplayer, and an expanded **late-game content** loop for farmers who've completed the Community Center. The update is available now on PC, with console and mobile versions following within 30 days.

### New Farm Map: Tropical Island

The headline feature is a new farm map type available when starting a new save file:

**Tropical Island Farm:**
- **1.5x the size** of the standard farm layout.
- Located on a sunny island with gentle waves lapping at the shore. The farm has a **white sand beach** frontier, **palm trees** (which drop coconuts passively), and a **tidal pool** that refills with tropical fish each season.
- **Unique crops** — The tropical map has exclusive access to "Island Blend" seeds (purchased from a new NPC — see below) that grow unique crops: Pineapple (summer), Mango (summer, trellis), Papaya (fall, trellis), and Sweet Potato (fall, underground).
- **No pet** — Instead of a cat/dog, your island farm starts with a **Tortoise** companion that slowly patrols your farm and occasionally digs up artifacts.
- **Seasonal cycle** — The Tropical Island still has seasons but with milder winter (crops grow, but slower). Snow never appears on this map.

**Existing farm note:** Players cannot switch their existing farm to the Tropical Island type. It requires a new save.

### Dedicated Server Support

Multiplayer gets a massive upgrade with **dedicated server binaries**:

**How it works:**
- A standalone server executable (`StardewServer.exe` on Windows, `StardewServer` on Linux/macOS) allows hosting a persistent farm world without needing a player client logged in.
- Servers support up to **8 players** (up from 4).
- Players can join/leave freely. The server continues running and advancing time even when the host is offline.
- Server admin commands: `/time` (set time of day), `/weather` (change weather), `/money <player> <amount>` (adjust gold).
- Config file supports: max players, port, password, seed override, and "sleep requirement" (all players must sleep vs. any player can end the day).

**Mod support:**
- Servers can load SMAPI mods server-side.
- Client mods sync automatically on join (when using SMAPI's mod-sharing feature).
- A new "mods whitelist" system lets server admins control which mods are allowed.

### Late-Game Expansion

For farmers who've completed the Community Center, Ginger Island, and the Summit, 1.7 adds:

**The Underwoods:**
A new subterranean area accessible from the Secret Woods after the Summit is reached. The Underwoods is a procedurally generated mine-like zone with:
- Unique **Glowing Mushrooms** that sell for premium prices.
- **Crystal Crabs** — New enemy that drops Crystal Shells (used for crafting light sources).
- **Ancient Tree Saplings** — Rare drops that grow into fruit trees in the Underwoods (fruit sells for 3x normal price).

**New NPC — Meridith:**
A traveling botanist who appears on the Tropical Island farm after year 2. She sells:
- Island Blend Seeds (seasonal tropical crops).
- Rare fertilizers (Fish Fertilizer — increases quality chance by 25%).
- Exotic fruit tree saplings (Mango, Papaya, Breadfruit).
- Offers quests to collect 10 of each new crop for special rewards.

**New buildings:**
- **Botany Lab** — Unlocked after completing a Meridith quest chain. Enables crop mutation (cross-breed two crops to create a hybrid).
- **Windmill Mk.2** — Upgraded windmill that processes 2x faster and produces superior flour (sells for 150g).
- **Well of Wishes** — A decorative structure that, once per season, lets you make a wish for a random buff (crop speed, fishing luck, mining luck) for the next 7 days.

### Multiplayer QoL Improvements

- **Shared chests** — Place a chest and set it to "Shared" in the menu. All players on the farm can access it regardless of cabin.
- **Co-op quest tracking** — Quest progress is visible to all online players and contributions are pooled.
- **Sleep Voting UI** — A clear interface shows who is ready for bed and who is still awake.
- **Player markers on map** — See all online players' locations on the world map.
- **Item gifting UI** — When giving a gift, you see each character's liked/loved categories highlighted (if discovered).
- **Marriage improvement** — Spouses in multiplayer now interact with all players' characters, not just the player who married them.
