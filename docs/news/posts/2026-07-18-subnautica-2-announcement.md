## Subnautica 2 Announced — Deep Dive into the Frozen Frontier

Unknown Worlds Entertainment has officially announced **Subnautica 2**, the long-awaited sequel to the beloved underwater survival game. Revealed during a dedicated showcase stream, the announcement came with a **3-minute gameplay trailer**, concept art gallery, and a confirmed **Early Access launch targeting Q1 2028**.

### The Setting — Planet 4546C's Frozen Ocean

Subnautica 2 moves the action from the tropical crater of the original to an entirely new region of Planet 4546C: the **Frozen Ocean**.

**Biome highlights from the trailer:**

- **The Borealis Ice Shelf** — A vast, cracked ice sheet covering a shallow entry zone. This is the spawn area, analogous to the Safe Shallows. Thermal vents keep patches of the ocean above freezing, creating a mosaic of open water and ice ceiling.
- **Glacial Trenches** — Deep, narrow canyons where the ice shelf fractures. Bioluminescent flora line the walls. These are mid-game exploration zones with new resources and creatures.
- **The Abyssal Cryovents** — Deep-sea volcanic vents that release supercooled brine instead of heat. These form unique "brinefall" formations — inverted waterfalls that pool in frozen lakes on the seafloor. Home to the largest creatures shown in the trailer.
- **Sub-glacial Rivers** — A network of liquid-water caves running beneath the ice cap, warmed by geothermal activity. These connect biomes horizontally and serve as fast-travel corridors.
- **The Aurora Wreckage (Partial)** — Debris from the Aurora crash has drifted into the frozen region. Sections of the wreck are accessible for salvage, but many are under crushing ice pressure, requiring upgraded vehicle depth modules.

### Full Co-op Campaign

The biggest mechanical announcement is **full cooperative play** integrated directly into the story campaign:

- **Up to 4 players** can explore the frozen ocean together.
- **Shared progression** — Story milestones, blueprints, and base constructions are shared by all players in a session.
- **Vehicle seats** — The new Seatruck (returning from Below Zero) has been redesigned with dedicated passenger cockpits. The Cyclops does not return, but a new "Icebreaker" submarine serves as the endgame mobile base.
- **Revive system** — Downed players can be revived by teammates within 30 seconds, or respawn at the nearest base with item loss.
- **Persistence** — Unknown Worlds confirmed dedicated servers are being considered but not yet confirmed for launch.

### New Vehicles

The reveal showcased two new vehicles:

1. **Sea Skiff** — An early-game inflatable boat with a small cargo hold and a mounted sonar ping. Faster than the Seaglide but less maneuverable. Can be equipped with a grappling hook.
2. **Icebreaker** — The endgame submarine. A heavily-armored vessel capable of breaking through thin ice and diving to the deepest zones. Features: onboard fabricator, 6 locker storage, docking bay for a single small vehicle, and a "Thermal Lance" attachment that can drill through ice formations and harvest resources.

### Creatures Shown

The trailer gave glimpses of several new leviathan-class creatures:

- **Shardjaw** — A segmented, centipede-like leviathan that burrows through ice. Ambushes prey by punching through the ice ceiling. Estimated length: 40 meters.
- **Brine Elemental** — A translucent, gelatinous leviathan that drifts in the abyssal cryovents. It's passive unless provoked, but its body slowly deals frost damage on contact.
- **Glowcrab Swarm** — Small, bioluminescent crabs that cling to the ice ceiling. They drop a resource called "Glow Gelatin" — used in thermal insulation crafting.
- **The Cryostalker** — A leviathan that stalks the sub-glacial rivers. It uses bioluminescent lures that mimic distress calls of smaller creatures. Sight is limited; sound is its primary hunting sense (reminiscent of the Ghost Leviathan but with an auditory hunting mechanic).

### Building and Survival

Base-building returns with a **frozen-environment twist**:

- **Thermal Insulation** — All base compartments now have an insulation rating. Build with "Reinforced Glass" and "Insulated Panels" (crafted from Glow Gelatin + Titanium) to prevent heat loss.
- **Internal Heating** — Base heaters are mandatory in deeper biomes. Running out of power in an uninsulated base causes pipes to freeze and structures to crack.
- **Bioluminescent Lighting** — A new lighting system using harvested bioluminescent materials. Lures creatures, both friendly and hostile.
- **Ice Fishing** — Drop a line through a hole in the ice. Different fish appear depending on the biome above you.

### Wishlisting and Follow

### Seabase Building in the Frozen Ocean

Base building gets a frozen-environment twist with new dedicated pieces:

- **Reinforced Hull segments** — Standard compartments can crack under ice pressure below -50 meters. Reinforced hull (crafted with Titanium + Glow Gelatin + Lead) is required for deep bases.
- **Heater Core** — A new base module that radiates warmth to nearby compartments. Prevents freezing and reduces power draw compared to running multiple wall heaters.
- **Ice Dock** — A moon-pool equivalent for the Icebreaker submarine. A vertical shaft cut through the ice shelf that the submarine can ascend into for repairs.
- **Biolume Garden** — A greenhouse module that uses bioluminescent flora instead of artificial light. Requires no power but produces less food per plant.
- **Thermal Spire** — A vertical structure that taps into geothermal vents for power. More efficient than thermal plants in the original game but requires deeper placement.

### Crew Management System

Subnautica 2 introduces a light **crew management** layer for solo and co-op players:

- **NPC crew members** — Unlockable characters with unique perks that join your mission. Each has a backstory and a personal questline.
- **Roles and bonuses:**
  - **Engineer** — Reduces vehicle power consumption by 15%. Can repair vehicles faster.
  - **Biologist** — Identifies creatures on scan with additional data (diet, aggression triggers, harvestable materials).
  - **Medic** — Produces healing items passively. Reduces respawn timer by 10 seconds.
  - **Navigator** — Reveals nearby points of interest on the map. Increases vehicle speed in explored areas.
- **Crew quarters** — Buildable compartments where crew members sleep and store personal items. A cozy base keeps crew morale high, which improves perk effectiveness.

### Early Access vs Full Release

Unknown Worlds confirmed their approach to Subnautica 2's release cadence:

| Phase | Content | Timeline |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **Early Access Launch** | 4 biomes, first 3 vehicles, ~10 creature types, co-op (up to 4 players), base-building core. Main story up to the "first major reveal." | Q1 2028 |
| **Update 1 — The Abyss** | 2 new deep biomes, new leviathan, vehicle upgrade module. Story continues to mid-game. | Late 2028 |
| **Update 2 — The Archives** | Ancient alien structure exploration, new tools, lore expansion. | Mid 2029 |
| **1.0 Launch** | Full story completion, all biomes, final boss encounter, all vehicles, full creature codex. | Late 2029 |
