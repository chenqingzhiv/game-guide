## Valheim Deep North Update — The Frozen Frontier

Iron Gate Studio has released the long-awaited **Deep North** update for Valheim, adding the frozen biome to the game's world generation. The update introduces the **Frost King boss**, a complete **cold-survival rework**, new ship types designed for ice navigation, and a progression tier that bridges the Mistlands and the Ashlands. The update is available now on Steam.

### The Deep North Biome

The Deep North occupies the polar regions of Valheim's procedurally generated world — the northernmost area beyond the Mountain biome. Access requires a **Moder-powered sail** or the new Icebreaker ship.

**Biome atmosphere:**
- Constant twilight — The sun never fully rises. Ambient light is a dim blue-gray, giving the world an ethereal glow.
- **Aurora Borealis** — Dynamic aurora effects that shift colors (green, blue, purple) and cast moving light across the ice.
- **Blizzard events** — Random weather events that reduce visibility to 10 meters, drain cold resistance faster, and extinguish campfires not protected by walls.
- **Ice caves** — Procedural cave systems beneath the ice cap. Warm interior pockets with unique flora and mineral deposits.

**Resources:**

| Resource | Source | Used For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Permafrost Stone | Ice wall mining | Deep North building base |
| Frost Core | Frost Golem drops | Frost resistance gear, Frost Forge |
| Glacial Crystal | Ice cave mining | Tier 3 upgrade materials |
| Arctic Moss | Cave walls (harvest) | Healing meads, frost-resistant potions |
| Hearthwood | Frozen tree harvesting | Deep North structural wood |
| Void Ore | Boss drop + deep mining | Endgame weapons and armor |

### New Boss: The Frost King

The Deep North's boss is **The Frost King**, a colossal frozen jotun imprisoned within the **Glacial Heart** — a massive crystal formation in the center of the biome.

**Summoning:**
To summon the Frost King, players must place 10 **Frost Cores** and 5 **Glacial Crystals** at the Altar of Frost (a runestone structure located at the Glacial Heart). The altar requires three players standing on pressure plates simultaneously — making this a strongly co-op encounter.

**Boss fight mechanics:**

- **Phase 1 (100% – 60% HP):** The Frost King swings massive ice fists in sweeping arcs. Telegraph attacks with a 1-second ground crack visual. Stomp attack creates a shockwave that Freezes players who don't dodge-roll through it.
- **Phase 2 (60% – 25% HP):** The boss summons **Ice Spires** from the ground — pillars that deal damage on rise and remain as cover. It also begins a Freezing Breath attack in a 120-degree cone.
- **Phase 3 (25% – 0% HP):** The Frost King shatters its own armor, gaining speed but losing defense. It attacks in combos (3-hit sequence) and summons **Frost Wisps** — small floating adds that explode on proximity, dealing frost damage and applying a stacking slow.

**Drops:**
- **Frost King Trophy** — Mountable on a stone pedestal.
- **Glacial Heartstone** — Required for the Frost Forge (new crafting station).
- **Void Ore** — 10–15 pieces.
- **Frost King's Mantle** — A cape that provides full frost immunity and a damage reduction buff in cold biomes.

### New Crafting Station: Frost Forge

A new workbench tier unlocked with the Glacial Heartstone. The Frost Forge allows crafting:

**Weapons:**
- **Glacial Blade** — A greatsword that deals frost damage and slows enemies on hit. Requires: 20 Void Ore, 10 Glacial Crystals, 5 Linen Thread.
- **Permafrost Bow** — Arrows fired from this bow freeze the ground beneath the target, creating a slippery surface. Requires: 15 Void Ore, 10 Fine Wood, 5 Glacial Crystals.
- **Frost Shatter** — A two-handed hammer with a ground-pound special attack that creates a frost nova. Requires: 25 Void Ore, 10 Iron, 10 Glacial Crystals.

**Armor:**
- **Hearthguard Set** — Full armor set (chest, legs, helm, cape). Each piece provides cold resistance plus a set bonus: -20% stamina drain in combat. Requires: Void Ore + Permafrost Stone + Arctic Moss.
- **Arctic Light Cloak** — A light armor chest piece for mages. Increases elemental damage by 15%. Requires: 10 Void Ore, 10 Linen Thread, 5 Glacial Crystals.

### New Ships

Two new ship types handle the Deep North's icy waters:

1. **Icebreaker** — A reinforced longship with a metal-reinforced bow that breaks through thin ice. Faster than the longship in open water. Can equip a "Ram" upgrade that damages enemies on contact. Requires: 50 Iron Nails, 20 Fine Wood, 10 Hearthwood, 5 Deer Hide.
2. **Snekkja** — A small, fast scout boat designed for navigating ice channels. Shallow draft — can pass through water that would beach a karve or longship. Can equip a "Depth Finder" that warns of shallow water ahead. Requires: 30 Iron Nails, 30 Hearthwood, 10 Deer Hide.

### Cold Survival Rework

The cold system has been completely overhauled for the Deep North:

- **Frost Resistance** — Now a scaled stat. Wearing frost-resistant gear gives a resistance value (1–100). Deep North ambient cold requires at least 60 resistance to survive without damage. Blizzard conditions require 80+.
- **Core Temperature** — A new UI element shows your internal temperature. Campfires, hearths, and hot springs raise it. Cold exposure, water, and blizzards lower it. When core temperature drops to 0, you take damage over time.
- **Warming items** — Hot stone (a craftable hand-warmer), hot mead (provides temporary temperature boost), and fire arrows (deal bonus damage to frost enemies).
- **Food rebalance** — Cooked meats provide more warmth than raw. New "Heartystew" recipe (2 Arctic Moss + 1 Mushroom + 1 Honey) provides a 30-minute warmth buff.

### Progression Path

The intended progression order for Deep North:

1. Explore Deep North shores in a Snekkja, establish a forward base.
2. Mine Permafrost Stone and Frost Cores for basic gear.
3. Find and clear ice caves for Glacial Crystals.
4. Build the Frost Forge and craft mid-tier gear.
5. Summon and defeat the Frost King.
6. Craft Void Ore gear and prepare for Ashlands.
