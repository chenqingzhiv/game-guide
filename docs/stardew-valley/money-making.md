## Year 1 Money

Spring: Fish in mountain lake. Plant strawberries at Egg Festival.
Summer: Blueberries (repeat harvest). Start preserving jars.
Fall: Cranberries (repeat harvest). Kegs if available.
Winter: Focus on mining + greenhouse if unlocked.

## Keg Empire

1 keg per 2 crops. 10 kegs = 200 crops.
Ancient Fruit Wine: 1650g per bottle.
Starfruit Wine: 2250g.
Aged in cellar to iridium: 3x value.

## Late Game

Greenhouse: 116 Ancient Fruit + 4 trees. Weekly harvest: 191,400g.
Pigs: 8 pigs = ~20 truffles/day = 21,300g/day.

Combine: Greenhouse Ancient Fruit + cellar aging + pig farm.
