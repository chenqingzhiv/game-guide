## Year 1 Summer: The Mid-Game Kickoff

### Preserve Jars vs Kegs — Which First?

| Machine | Cost | Materials | Output | Processing Time | Profit Per Fruit |
|---------|------|-----------|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| Preserve Jar | 30g + 1 Coal + 1 Wood + 1 Stone | 8 Copper | 2x base price + 50g | 2-3 days | 50g (blueberry) |
| Keg | 30g + 1 Copper + 1 Iron + 1 Oak Resin | 30 Wood + 1 Copper + 1 Iron | 3x base price | 7 days (wine) | 150g (blueberry) |

**Rule of thumb**: Build Preserve Jars first (Year 1 Summer) for blueberries. Build Kegs second (Year 1 Fall → Year 2) for Starfruit and Ancient Fruit.

Complete the Vault bundles (or pay Joja 42,500g) to unlock the Desert bus by mid-Fall. This gives access to:

- **Starfruit seeds**: 400g each from Sandy. Plant in greenhouse or summer only.

## Year 1 Winter: The Infrastructure Push

Winter has no crops (unless you have the greenhouse). Focus on:

| Activity | Why | Expected Value |
|----------|-----|---------------|
| Mining | Iridium sprinklers, tool upgrades | Priceless |
| Skull Cavern | Iridium, Prismatic Shards | 10,000-50,000g/day |
| Greenhouse unlock | Community Center Pantry | Long-term investment |
| Tapping trees | Oak Resin (for kegs) | 20+ tappers on oak trees |
| Fiber + wood farming | Clear forest for materials | Foundation for Year 2 |

**Winter Keg Math**: 1 oak tree with tapper = 1 Oak Resin every 7 days. Each keg requires 1 Oak Resin. You need ~6 oak trees per 10 kegs you want. Plant a dedicated oak grove near the train station.

| Step | Capital Required | Notes |

## Ginger Island — The Endgame Farm

Unlock Ginger Island (requires Community Center completion, Willy's boat repair for 200 Hardwood + 5 Batteries + 1,000g).

### Ginger Island Farm Setup

1. Clear all debris (about 3 days with iridium tools)
2. Plant Starfruit in ALL 360+ tiles
3. Deluxe Speed-Gro + Deluxe retaining soil (water once)
4. Harvest weekly (13 day growth → Speed-Gro = 9 days)
5. Process in 200+ kegs (build sheds on island)

**Weekly output**: 360 Starfruit → 360 Starfruit Wine = **810,000g/week**.

## Pro Money Making Tips

1. **Day 1 fishing optimization**: Fish at the mountain lake from Day 2 onward. Catfish (spring rain) sell for 200g each. Save all fish >100g for early seed money.
2. **Seed maker exploit**: Plant cheap wheat in summer/fall. Process every harvest through seed makers. Wheat has 1-day growth with Speed-Gro. Each wheat seed costs 10g. Each Seed Maker output gives 1-3 wheat seeds (avg 2). This effectively doubles your seeds.
3. **Tea saplings**: At Farming Lv2, Caroline's 2-heart event gives the Tea Sapling recipe (2 Wood + 5 Fiber + 2 Wild Seeds — sell for 500g each). This is the best Year 1 Spring money maker after fishing. Craft 50+ saplings for 25,000g profit.
4. **Fish Pond replicas**: Super Cucumbers (ocean) produce Iridium Ore. Lava Eels (mine L100) produce Spicy Eel and Magma Geodes. Rainbow Trout have a 0.5% chance for Prismatic Shard.
5. **Wine vs Jelly**: Wine (3x base price) beats Jelly (2x+50g) for high-value crops. Jelly beats Wine for low-value crops (wheat, corn). Calculate before processing.
6. **Fairy Rose honey**: Plant Fairy Rose near bee houses in fall. Fairy Rose honey sells for 680g base (952g with Artisan). 20 bee houses = 19,040g every 4 days.
7. **Penny-pinching**: Save fiber, wood, and stone from Day 1. You need 200+ Wood + 100+ Stone + 50+ Fiber for essential buildings. Don't sell raw materials.
8. **True perfection**: The in-game tracker shows your progress toward 100% Perfection. Money is only one component — you also need all recipes, artifacts, and Stardrops. But having 10M+ gold makes buying everything easy.
