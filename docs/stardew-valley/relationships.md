## Marriage Candidates

Easiest: Abigail (amethyst), Emily (cloth), Leah (salad).

## Universal Loves

Prismatic Shard, Rabbit's Foot: +80 points.

Artisan goods, fruits, cooked meals, gems: liked by most.

Birthday gifts give 8x friendship.

## Perks

2-8 hearts: events + recipes.
10 hearts: bouquet, then mermaid pendant for marriage.

Best spouse: Leah (helps farm) or Abigail (adventures).
