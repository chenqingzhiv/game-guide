## Stardew Valley Mining Guide

The Mines contain 120 floors of monsters, ores, and treasure. This guide covers everything from your first copper ore to deep Skull Cavern dives for iridium.

---

## Mines 1-120 Floor Progression

### Section 1: Floors 1-20 (The Dirt Floors)

| Floor Range | Theme | Ores | Monsters | Special Drops |
|------------|-------|------|----------|--------------|
| 1-10 | Brown dirt | Copper | Bats, Grubs, Cave Flies | Quartz, Earth Crystal |
| 11-20 | Brown dirt | Copper (more nodes) | Bats, Grubs, Cave Flies | First elevator floor (5) |
| Elevator floors | 5, 10, 15, 20 | — | — | Saves your progress |

**Goal**: Mine every copper node. Collect stone for staircases. Reach Floor 20 for the elevator.

**Combat tip**: Craft a Wooden Blade (from the Adventurer's Guild) or use the Rusty Sword. Kill 10 Bats → Adventurer's Guild quest unlocks.

### Section 2: Floors 21-40 (The Ice Floors)

| Floor Range | Theme | Ores | Monsters | Special Drops |
|------------|-------|------|----------|--------------|
| 21-30 | Ice/Snow | Iron, Frozen Geodes | Dust Sprites, Frost Jellies, Bats | Frozen Tear, Iron Ore |
| 31-40 | Ice/Snow | Iron, Frozen Geodes | Dust Sprites, Frost Jellies | Elevator at 30, 40 |

**Goal**: Mine iron ore. Kill 50 Dust Sprites (Lv32-39 are best) → Burglar's Ring reward.

**Combat tip**: Dust Sprites drop coal (essential for smelting). Kill as many as possible. Use a Steel Sword (5 Iron + 20 Stone, after reaching Floor 30).

### Section 3: Floors 41-80 (The Frozen/Warm Transition)

| Floor Range | Theme | Ores | Monsters | Special Drops |
|------------|-------|------|----------|--------------|
| 41-50 | Frozen | Iron, Gold (rare) | Ghosts, Skeletons, Bats | Ghostfish |
| 51-60 | Frozen/Water | Gold (common) | Ghosts, Skeletons, Magma Sparkers | Elevator at 50, 60 |
| 61-70 | Warm/Water | Gold | Magma Sparkers, Bats | Fire Quartz |
| 71-80 | Lava | Gold, Omni Geodes | Magma Sparkers, Shadow Brutes | Elevator at 70, 80 |

**Goal**: Gather gold ore for Quality Sprinklers. Ghosts drop cloth (sewing machine material).

**Combat tip**: Enemies get harder here. Upgrade to a Gold Sword by Floor 60. Use the Savage Ring (+1 speed on kill) if available.

### Section 4: Floors 81-120 (The Lava Floors)

| Floor Range | Theme | Ores | Monsters | Special Drops |
|------------|-------|------|----------|--------------|
| 81-90 | Lava | Gold, Iridium (rare), Omni Geodes | Shadow Brutes, Shadow Shamans, Magma Sparkers | Elevator at 90 |
| 91-110 | Lava | Gold, Iridium, Omni Geodes | Shadow Brutes, Shadow Shamans, Serpents | Iridium is farmable here |
| 111-120 | Dark/Lava | Gold, Iridium, Omni Geodes | Serpents, Shadow Brutes | Floor 100 → Skull Key, Floor 120 → Stardrop |

**Key rewards**:
- **Floor 100**: Skull Key (unlocks Skull Cavern in the Desert)
- **Floor 120**: Stardrop (+34 max energy, permanent)

## Skull Cavern Strategy

Skull Cavern is the endgame mining area in the Calico Desert. It has no elevator — you must descend by finding ladders or crafting staircases.

### Preparation Checklist

| Item | Quantity | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| Mega Bombs | 50+ | Clear stone clusters instantly, find shafts faster |
| Salads / Cheese | 50+ | Best healing-per-slot ratio (salad = 113 HP, cheese = 101) |
| Triple Shot Espresso / Coffee | 10+ | +1 speed bonus, stacks with food |
| Staircases | 10-100 | Skip infested floors, spiral floors |
| Desert Warp Totem | 1 | Arrive at 6am instead of 10am (bus) |
| Farm Warp Totem | 1 | Stay until 1:50am |
| Desert Sword / Galaxy Sword | 1 | Minimum damage 60+ |
| Iridium Band (ring) | 1-2 | +10% ATK, magnetism, light |

### Step-by-Step Skull Cavern Dive

1. **Check TV fortune**: Only go on lucky or very lucky days (higher shaft spawn rate)
2. **Warp at 6am**: Use Desert Warp Totem for maximum time
3. **Bomb clusters**: Don't mine individual rocks — bomb 3+ rock groups
4. **Prioritize shafts**: Shafts (hole in the ground) skip 3-15 floors. Always choose shafts over ladders.
5. **Ignore everything but Iridium**: Don't stop for gold, gems, or coal. Only pick up Iridium, Prismatic Shards, and Auto-Petter (treasure room)
6. **Staircase on bad floors**: Infested floors and spiral floors waste time. Place a staircase and descend immediately
7. **Leave before 1:50am**: You pass out at 2am and lose 1,000g

### Iridium Comparison

| Location | Iridium Per Hour | Efficiency |
|----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Normal Mines (Floors 110-120) | 0-5 ore | Low |
| Skull Cavern (Floors 1-50) | 10-30 ore | Medium |
| Skull Cavern (Floors 50-100+) | 50-100 ore | Excellent |
| Skull Cavern (Floor 200+, staircases) | 100-200 ore | Best |

## Geodes and the Blacksmith

### Geode Types and Contents

| Geode | Cost to Crack | Best Drops | Source |
|-------|--------------|-----------|--------|
| Regular Geode | 25g | Copper, Stone, Clay, early artifacts | Mines Floors 1-39 |
| Frozen Geode | 25g | Iron, Frozen Tear, Aquamarine | Mines Floors 40-79 |
| Magma Geode | 25g | Gold, Fire Quartz, Topaz, Rubies | Mines Floors 80-120 |
| Omni Geode | 25g | Everything — Prismatic Shard (0.4%), Diamonds | Any mine floor, Skull Cavern |

**Geode cracking strategy**: Save geodes until you can crack 50+ at once. The artifact drop rate is seeded — use the "open 1, check artifact, re-open if you don't get it" trick. Omni Geodes have a 0.4% chance for Prismatic Shard.

## Pro Mining Tips

1. **Elevator skip trick**: If you need a specific floor (e.g., 40 for Dust Sprites), use the elevator to nearby floor and exit/re-enter to reset the floor layout.
2. **Staircase economy**: Craft staircases from stone (99 stone = 1 staircase). Mine Floors 40-50 give the most stone per trip.
3. **Day 1 mine rush**: Start mining on Day 5. Reach Floor 40 by end of week 1 (copper → iron → better tools). Reach Floor 80 by end of first month.
4. **Monster loot stacking**: Burglar's Ring + Napalm Ring + Monster Musk (from Krobus) = maximum loot per Skull Cavern run.
5. **Luck food**: Pumpkin Soup (+2 luck, +2 defense) or Spicy Eel (+1 luck, +1 speed). Luck affects loot quality and mine shaft spawns.
6. **Glowstone ring**: Craft at Mining Lv4 (5 Iron + 5 Solar Essence). Combine it with Iridium Band at the Forge for permanent light.
7. **Bomb crafting**: Mega Bombs require 4 Solar Essence + 1 Gold + 1 Void Essence + 1 Coal. Solar Essences drop from Ghosts (Floors 50-70).
8. **Mining XP table**: Ore gives 5 XP per node. Gems give 50 XP each. Monster kills give 3-10 XP. The fastest XP is gem nodes in Skull Cavern.
