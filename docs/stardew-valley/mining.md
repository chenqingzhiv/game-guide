## Mine Floors

1-20: Copper, bats, slimes.
21-40: Ice, iron, dust sprites.
41-80: Gold, ghosts, magma sparkers.
81-120: Gold, iridium, shadow brutes.
Goal: Floor 100 (Skull Key), Floor 120 (Stardrop).

## Skull Cavern

Go on lucky day. Bring 50+ mega bombs, 50 salads, coffee. Use Desert Warp at 6am. Bomb clusters. Skip ores, only pick iridium.

Iridium: 50-100 per hour vs 0-5 in normal mines.

## Adventurers Guild

10 Slimes: Slime Charmer Ring (immune).
50 Dust Sprites: Burglar Ring (double loot).
80 Void Spirits: Savage Ring (+1 speed per kill).
150 Bats: Vampire Ring (+2 HP per kill).
