## Days 1-5

Day 1: Plant 15 parsnips. Visit Pierre. Buy potatoes.
Days 2-5: Water crops, fish in ocean, forage. Save 50g for backpack upgrade.

## Spring Crops

Strawberry: Best (20.8g/day). Buy at Egg Festival Day 13.
Potato: 5g/day. Cauliflower: 7.9g/day. Kale: 6.67g/day.

Strawberry strat: Plant on Day 13, harvest twice.

## Community Center

Spring Foraging: Leek, Daffodil, Dandelion - get Spring Seeds.
Spring Crops: Parsnips x5 - Speed-Gro.
**Priority:** Quality Crops Bundle for Preserves Jar.

## Energy

Eat raw parsnips early. Field snacks: 1 acorn + 1 maple seed + 1 pine cone = 45 energy.
Salmonberry season (15-18 Spring): Forage 30+ berries.
