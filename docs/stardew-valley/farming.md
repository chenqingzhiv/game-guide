## Best Crops

Spring: Strawberry (20.8g/day). Summer: Blueberry (20.8), Starfruit (18.9). Fall: Cranberry (18.9), Pumpkin (16.9).

All year: Ancient Fruit. Use seed maker to multiply. Coffee: 1 bean harvest every 2 days.

## Keg Empire

Ancient Fruit Wine: 1650g. Starfruit Wine: 2250g. Hops Pale Ale: 420g.

Cask cellaring: Aged to iridium = 3x value. Ancient Fruit: 4950g per bottle.

## Animals

Pigs are best: 8 pigs = ~20 truffles/day = 21,300g/day from truffle oil.

Chickens: 285g (gold mayo). Cows: 390g (cheese). Goats: 560g.

## Greenhouse

Fill with 116 Ancient Fruit + 4 fruit trees. Output: 191,400g/week from wine.
