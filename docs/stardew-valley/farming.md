## Fertilizer Guide

| Fertilizer | Effect | Crafting Recipe | Best Used On |
|-----------|--------|----------------|-------------|
| Basic Fertilizer | +1 soil quality | 2 Sap | Cheap crops (potato, kale) |
| Quality Fertilizer | +2 soil quality | 1 Fish + 1 Sap | High-profit crops (strawberry, blueberry) |
| Speed-Gro | 10% faster growth | 1 Pine Tar + 1 Clam | Multi-harvest crops |
| Deluxe Speed-Gro | 25% faster growth | 1 Oak Resin + 1 Coral | Starfruit, Ancient Fruit |
| Hyper Speed-Gro | 33% faster growth | 1 Radioactive Ore + 1 Bone Fragment | Endgame crops |
| Tree Fertilizer | Trees grow 1 stage per day | 1 Fiber + 1 Stone | Fruit trees |

**Important**: Fertilizer is consumed at the start of the season and when the crop is harvested. Apply BEFORE planting seeds.

## The Ancient Fruit Wine Empire

This is the single most profitable activity in Stardew Valley.

### Setup Steps

| Step | What To Do | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Find Ancient Seed (artifact spots, fishing treasure, bugs) | Donate to Museum → get Ancient Fruit seeds + Seed Maker recipe |
| 2 | Plant first Ancient Fruit in greenhouse | One plant produces forever (Spring-Summer-Fall in greenhouse) |
| 3 | Use Seed Makers on every harvest | Each ancient fruit → 1-3 seeds (avg 2) |
| 4 | Fill greenhouse with 116 Ancient Fruit | 1 harvest per week = 116 fruit |
| 5 | Build 116+ Kegs (outside) | Place in big sheds for space efficiency |
| 6 | Process all fruit into wine | Base wine = 1,650g. Aged = 4,950g |
| 7 | Build cellar casks | Age wine to iridium quality (2 seasons) |

## Pro Farming Tips

1. **Seed Maker abuse**: Put every low-quality crop through the Seed Maker. It averages 2 seeds per crop, and you can get Ancient Seeds as a rare bonus (0.5% chance).
2. **Junimo Huts**: After completing the Community Center, buy Junimo Huts from the Wizard. They auto-harvest your crops in a 17x17 radius. Place 1 hut per field quadrant.
3. **Giant crops**: Cauliflower, Melon, and Pumpkin can grow giant if planted in a 3x3 contiguous block. Use the hoe to break them for 15-21 crops instead of 9.
4. **Fertilizer persistence**: Deluxe Speed-Gro persists on multi-harvest crops (blueberries, cranberries) for the entire season. Apply once, benefit all season.
5. **Trellis plan**: Green Beans, Hops, and Grapes block player movement. Plant them on the edge of your field or leave 1-row gaps between trellis rows.
6. **Winter prep**: Fill the greenhouse by winter of Year 1. Without it, money stops in winter — focus on mining and upgrading tools instead.
7. **Irrigation upgrade timing**: Basic sprinklers are nearly useless. Rush to Quality Sprinklers (Farming Lv6) by summer Year 1. Iridium Sprinklers (Farming Lv9) should be your goal by Year 2 spring.
