## What is Stardew Valley?

Farming and life simulation RPG where you inherit your grandfather's farm and build a new life in Pelican Town. Farm crops, raise animals, mine for ores, fish the rivers and ocean, befriend NPCs, and restore the Community Center. **100K+ concurrent players on Steam (#12).**

### Game Pillars

| Pillar | Description | Progression |
|:-------|:------------|:------------|
| 🌱 **Farming** | Plant crops, raise animals, artisan goods | 🥕 → 🌾 → 🍷 |
| ⛏️ **Mining** | Explore mines, collect ores, gems, combat | 🪨 → 🗡️ → 💎 |
| 🎣 **Fishing** | Catch 80+ fish species, collect crab pots | 🐟 → 🐠 → 🐡 |
| 💖 **Relationships** | Befriend NPCs, date, marriage | 🗣️ → ❤️ → 💍 |
| 🏆 **Perfection** | Complete everything for true ending | 100% tracker |
