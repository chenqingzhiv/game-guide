## Fishing Basics

Cast: hold left click for distance. Reel: keep green bar on fish.

Rod upgrades: Bamboo (500g), Fiberglass (1800g + bait), Iridium (7500g + bait + tackle).

## Best Spots

Mountain Lake: Largemouth Bass, Sturgeon.
Forest Pond: Catfish, Woodskip.
Ocean: Tuna, Sardine.
River: Sunfish, Pike.

## Most Valuable

Legend (Spring, rain): 5000g. Glacierfish (Winter): 2500g. Lava Eel (Mines L100): 1500g.

Scorpion Carp (Desert): 675g. Sturgeon (Mountain): 125g.
