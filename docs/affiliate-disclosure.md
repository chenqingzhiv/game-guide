# Affiliate Disclosure

Game Guide Club is committed to transparency with our readers. Here's how affiliate links work on our site.

## What Are Affiliate Links?

Some of the links on Game Guide Club are "affiliate links." This means that if you click on one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a **small commission at no extra cost to you**.

These commissions help us maintain and grow the site — covering hosting costs, research time, and tool development so we can keep creating free, high-quality game guides.

## Where You'll Find Affiliate Links

### 🛒 "Buy on Steam" Buttons
Many of our game pages feature a green "Buy on Steam 🛒" button. These are **Steam affiliate links** — when you purchase a game through these buttons, we earn a commission (typically ~5% of the sale price).

### 📦 Amazon Product Links
If and when we recommend gaming gear, books, or accessories, we may use Amazon affiliate links as part of the **Amazon Services LLC Associates Program**.

### 🎮 Game Keys & Bundles
From time to time, we may link to authorized key sellers or bundle deals. These may also be affiliate links.

## Our Promise to You

- **Price is the same**: Affiliate links never increase the price you pay.
- **We only recommend what we genuinely use or believe in**: We don't promote games or products we wouldn't recommend to a friend.
- **We mark our links**: Affiliate links are clearly marked with a 🛒 icon or "Buy on Steam" button — you'll always know when a link is an affiliate link.

## Legal Stuff

Game Guide Club participates in:

- **Steam Partner Program** (store.steampowered.com)
- **Amazon Services LLC Associates Program** (amazon.com)

This disclosure is provided in compliance with the **Federal Trade Commission (FTC)** guidelines on endorsements and testimonials.
