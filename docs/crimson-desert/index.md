## What is Crimson Desert?

Open-world action-adventure RPG from Pearl Abyss (the creators of Black Desert Online). You play as Kliff, a mercenary navigating the continent of Pywel — a brutal world of rival factions, ancient ruins, and hidden powers. Combat is skill-based, exploration is rewarded, and the game expects you to figure out most of it on your own.

### Why Crimson Desert Matters

- **Deep combat system**: Hold-to-chain attacks, Force Palm mid-air combos, learn enemy skills by watching
- **Massive open world**: Pywel is vast — unlock fast travel via bell towers, Abyss Nexus portals, and Cresset waypoints
- **Hidden mechanics everywhere**: Triple jump with Force Palm, rob banks, recruit pets at Trust 100, clear bounties with Writ of Absolution
- **Abyss Artifacts skill tree**: Spend skill points on stats and abilities, learn rare moves from enemies
- **Rich worldbuilding**: The game never explains half of what you can do — you discover it

## Getting Started

Start in Pywel and follow the main story until you reach Turnali. From there, the world opens up. Focus on upgrading Health and Stamina first, unlock bell towers for fast travel, and experiment with Force Palm — it's the Swiss army knife of the game.
