## Train Basics

Trains transport items across long distances with massive throughput. Best for distances over 500m.

## Track Building

Build on foundations for alignment. Min turn radius: 8 foundations. Grade limit: 20 degrees.

Dual-track for main lines. Single-track for branches with passing sidings.

## Stations

Freight Platform (items), Fluid Platform (fluids), Empty Platform (overflow).

3-4 platforms per station optimal. Orient for straight entry/exit.

## Signaling

Block signals divide track into segments. Path signals at intersections. Chain signals BEFORE intersections.

Rule: Path at entrance, Chain 2-3 car lengths before, Block at exit.
