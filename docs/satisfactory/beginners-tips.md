## First Hour — Surviving the Drop

Your drop pod lands in a random location. Here's the exact sequence to get running fast:

1. **Collect 200 leaves + 50 wood** by hand-punching vegetation
2. **Find a pure iron node** near your pod — scan with F
3. **Build a Portable Miner** on the iron node (50 leaves → biomass, 3 iron plates)
4. **Craft a Smelter** — smelt 120 iron ore into plates
5. **Build a Constructor** — make 60 iron rods + 120 screws
6. **Craft a Biomass Burner** (needs 30 biomass from leaves)
7. **Build the M.A.M.** (Molecular Analysis Machine) to unlock the hub upgrades

**Pro tip:** Scout your starting area thoroughly. Look for at least 2 iron nodes, 1 copper node, and 1 limestone node within 150m before placing foundations.

### Resource Node Tiers

| Node Type | Purity | MK1 Miner Output | MK2 Miner Output | MK3 Miner Output |
|-----------|--------|------------------|------------------|------------------|
| Impure    | 1x     | 30/min           | 60/min           | 120/min          |
| Normal    | 2x     | 60/min           | 120/min          | 240/min          |
| Pure      | 4x     | 120/min          | 240/min          | 480/min          |

Always build your main factory near at least one pure node of each starter resource.

## Power Management — Don't Brownout

Power in Satisfactory is unforgiving. A brownout causes production to slow, which starves the power plant, creating a death spiral.

### Biomass Phase (Tiers 0-2)

| Fuel Source | MJ per Item | Burn Time | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Leaves      | 5           | 1.0s      | Collector output, terrible |
| Wood        | 15          | 3.0s      | Chop trees, much better |
| Biomass      | 60          | 12.0s     | Crafted from leaves/wood |
| Solid Biofuel| 180         | 36.0s     | Crafted from biomass — use this! |

**Tip:** Craft leaves → biomass → solid biofuel. A single stack of solid biofuel (50) burns for 30 minutes. Chain 4+ biomass burners before you research coal.

### Coal Power (Tier 3)

The jump to coal changes everything. Build near a water source + coal node:

- **Ratio:** 8 Coal Generators : 3 Water Extractors : 120 coal/min (1 normal node)
- **Water pipelay:** Each generator needs 45 m³/min. 3 extractors at 120 m³/min = 360 m³/min for 8 gens.
- **Placement:** Build extractors at or below water level. Use pumps on uphill sections (head lift: 20m base + 20m per pump).

### Fuel Generators (Tier 5+)

Fuel generators produce 150 MW each (vs 75 MW for coal). Diluted Packaged Fuel + Fuel Generator builds can produce 30,000+ MW from a single pure oil node.

## Factory Layout — The Manifold Method

Satisfactory rewards scalable layouts over complex load balancing.

### Standard Production Line

```
[Input belt] → [Smelter/Refinery] → [Constructor] → [Assembler] → [Manufacturer] → [Storage Container]
```

**Manifold System:** Run one main belt past all machines. Use splitters to feed each machine in sequence. The first machines fill up first, then overflow to the rest. Benefits:

- Zero math per machine
- Automatically balances over time
- Easy to expand by extending the belt
- Works for any production ratio

**Recommended floor plan:**

- Each production floor: 8-12 foundation width, unlimited length
- Vertical gap: 3 walls (36m) between floors for belts
- Logistics floor below: run all belts, pipes, and power
- Use conveyor wall holes to route belts cleanly between floors

### The Mega-Base Transition

When you unlock Mk.3 belts (270/min) and Mk.2 miners, rebuild your starter base. Don't tear down the old one — just build new, larger production lines elsewhere. Turn the old base into a parts mall for building materials.

## Common Beginner Mistakes

1. **Building too close to nodes** — leave 3-4 foundation gap for future belt work
2. **Overscaling before coal** — keep biomass factories tiny; upgrade at coal
3. **Hand-feeding machines** — automate everything, even if it runs slow
4. **Complex load balancers** — manifolds are simpler and equally efficient
5. **Ignoring the AWESOME Sink** — pump overflow into the sink for tickets (unlock: ladders, walls, foundations)
6. **No foundations** — everything snaps at right angles on foundations. Build on foundations from day one.
7. **Forgetting vertical space** — stacking floors multiplies your build area without expanding the footprint

## Exploration Tips

- Craft the Xeno-Basher (melee weapon) and Xeno-Zapper early
- Collect **Power Slugs** (blue/yellow/purple) for overclocking
- Use Beacons to mark resources you find
- Open **Drop Pods** for hard drives (see Alternate Recipes guide)
- Scan for quartz + sulfur early — required for MAM research
- Build **Lookout Towers** to scout terrain before placing foundations
