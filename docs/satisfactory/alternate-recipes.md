## Finding Hard Drives

Hard drives are required to unlock alternate recipes. They're found in **Crash Sites** (downed drop pods) scattered across the map.

### How to Scan

1. Build the **M.A.M.** (Molecular Analysis Machine) at your hub
2. Research **Hard Drive Scan** (tier 0 — costs 10 Alien Protein from animal remains)
3. Hard drives are permanently marked on the compass once scanned
4. Each drive takes **10 minutes** to analyze in the M.A.M.

### Crash Site Requirements

| Crash Site Type | Required Item | Quantity |
|----------------|---------------|----------|
| Low-tier (Grasslands) | Iron Rods | 50 |
| Mid-tier (Rocky Desert) | Rotors | 10 |
| Mid-tier (Forest) | Cable | 50 |
| High-tier (Northern Forest) | Computers | 5 |
| High-tier (Swamp/Spire Coast) | Heavy Modular Frames | 3 |
| Late-game (Dune Desert) | Supercomputers | 2 |

Bring extra materials to every crash site. Scan the wrecks with the **Object Scanner** (F) to identify the repair requirement.

### Best Exploration Route

Start from the Grass Fields and expand outward:

1. Grass Fields → 10+ drives (easy, no enemies)
2. Rocky Desert → 8+ drives (be prepared for hog attacks)
3. Northern Forest → 12+ drives (best concentration, but spitters)
4. Dune Desert → 15+ drives (massive area, difficult terrain)
5. Red Jungle / Spire Coast → late-game, dangerous fauna

## S-Tier Recipes — Always Grab These

These recipes are so powerful they reshape your factory design.

| Recipe | Inputs | Outputs | Benefit |
|--------|--------|---------|---------|
| **Solid Steel Ingot** | 3 Iron Ingot + 3 Coal → 3 Steel Ingot | 3/min | 2x steel output from same input. Replaces standard steel. |
| **Caterium Wire** | 1 Caterium Ingot → 8 Wire | 8/min | 4x wire from same input. Massive copper savings. |
| **Cast Screw** | 2 Iron Ingot → 50 Screws | 50/min | Eliminates the entire screw production chain. |
| **Heavy Oil Residue** | 3 Crude Oil → 4 HOR + 2 Polymer Resin | 4 HOR/min | 6x fuel potential when combined with Diluted Fuel. |
| **Diluted Packaged Fuel** | 1 HOR + 2 Water + 1 Empty Can → 2 Fuel + Empty Can | 2/min | Turns HOR into massive fuel output. |
| **Pure Iron Ingot** | 7 Iron Ore + 2 Water → 13 Iron Ingot | 13/min | 2.5x ingot output from the same ore node. |
| **Recycled Plastic/Rubber** | 1 Polymer Resin + 2 Fuel → 2 Plastic/Rubber | 2/min | Oil mega-plant enabler. |

### Why These Are S-Tier

**Solid Steel Ingot** alone halves your coal and iron requirements for steel production. Combined with **Cast Screw**, you can build a zero-screw, high-steel factory from less raw material than a basic iron/copper setup.

**Heavy Oil Residue + Diluted Packaged Fuel + Recycled Plastic/Rubber** is the oil mega-plant combo. A single pure oil node (600/min with Mk.2 pump) can produce enough fuel for 200+ fuel generators (~30,000 MW) and 1200 plastic/min.

## Best Production Combos

### Zero-Copper Factory
- **Cast Screw** + **Solid Steel Ingot** + **Steel Screw**
- No copper required for rotor/stator production
- Pure iron → steel → heavy parts

### The Oil Mega-Plant (Pure Oil Node)
```
Crude Oil (600/min)
  → Heavy Oil Residue (800 HOR/min)
    → Diluted Packaged Fuel (800 Fuel/min)
      → Recycled Plastic + Recycled Rubber
      → Turbofuel (600 Turbofuel/min)
= 200+ Fuel Generators + 1200 Plastic/min + 1200 Rubber/min
```

### High-Volume Circuit Board Factory
- **Pure Copper Ingot** + **Steamed Copper Sheet**
- **Silica (Alt)** for circuit boards
- Double output from every node
