## Ender Dragon

Prep: Full diamond Protection IV, Sharpness V sword, 12+ end crystals, 20+ blocks, water bucket, golden apples, slow falling potions.

Strategy: Destroy 10 crystals, hit dragon between breaths, collect Dragon Egg + Elytra.

## Wither

Summon: 4 Soul Sand in T-shape + 3 Wither Skulls.

Fight in a 3-block high tunnel (can't fly). Use Smite V sword (4x damage to undead).

Loot: Nether Star > Craft Beacon.

## Elytra

Find in End Ship (floating next to End City).

Upgrades: Unbreaking III + Mending = infinite flight.
Firework Rockets for propulsion.

Tip: Use Slow Falling potion for first flights.
