# 🌱 Minecraft Farming Guide

> Data version: Minecraft 1.21 | Last updated: 2026-07

Farming is the backbone of any successful Minecraft survival world. This guide covers manual crop farming, animal breeding strategies, villager trading halls, and fully automatic farm designs for infinite resources.

---

## 🌾 Crop Farming Mechanics

### Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | Detail |
|:---------|:--------|
| **Hydration** | Crops must have water within 4 blocks (diagonal counts) |
| **Light level** | Minimum of 9 (torches stop zombie spawning too) |
| **Growth rate** | Random tick every ~0.05s on average |
| **Bone meal** | Fully grown in 1–3 bone meal uses |
| **Farmland** | Hoe grass/dirt. Trample risk without light + player jumping |

### Best Crops for Food

| Crop | Growth Time | Yield | Saturation | Notes |
|:-----|:------------|:------|:-----------|:------|
| Carrots 🥕 | ~0.5 day | 1–5 | 4.8 | Good raw, even better as golden carrots |
| Potatoes 🥔 | ~0.5 day | 1–5 | 5.0 | Baked potatoes = 6 hunger bars |
| Wheat 🌾 | ~1 day | 1–3 | N/A | Bread (5 hunger) or cake |
| Beetroot 🌿 | ~1 day | 1–3 | 1.2 | Lowest efficiency — skip |
| Sweet berries 🫐 | 7 days regrow | 2–4 | 2.0 | Passive hedge food, damages mobs |

> 🏆 **Best food crop:** Carrots → craft with gold nuggets → **Golden Carrots** (6 hunger, 14.4 saturation — best in the game)

### Best Crops for Trading

| Crop | Trade | Emeralds/Stack |
|:-----|:------|:---------------|
| Wheat → Farmer | 20 wheat → 1 emerald | 3.2 emeralds per stack |
| Carrots → Farmer | 22 carrots → 1 emerald | 2.9 emeralds per stack |
| Potatoes → Farmer | 26 potatoes → 1 emerald | 2.5 emeralds per stack |
| Beetroot → Farmer | 14 beetroot → 1 emerald | 4.6 emeralds per stack |

### Essential Villager Professions

| Profession | Workstation | Best Trade |
|:-----------|:------------|:-----------|
| **Librarian** 📚 | Lectern | Mending (10–38 emeralds), Enchanted books |
| **Armorer** 🛡️ | Blast furnace | Diamond armor (later tiers) |
| **Toolsmith** ⛏️ | Smithing table | Diamond pickaxe, axe, shovel |
| **Weaponsmith** ⚔️ | Grindstone | Diamond sword + enchants |
| **Farmer** 🌾 | Composter | Golden carrots, emeralds from crops |
| **Cleric** 💜 | Brewing stand | Ender pearls, bottles o' enchanting |
| **Fisherman** 🎣 | Barrel | Enchanted books, emeralds from fish |
| **Butcher** 🥩 | Smoker | Emeralds from raw meat |

### Villager Trading Hall Setup

1. **Breeding:** 2 villagers + 3 beds + 1 workstation. Drop food (bread, carrots) for them.
2. **Job locking:** Place workstation → villager gets profession. Trade once to lock.
3. **Zombie curing:** Zombie → weakness potion + golden apple = cured villager (permanent 40–60% discount).
4. **Curing cycle:** Multiple cures stack discounts (up to 1 emerald trades).

## ⚙️ Automatic Farm Designs

### Auto Wheat Farm (Piston + Observer)

| Component | Quantity | How it works |
|:----------|:---------|:-------------|
| Farmland | 15×15 (225 blocks) | Hoe tilled dirt |
| Water source | 1 block center | Hydrates farmland |
| Observer | 1 per row | Detects crop growth |
| Piston | 1 per row | Push water to harvest |
| Water stream | 1 per row | Flows over crops → breaks them |
| Collection | Hopper + chest | Under the water stream end |

> **Yield:** ~3 stacks of wheat per harvest. Harvest every ~30 minutes.

### Auto Chicken Cooker

1. Put chickens on a hopper
2. Eggs drop onto the hopper → dispenser throws eggs
3. New chicks grow
4. When adult, lava blade kills them
5. Cooked chicken collected below

| Output | Rate |
|:-------|:-----|
| Cooked chicken | ~2 stacks/hour |
| Feathers | ~1 stack/hour |
| XP | Negligible |

### Sugar Cane Farm (Observer + Piston)

| Component | Purpose |
|:----------|:--------|
| Water line | Sugar cane grows on sand next to water |
| Observer | Detects when cane reaches 3 blocks tall |
| Piston | Breaks the 2nd block (leaves base to regrow) |
| Hopper line | Collects falling sugar cane items |

**Yield:** ~4 stacks/hour of sugar cane. Double by building two layers high.

## 🌿 Advanced Techniques

### Stem Crops (Melons & Pumpkins)

- Plant seeds on farmland
- Grows stem → then fruit on adjacent **dirt/grass block**
- Auto-harvest: Observer detects stem → piston breaks fruit
- **Max profit:** Pumpkin → 6 emeralds per pumpkin to farmer (Jack o'Lantern trade)

### Bamboo Farm

| Feature | Detail |
|:--------|:--------|
| Growth rate | Fastest plant in game (~3 seconds per block) |
| Auto-farm | Observer + piston = infinite bamboo |
| Use | Fuel (bamboo smelts 0.25 items — use as filler) |
| Use #2 | Scaffolding for building |
| Use #3 | Sticks (2 bamboo = 2 sticks) |

### Cactus Farm

| Feature | Detail |
|:--------|:--------|
| Placement | On sand, no water needed |
| Auto-farm | Plant cactus next to a block stop — 3-high cactus breaks above block |
| Use | Furnace fuel (poor), green dye, XP fuel |
| Warning | Cactus deals damage — don't stand next to it! |
