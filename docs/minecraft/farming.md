## Crops

Wheat: 2-3 days. Makes bread.
Carrots: 2-3 days. Baked carrots.
Potatoes: 2-3 days. Best food: baked potatoes.
Beetroot: 2-3 days. Red dye.

Water range: 4 blocks. Max 9x9 farmland.

## Animals

Cows: Wheat to breed. Leather + beef.
Sheep: Wheat. Wool + mutton.
Chickens: Seeds. Feathers + chicken.

Optimal: 4+ cows, 4+ sheep, 2+ chickens.

## Villager Trading

Librarian: Mending book (best enchantment).
Farmer: 22 carrots = 1 emerald.
Fletcher: 32 sticks = 1 emerald (infinite).
Armorer: 15 coal = 1 emerald.

Cure zombie villagers for discount prices!
