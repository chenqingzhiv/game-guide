# 🏕️ Minecraft Beginner's Guide — Survive Your First Days

> Data version: Minecraft 1.21 | Last updated: 2026-07

So you've spawned into a blocky world with nothing but your fists. Don't panic — every Minecraft player has been there. This guide walks you through your first day, first night, and the crucial early-game milestones.

---

## ⏱️ First 10 Minutes: Speedrun Your First Day

A Minecraft day lasts **10 minutes of daylight**. You have until sundown (the sky turns orange, then dark) to secure shelter and basic gear.

| Time Elapsed | Sky Color | Priority Task |
|:-------------|:----------|:--------------|
| 0:00 | Blue / morning | Punch tree, collect logs |
| 2:00 | Bright | Craft wooden tools, find stone |
| 5:00 | Still bright | Mine stone for furnace, find coal |
| 8:00 | Orange sunset | **Build a shelter** (critical) |
| 9:00 | Red dusk | Light it up, close the door |
| 10:00 | Dark | Survive the night |

### Step 1: Punch a Tree (0:00–0:30)

- Look for any tree with a thick trunk (oak, birch, spruce).
- Hold left-click on the log until it breaks. Collect **at least 8 logs**.
- Oak has a chance to drop **apples** — these are free early food!

### Step 2: Craft Your Starter Tools (0:30–2:00)

Open your inventory (**E** key) and turn logs into planks, then a crafting table:

1. 1 log → 4 oak planks
2. 4 planks → **Crafting Table** (2×2 grid, fill all slots)
3. 2 planks → **Sticks** (vertical stack, 2 planks → 4 sticks)
4. Place the crafting table on the ground and right-click it

From the 3×3 grid, craft in this order:

| Tool | Materials | Why You Need It |
|:-----|:----------|:----------------|
| Wooden Pickaxe | 3 planks + 2 sticks | Mine stone |
| Wooden Sword | 2 planks + 1 stick | Defend yourself |
| Wooden Shovel | 1 plank + 2 sticks | Dig dirt / gravel faster |
| Wooden Axe | 3 planks + 2 sticks | Cut more logs efficiently |

### Step 3: Find Stone & Coal (2:00–5:00)

- Look for exposed stone on cliff sides, hills, or any cave entrance.
- Mine **8–12 cobblestone** with your wooden pickaxe.
- **Coal ore** has black speckles on grey stone. Mine at least 4 pieces.
- No coal? Mine **wood logs** and smelt them into **charcoal** (same function).

### Step 4: Build a Furnace (5:00–7:00)

With 8 cobblestone, craft a **Furnace**. Use it to:

- Smelt iron ore → iron ingots
- Cook raw food
- Smelt charcoal/coal for torches

**Torch recipe:** 1 stick + 1 coal/charcoal = **4 torches**. Make at least 8.

## 🏠 Shelter: Surviving the First Night

Your shelter doesn't need to be pretty — it needs to be **dark-proof**.

### Quick Shelter Options (Ranked)

| Shelter Type | Time to Build | Safety | Notes |
|:-------------|:-------------|:-------|:------|
| **Dig into a hill** | 10 seconds | ★★★★ | Fastest — dig 3 blocks in, seal with dirt |
| **Dirt/wood box** | 30 seconds | ★★★ | 5×5×3 box, door, torches |
| **Tree platform** | 1 minute | ★★ | Mobs can't reach you but can glitch |
| **Claim a cave** | 2 minutes | ★★★ | Block off entrance with cobblestone |

### Shelter Checklist

- [ ] 4 walls + roof (any solid block)
- [ ] A door (3 planks = 2 doors)
- [ ] **Torches inside** (light level ≥ 8 prevents mob spawns)
- [ ] A furnace and crafting table inside
- [ ] A bed (if you found sheep) — 3 wool + 3 planks

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Place your door facing **inward** to keep spiders out. Better yet, use a **trapdoor** entrance — mobs can't open trapdoors!

## 🛠️ Day 2+: Tools of the Trade

### Tool Progression Tree

```
Wooden → Stone → Iron → Diamond → Netherite
```

| Tool Tier | Durability | Mine Speed | How to Get |
|:----------|:-----------|:-----------|:-----------|
| Wood | 60 | 2× | Craft from planks |
| Stone | 132 | 4× | Craft from cobblestone |
| Iron | 251 | 6× | Smelt iron ore → ingots |
| Diamond | 1562 | 8× | Mine diamond ore with iron pickaxe |
| Netherite | 2031 | 9× + lava-proof | Upgrade diamond in smithing table |

### Day 2 Priority Checklist

1. **Upgrade to stone tools** — stone pickaxe, axe, sword
2. **Find iron** — mine at Y-level 15, look for beige speckled ore
3. **Build a farm** — break grass for seeds, hoe dirt near water
4. **Find sheep** — kill 3 for wool → craft a bed
5. **Explore a cave** — carefully, with torches and food

## 🆘 Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

| Mistake | Consequence | Fix |
|:--------|:------------|:----|
| Wandering at night without shelter | Death, lost items | Build a hole-in-the-wall ASAP |
| Mining straight down | Fall into lava | Staircase or 2×1 shaft |
| Not carrying a water bucket | Burned by lava | Always have a bucket on hotbar |
| Fighting with low hunger | No health regen | Eat before combat |
| Digging "suspicious gravel/sand" | Cave-in suffocation | Dig around, not under |
| Punching a creeper | Explosion | Back away — use bow or strafe |
