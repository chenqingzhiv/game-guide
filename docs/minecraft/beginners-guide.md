## Day 1

1. Punch 5 logs from a tree
2. Craft Planks > Crafting Table > Wooden Pickaxe > Wooden Sword
3. Mine 8 Cobblestone
4. Craft Furnace + Stone tools + Sword
5. Kill 3 sheep for wool (bed) OR build dirt hut
6. Sleep or seal yourself in before nightfall

## Days 2-3

1. Find cave system
2. Mine down to Y=-58 (best for diamonds in 1.21+)
3. Gather 20+ Iron Ore (smelt into ingots)
4. Craft Iron Pickaxe + Sword + Shield
5. Build a proper base: 10x10 enclosed, lit up
6. Start a wheat farm (hoe + water + seeds)

## Nether Prep

Mine 4+ Diamonds at Y=-58. Craft Diamond Pickaxe + Enchanting Table.
Find 10 Obsidian. Build Nether Portal: 4x5 frame.

Explore Nether: Find Fortress for Blaze Rods (potions).
