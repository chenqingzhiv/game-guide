## Enchanting Table

15 bookshelves around table (1 block gap, 2 high) = max level 30.

Levels: 1-8 (0 books), 9-16 (15), 17-24 (30), 25-30 (30 max).

## Best Enchantments

Sword: Sharpness V + Looting III + Fire Aspect II + Unbreaking III + Mending.
Pickaxe: Efficiency V + Fortune III/Silk Touch + Unbreaking + Mending.
Armor: Protection IV + Unbreaking + Mending.
Bow: Power V + Flame + Infinity/Mending.

## Getting Mending

1. Librarian villager (cure zombie for 1 emerald Mending book).
2. AFK fish farm (~2% chance).
3. Dungeon chests.
4. End Cities.

Fastest: Zombie villager curing.
