## Melee Combat

Shield in offhand: Blocks 100% damage from front. Right-click to block.

Critical hits: Jump before swinging. +50% damage.

Sweep attack: Hold attack to sweep (Java). Hits multiple enemies.

## Ranged Combat

Bow: Hold to charge. Full charge = 150% damage.
Crossbow: Pre-load, fire instantly. Multishot fires 3 arrows.

Tip: Use piercing/spectral arrows for special effects.

## Boss Strategies

Ender Dragon: Destroy crystals first. Use beds as explosives. Slow falling potion.
Wither: Fight underground (3-high tunnel). Smite V sword (4x to undead).
