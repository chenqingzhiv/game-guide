## New Buildings

Gauss Turret (iron+copper), Laser Turret (titanium+circuit), Missile Launcher (steel+processor).

Shield Generator: Base protection. Planetary Shield: Full coverage.

Combat Logistics Station: Ammo supply via drones.

## Research Tree

Basic Combat (Gauss) > Energy Weapons (Laser) > Explosives (Missile) > Shield Tech > Fleet Command > Planetary Fortress.

Priority: Rush Laser Turrets + Shields.

## Hive Brain

Endgame boss: Clear all bases in system, build 200+ ships, attack Hive Relay.

Reward: Hive Core + Cosmology research boost.
