## Threat Levels

### Game Start Settings

| Setting | Behavior | Best For |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Passive | No attacks | Pure building |
| Low | Scout raids | Learning combat |
| Medium | Regular assault waves | Standard play |
| High | Constant attacks | Experienced |
| Very High | Max aggression | Challenge runs |

### Threat Mechanic

Threat increases with activity. At 100%, attack wave spawns.

| Activity | Threat Increase |
|----------|----------------|
| Power generation | +1/min per 10 MW |
| Resource extraction | +2/min per miner |
| Planetary scanning | +5 per scan |
| Base destruction | +50 instant + 10/min |
| Space flight near hive | +2/min per ship |

### Threat by Phase

| Phase | Threat | Enemies | Defense |
|-------|--------|---------|---------|
| Early (red/blue) | 0-20% | Scouts | 4-6 Gauss |
| Mid (yellow/purple) | 20-60% | Raiders | 12+ turrets |
| Late (green) | 60-90% | Fleets | Shield + grid |
| Endgame | 90-100% | Hive attack | Fortress |

### Placement

360° ring 40-50 tiles from factory. Overlap fields of fire.

**Kill box:** 10 Gauss entry + 4 Missiles behind + 2 Lasers flanks + Shield Generator.

### Ammo Supply

| Turret | Ammo | Per 10 Turrets |
|--------|------|----------------|
| Gauss | Iron slugs | 2 ingots/min |
| Laser | 1.5 MW | 15 MW |
| Missile | Explosive missile | 3/min per 4 |
| Plasma | Plasma capsule | 1/min per 2 |
| Implosion | Implosion shell | 2/min per turret |

Buffer ammo: 1,000 slugs per Gauss = 10 min at max fire rate.

## Shields

| Type | Coverage | HP | Power | Note |
|------|----------|-----|-------|------|
| Generator | 20m radius | 10,000 | 50 MW | Local |

**Battery backup:** 20+ accumulators connected to the shield keeps it up during power dips.

## Space Combat

### Ships

| Ship | HP | Dmg | Speed | Role |
|------|-----|-----|-------|------|
| Corvette | 500 | 20 | Fast | Swarm |
| Destroyer | 2,000 | 50 | Medium | Damage |
| Cruiser | 8,000 | 120 | Slow | Tank |
| Dreadnought | 25,000 | 400 | Very Slow | Core killer |

**Fleet:** 30 corvettes + 10 destroyers + 5 cruisers. Ctrl+1..3 for groups.

### Combat Logistics Stations

Place 1-2 per planet. Auto-supply ammo, repair, store materials. Set to "Retreat" mode to avoid destruction.

## Hive Assault

### Preparation

1. Clear all ground bases (-20% hive HP)
2. 200+ ships (100 corvs + 50 dest + 50 cruisers)
3. Forward repair station
4. Destroy relay stations first

### Hive Components

| Unit | Danger | Strategy |
|------|--------|----------|
| Fighters (20-40) | Medium | Lasers + corvette screen |
| Bombers (5-10) | High | Missiles + destroyers |
| Hive Core shield | Very High | Focus fire all ships |
| Relay stations | Low-Med | Destroy first |

### Pro Tips

1. **Rush Laser Turrets** — no ammo logistics
2. **Shield before missiles** — protects while turrets work
3. **Leave bases alive** — clear halfway for farming (see Dark Fog guide)
4. **Upgrade mecha combat** — 50% speed + 100% damage from tech
5. **Battle Analysis building** predicts next 3 attacks
