## Threat Levels

Passive: No attacks.
Low: Scout raids. 4-6 turrets.
Medium: Regular assault. 12+ turrets + missiles.
High: Fleet attacks. Planetary shield.

Threat increases with power gen and resource extraction.

## Turrets

Gauss (20 dmg, medium range) > Laser (30, long, no ammo) > Missile (80, very long) > Plasma (150, long) > Implosion (300, short AOE).

Shield Generator: Absorbs all damage while powered. Battery backup critical!

## Dark Fog Bases

Scout Outpost (easy, low-tier loot).
Mining Base (medium, processed ores).
Military Base (hard, combat tech).
Relay Station (very hard, rare components).

Leave some bases alive for resource farming!
