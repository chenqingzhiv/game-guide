## 📖 Equipment Guide

### How Tiers Work

Equipment is organized by material tier. Each tier unlocks access to the next:

| Tier | Material | Gear Requires | Unlocks |
|:----|:---------|:--------------|:--------|
| 1 | Copper | Workbench | Tin mining (Clay Caves) |
| 2 | Tin | Tin Anvil | — |
| 3 | Iron | Iron Anvil | Gold & Scarlett mining (Azeos Wilderness) |
| 4 | Gold | Gold Anvil | — |
| 5 | Scarlett | Scarlett Anvil | Octarine mining (Desert of Beginnings) |
| 6 | Octarine | Octarine Anvil | Solarite mining (Sunken Sea) |
| 7 | Solarite | Solarite Anvil | Galaxite mining (Crystal Cavens / Forge) |
| 8 | Galaxite | Galaxite Anvil | Endgame equipment |

### Best-in-Slot Recommendations

- **Early Mining:** Copper Pickaxe → Tin Pickaxe (upgrade as soon as you find tin)
- **Mid Mining:** Iron Pickaxe → Scarlett Pickaxe (rush Scarlett for the damage jump)
- **Late Mining:** Octarine Pickaxe → Solarite Pickaxe → Galaxite Pickaxe
- **Early Combat:** Copper Sword → Tin Sword (or skip straight to Iron)
- **Mid Combat:** Iron Sword → Scarlett Sword (craft immediately on reaching Azeos)
- **Late Combat:** Solarite Sword or Rune Song (Rune Song is best for magic builds)
- **Ranged:** Copper Bow → Iron Bow → Octarine Bow (safest boss-killing option)
- **Early Armor:** Miner's set for mining speed, or mix and match defense pieces
- **Mid Armor:** Hunter set for ranged builds, Larva set for poison resistance
- **Late Armor:** Scarlett set for HP stacking, Ra-Akar's set for magic builds
