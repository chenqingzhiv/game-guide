## Day 1

1. Speak to the Core (central crystal)
2. Gather 10 Wood, 10 Stone, 5 Fiber
3. Craft Workbench > Stone Pickaxe > Stone Sword
4. Set up camp near the Core (safe zone)
5. Mine Dirt walls, find Copper ore
6. Cook raw meat on Campfire

## First Biomes

Dirt Biome (Easy): Wood, stone, copper
Larva Hive (Medium): Bug meat, silk
Clay Caves (Medium): Clay, tin ore
Azeos Wilderness (Hard): Iron, gold

Order: Core > Dirt > Larva > Clay > Azeos > Ruins

## Upgrades

Glowing Ring (first boss) for light.
Iron Pick for harder ores.
Ranged weapon for safe boss fights.
Lantern for permanent light.

Mining tip: Ores respawn after 10 minutes.
