# Core Keeper Items & Resources Database

**Every material you can dig, mine, and craft in the underground world of Core Keeper.** From Copper to Galaxite, this guide covers what you need and where to find it. Use the tables below to plan your mining routes and crafting progression.

> 💡 **Quick Tip:** You need progressively better pickaxes to mine higher-tier ores. Always prioritize upgrading your pickaxe before exploring new biomes.

---

## ⛏️ Ores & Metals Progression

| Ore / Metal | Pickaxe Tier Required | Found In (Biome) | Smelted Into | Used For |
|:------------|:---------------------|:-----------------|:-------------|:---------|
| **Copper Ore** | Copper (Starter) | Dirt Biome, Clay Caves | Copper Bar | Basic tools, workbenches |
| **Tin Ore** | Copper | Clay Caves, Larva Hive | Tin Bar | Bronze ingots, early upgrades |
| **Iron Ore** | Bronze | Forgotten Ruins, Desert of Beginnings | Iron Bar | Mid-game tools, armor |
| **Gold Ore** | Iron | Desert of Beginnings, Sunken Sea | Gold Bar | Jewelry, high-end crafting |
| **Platinum Ore** | Iron | Sunken Sea, Azeos' Wilderness | Platinum Bar | End-game armor & weapons |
| **Scarlet Ore** | Platinum | Molten Quarry | Scarlet Bar | End-game gear |
| **Octarine Ore** | Scarlet | Shimmering Frontier (end-game) | Octarine Bar | Post-game gear |
| **Galaxite Ore** | Octarine | Passage (post-game) | Galaxite Bar | Cosmic-tier gear |
| **Iron Ore Fragment** | Bronze | Dirt Biome (wild deposits) | Iron Bar (rare) | Alternative iron source |

## 📊 Biome Resource Map

| Biome | Primary Resources | Danger Level | Recommended Gear |
|:------|:-----------------|:-------------|:-----------------|
| **Dirt Biome** | Wood, Fiber, Copper, Tin | 🟢 Low | Copper tools |
| **Clay Caves** | Clay, Tin, Copper, Bomb Peppers | 🟡 Medium | Bronze tools |
| **Larva Hive** | Larva Meat, Tin, Silk, Emeralds | 🟡 Medium | Bronze armor |
| **Forgotten Ruins** | Iron, Gold, Emeralds, Cavelings | 🔴 High | Iron tools |
| **Desert of Beginnings** | Iron, Gold, Amethysts, Scarlet hints | 🔴 High | Iron pickaxe |
| **Sunken Sea** | Platinum, Gold, Diamonds, Fish | 🔴 High | Iron+ gear |
| **Azeos' Wilderness** | Platinum, Diamonds, Emeralds, Feathers | 🔴 High | Platinum gear |
| **Molten Quarry** | Scarlet Ore, Topaz, Lava enemies | ⚫ Extreme | Platinum+ gear |
| **Shimmering Frontier** | Octarine, Crystals, Cosmic materials | ⚫ Extreme | Scarlet gear |
| **The Passage** | Galaxite, Cosmic Dust, End-game loot | ⚫ Extreme | Octarine gear |

### Recommended Biome Order

> **Dirt Biome → Clay Caves → Larva Hive → Forgotten Ruins → Desert of Beginnings → Sunken Sea → Azeos' Wilderness → Molten Quarry → Shimmering Frontier → The Passage**
