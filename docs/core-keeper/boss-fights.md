## Boss Progression

Glurch (Dirt): Giant slime. Kite and shoot.
Ghorm (Dirt, deep): Huge worm. Dodge charge attack.
Malugaz (Clay): Fire mage. Use ranged + dodge fireballs.
Azeos (Wilderness): Sky boss. Shoot weak points.

Loot: Each boss drops unique crafting materials.

## Boss Prep

- Full armor set of current biome
- Ranged weapon (safest)
- 10+ food, 5+ bandages
- Clear arena of adds before engaging

Tip: Boss HP scales with player count.
