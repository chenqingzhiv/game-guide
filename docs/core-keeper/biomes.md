## Biome Details

### 1. Dirt Biome (Starter)

The Dirt Biome surrounds The Core and is the first area every player explores.

- **Environment:** Brown dirt walls, wooden bridges, pocket caverns
- **Difficulty:** Easy — enemies deal minimal damage
- **Required Gear:** Copper Pickaxe, Copper Sword, Fiber Hood/Tunic/Pants
- **Ores & Resources:** Copper Ore (abundant, orange-brown nodes), Tin Ore (rare near Clay Caves border), Wood from trees, Fiber from grass
- **Enemies:** Slime (basic, drops Slime), Boulder Slime (larger, drops Pebbles), Caveling (armed with pickaxe or sword, drops Caveling Meat), Gloop (ranged attacker, drops Gloop Goo)
- **Food Sources:** Caveling Meat, Gloop Goo
- **Points of Interest:** Caveling Camps (small huts with loot chests), The Core (respawn point), abandoned minecarts marking progression
- **Boss: Glurch the Abominous Swarm** — naturally present in Glurch's Lair (large egg-shaped room). Drops Glurch Eye, Copper Bar x10, Rope Ladder BP. Strategy: keep moving, avoid slime trail, use ranged attacks from platforms.

### 2. Clay Caves

Orange-brown clay walls that require a Tin Pickaxe or better to mine efficiently.

- **Environment:** Dense clay walls, narrow tunnels, underground lakes
- **Difficulty:** Medium — enemies hit harder than Dirt Biome
- **Required Gear:** Tin Pickaxe, Copper Armor (minimum)
- **Ores & Resources:** Tin Ore (primary, green-tinted nodes), Clay (for Clay Furnace), Gold Ore (rare deep pockets near Azeos transition)
- **Enemies:** Clay Guardian (humanoid golem, drops Clay Parts), Caveling, Rolly Slug (curls into a ball and rolls), Larva (rare, near Hive transition)
- **Food Sources:** Rolly Slug Meat, Caveling Meat
- **Points of Interest:** Clay Furnace Ruins, Ghorm's Tunnel (circular path carved by Ghorm), Underground Lakes (water source for farming)
- **Boss: Ghorm the Devourer** — roaming boss patrolling Ghorm's Tunnel. Cannot be summoned; must be hunted. Drops Ghorm's Husk, Ghorm's Jewel, Tin Bar x10. Strategy: chase while it burrows, mine through walls to intercept.

### 3. Forgotten Ruins

Ancient stone ruins filled with mechanical enemies and traps. Walls are stone/marble that cannot be mined without a Drill.

- **Environment:** Stone brick rooms, pressure plate traps, conveyor belts
- **Difficulty:** Medium — trap damage can be lethal
- **Required Gear:** Tin Pickaxe, Tin Armor, ranged weapon recommended
- **Ores & Resources:** Ancient Tech Scrap (from machines and chests), Gold Ore (rare), Bomb Cores (from enemies)
- **Enemies:** Robot Arm (melee, swings in an arc), Caveling Brute (stronger caveling with a club), Spore Cap (explosive, drops Spore Cap)
- **Traps:** Dart Traps (wall projectiles), Spike Traps (floor spikes), Bomb Traps (pressure plate triggered)
- **Food Sources:** Caveling Meat, Spore Cap
- **Points of Interest:** Ancient Forge (upgraded crafting station), Old Power Cores (lore), Ruins Loot Rooms
- **Boss: Malugaz the Corrupted** — summoned via Malugaz Skull found in ruins. Fought in a large arena room. Drops Malugaz Heart, Gold Bar x10, Ancient Gemstone. Strategy: destroy healing totems first, dodge shockwave attack.

### 4. Larva Hive

A fleshy, organic biome with tunnel-like passages. Walls require an Iron Pickaxe.

- **Environment:** Fleshy pink walls, egg sacs, hive structures
- **Difficulty:** Medium-Hard — enemies swarm
- **Required Gear:** Iron Pickaxe, Tin Armor minimum, AoE weapons
- **Ores & Resources:** Larva Silk (wall nodes + enemies), Larva Carapace (armor crafting), Bomb Cores, Scarlet Ore (rare, deep in hive)
- **Enemies:** Larva (charges), Larva Spitter (ranged acid), Hive Larva (large, spawns smaller larva), Bomb Larva (explodes on death)
- **Food Sources:** Larva Meat, Larva Egg
- **Points of Interest:** Hive Egg Chambers (large egg rooms), Silk Veins (renewable silk), Hive Mother Arena (central chamber)
- **Boss: Hive Mother** — summoned via Hive Mother Crystal (crafted from Hive Parts) at the central hive chamber. Drops Hive Mother Trophy, Scarlet Bar x5, Larva Ring. Strategy: prioritize killing spawned larva, use AoE attacks.

### 5. Azeos Wilderness

A surface biome with grass, trees, and open sky. Requires defeating Ghorm to unlock.

- **Environment:** Green grass, blue sky, giant trees, waterfalls
- **Difficulty:** Medium-Hard — large enemies with high HP
- **Required Gear:** Iron Tools, Iron Armor, ranged weapon
- **Ores & Resources:** Iron Ore (abundant, grey nodes), Gold Ore (deep, shiny yellow), Scarlet Ore (rare, purple-red nodes), Wood from giant trees
- **Enemies:** Caveling Guard (armored, shield + spear), Rolly Slug (Big) (large variant, high damage), Bat (flying, large groups), Caveling Shaman (ranged magic)
- **Food Sources:** Caveling Meat, Mushroom, Berry Bushes
- **Points of Interest:** Sky Titan Altar (summon Azeos), Giant Trees (rare wood), Wilderness Lake (fishing), Caveling Village (larger camp with better loot)
- **Boss: Azeos the Sky Titan** — summoned at Sky Titan Altar with Azeos Feather (found in wilderness). Drops Azeos Crown, Azeos Feather, Iron Bar x15. Strategy: dodge lightning strikes, attack during landing phase.

### 6. Desert of Beginnings

A vast desert biome with sand walls, cacti, and buried ruins. Very dangerous.

- **Environment:** Yellow sand walls, cactus plants, sandstone ruins
- **Difficulty:** Hard — high damage enemies, environmental hazards
- **Required Gear:** Scarlet Tools, Iron Armor minimum, Scarlet Armor recommended
- **Ores & Resources:** Scarlet Ore (abundant, purple-red nodes), Octarine Ore (rare, deep pockets), Sand, Cactus Fiber
- **Enemies:** Scarab (beetle, charges), Desert Caveling (sand-colored, higher stats), Sand Worm (burrows and emerges), Sand Bat (flying variant)
- **Food Sources:** Scarab Meat, Cactus Fruit, Desert Mushroom
- **Points of Interest:** Sand Titan Altar (summon Ra-Akar), Desert Ruins (buried ancient tech), Oasis (water source), Boiling Geysers (environmental hazard)
- **Boss: Ra-Akar the Sand Titan** — summoned at Sand Titan Altar with Ra-Akar Scarab (found in desert ruins). Drops Ra-Akar Fang, Ra-Akar Wing, Scarlet Bar x15. Strategy: watch for burrow attack, attack when surfaced.

### 7. Sunken Sea

An underwater biome requiring swimming gear. Accessible after defeating Azeos and Ra-Akar.

- **Environment:** Blue water walls, coral reefs, seaweed, shipwrecks
- **Difficulty:** Hard — unique movement constraints
- **Required Gear:** Scarlet+ Tools, Osmosis Gear (swim speed / fins), Octarine Weapons recommended
- **Ores & Resources:** Octarine Ore (primary, blue-green nodes), Galaxite Ore (rare, deep sea caves), Coral, Seaweed
- **Enemies:** Crab (melee, armored shell), Jellyfish (ranged electric), Submarine Guard (mechanical, high HP), Angler Fish (ambush)
- **Food Sources:** Fish (fishing), Crab Meat, Seaweed
- **Points of Interest:** Sea Titan Altar, Sunken Shipwrecks (loot + lore), Coral Reefs (octarine clusters), Abyssal Trenches (galaxite areas)
- **Boss: Omoroth the Sea Titan** — summoned at Sea Titan Altar with Omoroth Scale (from shipwrecks). Drops Omoroth Scale, Octarine Bar x15, Ocean Ring. Strategy: manage oxygen, use swim speed buffs, ranged attacks.

### 8. Shimmering Frontier (v1.0)

Endgame biome added in Core Keeper 1.0. Crystal-themed with highest difficulty.

- **Environment:** Purple/blue crystal walls, glowing nodes, alien landscape
- **Difficulty:** Very Hard — highest enemy damage and HP
- **Required Gear:** Octarine+ Tools, Galaxite Weapons, endgame armor sets
- **Ores & Resources:** Galaxite Ore (abundant, purple crystal nodes), Core Nodes (rare, ultimate crafting), Shivervine, Crystal Shards
- **Enemies:** Shiverling (crystalline humanoid), Crystal Crab (armored, reflective shell), Void Enemy (shadow, teleports), Crystal Flyer (airborne ranged)
- **Food Sources:** Crystal Fruit, Shiverling Meat, Void Essence
- **Points of Interest:** Core Node Deposits (late-game), Druidra's Grove (boss arena), Crystal Caverns (dense ore), Atlantean Ruins (final boss)
- **Bosses (3 endgame):**
  1. **Druidra** — Druidra's Grove. Drops Druidra Petal, Galaxite Bar x10. Strategy: destroy healing vines, stay mobile.
  2. **Morpha** — Shifting Crystal Maze. Drops Morpha Core, Shivervine x5. Strategy: move between platforms, clear slimes.
  3. **Atlantean Worm** — Atlantean Ruins. Drops Atlantean Worm Core (ultimate crafting). Phase-based fight requiring all previous gear.

## Pro Tips

1. **Light your path** — hostile mobs spawn in darkness. Place wall torches every 20 blocks.
2. **Mark your way back** — use colored floor tiles or torches to avoid getting lost.
3. **Biome transitions** — wall color changes signal you've entered a harder area.
4. **Rope ladders** — always carry 20+ when exploring downward.
5. **Fishing** each biome yields unique fish; Sunken Sea has the best recipes.
6. **Trap avoidance** — in Forgotten Ruins, trigger pressure plates from a distance.
7. **Oxygen** — craft Osmosis Gear before exploring the Sunken Sea.
