## Biome List

Dirt Biome: Starter area. Copper, wood, slimes.
Larva Hive: Bug meat, silk, larvae enemies.
Clay Caves: Tin, clay ore, clay guardians.
Azeos Wilderness: Iron, gold, large enemies.
Forgotten Ruins: Ancient tech, robots, traps.
Desert of Beginnings: Endgame area.

## Resources by Biome

Tin: Clay Caves (green-tinted rock)
Iron: Azeos Wilderness
Gold: Azeos Wilderness (deep)
Ancient Tech: Forgotten Ruins

Pro tip: Always carry torches. Biomes get progressively harder.
