# Raft Cooking Recipes & Food Guide

**Good food is the difference between surviving and thriving at sea.** This guide covers every cooking station, recipe, food buff, and the best foods for every situation in Raft.

---

## 🔥 Cooking Stations

| Station | Fuel | Unlock | Crafting |
|:--------|:-----|:-------|:---------|
| **Grill** | Planks | Start | 4x Planks + 4x Scrap + 1x Nail |
| **Cooking Pot** | Planks | Early | 4x Planks + 6x Scrap + 2x Nail + 1x Circuit Board |
| **Juicer** | - (Manual) | Early | 4x Planks + 4x Scrap + 1x Hinge + 1x Circuit Board + 1x Bolt |
| **Biofuel Refiner** | - | Mid-game | 6x Planks + 8x Scrap + 4x Nail + 2x Circuit Board + 1x Bottle |

## 🥩 Grilled Foods (Simple Cooking)

The Grill is the simplest cooking station — place raw food on it and wait for it to cook.

| Raw Food | Cooked Result | Hunger | Health | Thirst |

| Raw Potato | Baked Potato | +15 | +3 | -5 |

## 🏆 Best Food Recommendations

| Situation | Best Food | Why |
|:----------|:----------|:----|
| **Early Game (Day 1-10)** | Baked Potato / Grilled Fish | Easy to make, good value |
| **Exploring Islands** | Fish Stew / Simple Juice | Balanced food + thirst |
| **Combat / Shark Hunting** | Shark Dinner | +30% armor buff lasts 5 min |
| **Diving / Treasure Hunting** | Premium Juice | Sprint + Swim buffs combined |
| **Long Sailing** | Hearty Stew | Highest hunger value per slot |
| **Building** | Catfish Deluxe | Good hunger + affordable ingredients |
| **Survival Efficiency** | Salmon Salad | Best thirst-to-hunger combo |

## ⚡ Biofuel Refiner

Turn excess food into fuel for your engines:

| Input | Biofuel Output | Processing Time |
|:------|:---------------|:----------------|
| 5x Raw Potato | 1 Biofuel | 90s |
| 4x Any Fruit | 1 Biofuel | 90s |
| 3x Raw Fish | 1 Biofuel | 90s |
| 2x Cup of Vegetables | 1 Biofuel | 90s |

> 💡 **Tip:** Build a farm before you build engines. You'll need a steady stream of potatoes to keep the engines running during long voyages.
