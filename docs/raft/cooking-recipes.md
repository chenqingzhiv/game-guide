# Raft Cooking Recipes & Food Guide

**Good food is the difference between surviving and thriving at sea.** This guide covers every cooking station, recipe, food buff, and the best foods for every situation in Raft.

> 💡 **Food Basics:** Your hunger and thirst drain continuously. Hunger depletes at ~0.09/s, thirst at ~0.11/s. When either reaches zero, you lose ~0.75 HP per second. Cooked food always gives better stats than raw ingredients — never eat raw food unless it's an emergency.

## 🏆 Best Food Recommendations

| Situation | Best Food | Why |
|:----------|:----------|:----|
| **Early Game (Day 1-10)** | Baked Potato / Grilled Fish | Easy to make, good hunger value |
| **Exploring Islands** | Fish Stew / Simple Juice | Balanced food + thirst recovery |
| **Combat / Shark Hunting** | Shark Dinner | 🛡️ +30% armor buff lasts 5 min |
| **Deep Diving** | Head Broth / Premium Juice | 🫁 Oxygen buff + swim speed combined |
| **Long Sailing** | Hearty Stew | Highest hunger value per inventory slot |
| **Building / Base Work** | Catfish Deluxe | Good hunger + affordable ingredients |
| **Survival Efficiency** | Salmon Salad / Vegetable Soup | Best thirst-to-hunger ratio |
| **Resource Gathering** | Mushroom Omelette | ⛏️ Stomach buff extends mining sessions |
| **Story Island Progress** | Goat Stew + Premium Juice | Sprint + swim combo for exploration |

### 🏃 Buff Stacking Strategy

You can eat **one food and drink one juice** simultaneously for stacked buffs. Best combo for exploration:

> **Shark Dinner** (+30% Armor) + **Premium Juice** (+15% Sprint & Swim) = ultimate survival loadout

## 💡 Advanced Cooking Tips

1. **Always cook food** — raw ingredients give terrible stats and no buffs
2. **Prioritize the Cooking Pot** — soups and stews give bonus hunger effects that raw grilled food can't match
3. **Save berries for juice** — eating berries raw is wasteful; juice gives better thirst recovery plus buffs
4. **Stockpile potatoes** — they're the backbone ingredient for most recipes AND biofuel
5. **Fish in deeper water** — bigger fish (Pompano, Salmon) give more hunger per catch
6. **Hang recipe notes** — place found blueprints on your raft wall to remember ingredient combos
7. **Milk goats regularly** — Head Broth and other milk-based recipes are end-game essential for deep diving
