## First Day

1. Hook floating debris
2. Collect 10 Plastic, 6 Wood, 5 Palm Leaf, 3 Scrap
3. Craft Plastic Hook > Simple Purifier
4. Expand raft to 4x4
5. Build Research Table + Collection Net
6. Craft Fishing Rod for food

Pro tip: Shark attacks every few minutes. Swim in bursts.

## Essential Upgrades

1. Collection Net: Auto-collects items.
2. Water Purifier: Infinite water.
3. Grill + Smelter.
4. Receiver + Antennas: Find story islands.
5. Engine: Move against wind.

## Resource Management

Water: 1 cup every 3 min. Coconuts provide both drink and food.
Food: Cooked fish (13 hunger). Cooked potato (12). Cooked beet (10).

Always carry backup water and food when exploring islands.
