# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 6, 2026**

## Who We Are

Game Guide Club (game-guide.club) is a website dedicated to providing game guides, tips, and tools for gaming enthusiasts.

## Information We Collect

### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Browser type and version
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Approximate geographic location (country/city level only)
- Referring website or search term

This data is **anonymized** — we cannot identify individual visitors. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Cookies
Our site uses cookies for:

- **Google Analytics**: to track anonymous usage statistics
- **Site functionality**: to remember your preferences (e.g., dark mode, navigation state)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work as intended.

## Amazon Associates

Game Guide Club is a participant in the **Amazon Services LLC Associates Program**, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

As part of this program, when you click on an Amazon link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at **no additional cost to you**.

## Steam Affiliate Links

Some of our game pages contain affiliate links to **Steam** (store.steampowered.com). If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission. These links are clearly marked with a 🛒 icon.
