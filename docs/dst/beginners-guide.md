## First 10 Days

Day 1: Collect 30 grass, 30 twigs, flint, logs. Craft tools + torch.
Day 2: Science machine.
Day 3-4: Find base location near beefalo.
Day 5: Fire pit, chest, crock pot.
Day 5-10: Explore map, collect food.

Critical: Day 15 = first hound attack.

## Season Survival

Autumn (Days 1-20): Mild, explore.
Winter (21-35): Cold. Thermal stone, winter hat.
Spring (36-55): Rain. Umbrella, lightning rod.
Summer (56-70): Overheating. Ice flingo, chilled amulet.

## Sanity

Cooked meat: +5. Top hat: +3.3/min. Flowers: +5. Fire at night: +1/min. Darkness: -20/min.

Best sanity food: Cooked green mushrooms (+15 sanity). Taffy: 3 honey + 1 stick (+15 sanity).
