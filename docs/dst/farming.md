## Crops

Carrot: 10 days, 12.5 hunger. Corn: 15 days, 25. Dragon Fruit: 10 days, 75 (best for Dragon Pie). Pumpkin: 10 days, 37.5.

Best: Dragon Fruit Pie = 75 hunger, 40 health, 5 sanity.

## Giant Crops

Same crop close together + fertilizer + water + talking. Yield: 6-8 per harvest.

Best combo: Dragon Fruit + Tomato + Carrot.

## Bee Boxes

6-8 boxes with 20+ flowers nearby. 1 honey per 3.4 days.

Best recipes: Bacon & Eggs (75 hunger), Honey Ham (75), Meatballs (62.5), Pierogi (37.5+40 health).
