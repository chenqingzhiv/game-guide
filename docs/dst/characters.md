## Best for Beginners

Wilson: Balanced, grows beard.
Wendy: Ghost sister fights for you.
Wickerbottom: Knows all recipes.
Wolfgang: Strong when full.
Woodie: Werebeaver form.

Wendy is the strongest beginner.

## Intermediate

Wigfrid: Only meat. +25% damage, -25% taken.
Webber: Spider friends.
Winona: Repair buildings.
Warly: Chef. 5 food slots.
WX-78: Robot. Eats gears.

## Advanced

Wes: All stats reduced, challenge mode.
Maxwell: Powerful late-game.
Wurt: Merm allies.
Wormwood: Plant-based.
Walter: Pet Woby.
