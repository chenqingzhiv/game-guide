## Seasonal

Deerclops (Winter): 4000 HP, 75 AoE. Kite 3 hits, dodge.
Bearger (Autumn): 6000 HP, 100. Kite 4-5 hits.
Moose/Goose (Spring): 4000 HP. Use range.
Dragonfly (Summer): 27500 HP. Use pan flute.

## Raid Bosses

Spider Queen: 2500 HP. Fire staff.
Bee Queen: 22500 HP. Campfire wall + kiting.
Klaus: 10000 HP. Kill deer first.
Toadstool: 52500 HP. Weather pain.
Fuelweaver: 16000 HP. Don't let him heal.

## Loot Priority

Deerclops Eyebrella: rain protection.
Bearger Fur: insulated pack.
Dragonfly Scales: infinite fuel furnace.
Bee Queen Crown: best head slot.
