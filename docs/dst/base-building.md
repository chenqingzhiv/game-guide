## Location

Near beefalo, central map, near sinkhole, away from spider dens. Deciduous forest is best.

## Layout

18x18 tiles. Farm area, chests, icebox, drying racks, science/alchemy, garden, bee boxes.

## Walls

Grass (300 HP): temporary. Wood (500): standard. Stone (1000): endgame. Thulecite (2000): best.

Double-wall with 1-tile gap. 20+ tooth traps outside.
