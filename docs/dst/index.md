## What is DST?

Multiplayer survival in a dark fantasy world. Manage hunger, sanity, and health through harsh seasons. Fight seasonal giants and raid bosses. Steam #19 most played with 84K concurrent players.

### Survival Fundamentals

| Stat | How to Manage | Danger If Low |
|:-----|:--------------|:--------------|
| ❤️ **Health** | Healing food, tents, salves | Death |
| 🍖 **Hunger** | Cooked food, crock pot recipes | Starvation → health drain |
| 🧠 **Sanity** | Flowers, cooked green caps, tents | Shadow creatures attack |
