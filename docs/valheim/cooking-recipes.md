## Food Stats

Health (tanks, boss fights), Stamina (builders, archers), Health Regen, Stamina Regen.

Eat 3 foods at once.

Tiers: Yellow (30-50), Green (50-80), Blue (80-100), Purple (100+).

## Best Combos

Early: Queens Jam + Carrot Soup + Grilled Neck Tail.
Mid: Sausages + Turnip Stew + Wolf Skewer.
Endgame Tank: Lox Meat Pie + Honey Glazed Chicken + Fish Wraps (245 HP).
Endgame Mage: Yggdrasil Porridge + Misthare Supreme (260 stamina).

## Mead

Health Mead (heal 50-75), Stamina Mead (recover 50), Tasty Mead (+100% stam regen).

Resist: Frost, Poison, Fire. Build 3+ fermenters for batch brewing.
