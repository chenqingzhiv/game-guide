## Architecture

Steep roofs (47): Classic Viking longhouse.
Moderate (26): Modern Valheim style.
Flat: Castle aesthetic with tarred shingle.

Materials: Core Wood + Thatch (classic), Stone + Dark Wood (castle), Marble (Mistlands).

## Interior Layout

Great Hall: Central hearth, long dining table, trophy mounts, wall sconces every 4m.

Upper Floor: Individual rooms with bed + chest + torch. Shared balcony overlooking hall.

Basement: Forge line + smelter with chimney + storage.

## Comfort Level

Max 17 items: Dragon bed (2), banners (4), rugs (4), trophies (4), hearth (1), sitting log (4).

17 min Rested buff = +100% XP, +50% health regen.
