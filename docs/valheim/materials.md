# 📦 Valheim Materials & Forging Guide

*Last updated: July 2026 | Game version: 0.219.14+*

## 🔨 Metal Smelting Guide

### Smelting Ratios

| Ore | Smelter | Fuel per Ingot | Time | Yield |
|:----|:-------|:--------------:|:----:|:----:|
| Copper Ore | Smelter | 2 Coal | 60s | 1 Copper |
| Tin Ore | Smelter | 2 Coal | 40s | 1 Tin |
| Iron Scraps | Smelter | 3 Coal | 45s | 1 Iron |
| Silver Ore | Blast Furnace | 3 Coal | 50s | 1 Silver |
| Black Metal Scraps | Blast Furnace | 2 Coal | 30s | 1 Black Metal |
| Flint Metal Ore | Blast Furnace | 4 Coal | 60s | 1 Flint Metal |

### Alloy Crafting

| Alloy | Component 1 | Component 2 | Ratio | Made In |
|:------|:------------|:------------|:-----:|:--------|
| **Bronze** | Copper | Tin | 2:1 | Forge |
| **Black Metal** | Black Scrap | — | 1:1 | Blast Furnace |

**Bronze efficiency tip:** The 2:1 ratio means every 6 Copper + 3 Tin = 3 Bronze. Always mine Tin and Copper in a **2:1 ratio** to avoid excess.

### Fuel Efficiency

| Fuel Type | Burn Time | Best For |
|:----------|:---------:|:---------|
| Wood | 15s each | Early game, quick smelting |
| Coal | 30s each | All stages — most efficient |
| Core Wood | 25s each | Decent alternative to coal |

**Pro tip:** 1 Wood → 1 Coal via Charcoal Kiln. Wood is renewable (plant trees), so turn all spare wood into coal.

## 📋 Pre-Boss Material Checklist

### Before Eikthyr
- [ ] 10 Wood, 5 Stone (basic shelter)
- [ ] Flint axe, hammer, crafting table
- [ ] Leather tunic (optional)
- [ ] Bow + 50 arrows

### Before The Elder
- [ ] **12 Surtling Cores** (smelter + kiln)
- [ ] **6 Tin + 3 Copper** → 3 Bronze (weapons)
- [ ] Finewood bow
- [ ] Bronze axe (for finewood)

### Before Bonemass
- [ ] **At least 60 Iron** (mace + shield + armor)
- [ ] **30 Ancient Bark** (Longship + Fermenter)
- [ ] **Poison Resistance Mead** (mandatory)
- [ ] Stagbreaker (for Bonemass adds)

### Before Moder
- [ ] **50+ Silver** (Frostner + armor)
- [ ] **Wishbone** (from Bonemass)
- [ ] **Obsidian arrows** (100+)
- [ ] **Health & Stamina meads**
- [ ] Lox cape (cold resist + insulation)

### Before Yagluth
- [ ] **Padded armor set** (full set)
- [ ] **Black Metal weapons** (sword or mace)
- [ ] **Fire Resistance Barley Wine** (mandatory)
- [ ] Lox pie, fish wraps (high-tier food)

### Before The Queen
- [ ] **Carapace armor set**
- [ ] **Krom, Mistwalker, or Demolisher**
- [ ] **Wisps** (Wisp light required)
- [ ] **Refined Eitr** (if using magic)
- [ ] **Major healing + stamina meads**

### Before Fader (Ashlands Boss)
- [ ] **Flint Metal gear** (weapon + shield)
- [ ] **Ashlands ship**
- [ ] **Asksvin saddle** (mount)
- [ ] **Lava-resistant food**
- [ ] **Best meads** (major healing + stamina)
