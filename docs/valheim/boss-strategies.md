## Boss Order

Eikthyr (Meadows) > Elder (Black Forest) > Bonemass (Swamp) > Moder (Mountains) > Yagluth (Plains) > Queen (Mistlands).

## Key Prep

Eikthyr: Bow + 100 fire arrows. Kite and shoot.
Elder: Fire arrows. Use terrain for cover.
Bonemass: Poison res mead + blunt weapon + 200 arrows.
Moder: Need cold res. Frost arrows + stagbreaker.

## Rewards

Eikthyr: Hard Antler (pickaxe).
Elder: Swamp Key (sunken crypts).
Bonemass: Wishbone (buried treasure).
Moder: Dragon Tear (artisan table).
Yagluth: Fuling totem.
Queen: Seal breaker.
