## Day 1

1. Collect 20 Wood + 20 Stone + 10 Fiber
2. Build Pickaxe, Hatchet, Campfire, Bed
3. Craft 5 Pal Spheres
4. Catch your first Pal (Lamball or Cattiva)

Pro tip: Weaken Pals to 30% HP before throwing spheres.

## First 5 Days

- [ ] 4x4 base
- [ ] Craft 20+ spheres
- [ ] Catch 10+ Pals
- [ ] Build Palbox
- [ ] Assign Pals to tasks
- [ ] Cook food
- [ ] Craft a Bow

Base location: Flat near water + trees + ore.

## Tech Tree Priority

Palbox (2) > Bed (3) > Bow (5) > Workbench (7) > Mount (10) > Furnace (12) > Grappling Gun (15)

## Best Early Pal

Daedream (auto-combat follow mode, strong early). Use Lifmunk for gathering, Cattiva for transport.
