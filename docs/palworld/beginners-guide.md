## Palworld Survival — Your First Week

This guide covers your first 7 days in Palworld. Follow this progression to establish a strong base, catch a solid team, and set yourself up for mid-game success.

---

## Day 1: Establish Shelter and Tools

### Priority Checklist

1. **Collect resources**: Gather at least 20 Wood, 20 Stone, and 10 Fiber from the ground
2. **Build a Pickaxe** (5 Wood + 5 Stone) — mine ore nodes for stone and Paldium fragments
3. **Build a Hatchet** (5 Wood + 5 Stone) — chop trees efficiently
4. **Build a Campfire** (10 Wood + 2 Stone) — cook raw meat, survive nights
5. **Craft 5 Pal Spheres** (3 Paldium + 1 Wood + 1 Stone each) — Paldium found in blue ore rocks
6. **Catch your first Pal**: Weaken to ~30% HP, then throw a sphere

### Best First Pals

| Pal | Location | Work Suitability | Why Catch It |
|-----|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Lamball | Starting area | Farming (Lv1) | Wool for cloth, food source |
| Cattiva | Starting area | Handiwork (Lv1), Transporting (Lv1) | Best early worker — boosts carrying capacity |
| Chikipi | Starting area | Gathering (Lv1) | Eggs for food, easy catch |
| Foxparks | Starting area | Kindling (Lv1) | Portable fire — craft its harness for flamethrower |
| Tanzee | Forest areas | Handiwork (Lv1), Lumbering (Lv1) | Shoots guns, helps with base work |

**Pro tip**: Aim for the head when weakening Pals — it does slightly more stun damage. Use a club or bare fists to avoid killing them accidentally.

## Days 2-3: Build Your First Base

### Base Location Requirements

Pick a spot that has ALL of these within a short walking distance:

| Resource | Why You Need It | Ideal Proximity |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| Trees | Wood for building, fiber, charcoal | Within 20 steps |
| Stone/Ore | Stone for base, Paldium for spheres | Within 30 steps |
| Water | Pal fluid, drinking, fishing later | Within 50 steps |
| Flat ground | Building space for 4x4 base | At least 6x6 tiles |

**Recommended starting location**: The plateau at coordinates 180, -430 (near the initial spawn point). Flat, close to forests, ore nodes, and water.

### Base Setup (4x4 Layout)

```
[ WALL ][ WALL ][ WALL ][ WALL ]
[ WALL ][ Palbox][ Bed  ][ WALL ]    ← Palbox (center)
[ WALL ][Chest ][Feed ][ WALL ]    ← Feed box + storage
[ WALL ][ WALL ][ DOOR ][ WALL ]
```

### Essential Base Buildings

| Building | Materials | Tech Level | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------|
| Palbox | 5 Paldium + 10 Wood + 10 Stone | Lv2 | Assign Pals, fast travel |
| Bed | 20 Wood + 10 Fiber | Lv3 | Sleep through night |
| Pal Bed | 30 Wood + 10 Fiber | Lv4 | Pals sleep = less downtime |
| Feed Box | 30 Wood + 5 Stone | Lv5 | Auto-feed assigned Pals |
| Workbench | 20 Wood + 10 Stone | Lv7 | Craft weapons, tools, spheres |
| Campfire x2 | 20 Wood + 4 Stone | Lv1 | Cook food, warmth at night |

## Days 4-5: Gear Progression

### Tech Tree Priority

| Tech Level | Unlock | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| 2 | Palbox | Required for everything |
| 3 | Bed | Skip night, set spawn |
| 5 | Bow + Arrow | Ranged damage, hunt safely |
| 6 | Cloth Outfit | Cold resistance, unlock armor slot |
| 7 | Workbench | Craft more complex items |
| 8 | Old Bow (upgrade) | Double damage over basic bow |
| 9 | Cooking Pot | Recipes with stat bonuses |
| 10 | Mount (Nitewing or Eikthyrdeer) | Fast travel |
| 12 | Furnace | Smelt ore into ingots |
| 13 | Crossbow | Major DPS upgrade over bow |

```
Club (Lv1) → Bow (Lv5) → Old Bow (Lv8) → Crossbow (Lv13) → Musket (Lv21) → Assault Rifle (Lv30)
```

## Pro Survival Tips

1. **Temperature management**: Use the Cloth Outfit by day 4. Cold at night = move near fire or equip a torch. Heat in desert = craft a cool shirt or bring ice Pals.
2. **Pal sphere economy**: Always have 10+ basic spheres on hand. Weaken Pals with a bow from range before throwing spheres. Back-crit sphere throws have a higher catch rate.
3. **Fast travel network**: Build a Palbox near each new area you explore. You can fast travel between Palboxes for free.
4. **Stamina management**: Climbing and sprinting share the stamina bar. Don't climb vertical surfaces unless you're sure you can reach the top.
5. **Night strategy**: Nights are dangerous — stronger Pals spawn. Sleep in your bed or use the early hours to mine ore near your base.
6. **Capture experience**: Each unique Pal capture gives bonus XP. Catch every species you see at least once for rapid leveling.
7. **Ranch building**: Build a Ranch (Lv12) to passively produce resources from Pals — Wool (Lamball), Milk (Mozzarina), Honey (Cinnamoth), Eggs (Chikipi).
8. **Base defense**: Walls are essential by day 5. Raids start around level 7. Build wooden walls around your base perimeter and assign combat Pals to defense.
