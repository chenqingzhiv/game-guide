## Breeding Basics

Build a Breeding Farm (level 19). Place 1 male + 1 female Pal. They produce eggs. Incubate eggs. Child inherits random traits from parents.

## Trait Inheritance

Parents pass 2-4 traits each. Rarer traits have lower inheritance chance.

Best traits: Legend, Lucky, Musclehead, Ferocious.

Speed traits: Swift, Runner, Nimble.

## Best Breeding Combos

Anubis: Relaxaurus + Celaray (best worker)
Jormuntide Ignis: Blazehowl + Jormuntide
Grizzbolt: Mossanda + Rayhound

Goal: Get Legendary + Lucky + Musclehead + Ferocious on your combat Pals.
