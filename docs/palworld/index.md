## What is Palworld?

Open-world survival crafting where you collect Pals to fight, build, farm, and explore. **Pokémon with guns meets factory automation** — catch wild Pals, assign them to production lines, ride them across the world, and fight tower bosses.

### Why Palworld Matters

- **100+ Pal species**: Each with unique work skills, combat moves, and passive traits
- **Base automation**: Pals mine, craft, cook, water crops, generate power
- **Breeding system**: Pass down traits for perfect worker or fighter Pals
- **Open world**: Five major zones with increasing difficulty
- **Co-op multiplayer**: Explore and build with friends

## Getting Started

1. **Spawn → gather wood/stone → build palbox**
2. Catch your first Pals (Lamball, Cattiva for base workers)
3. Build basic production: berry farm, logging site, stone pit
4. **Level 10**: Unlock saddle → explore mid zones
5. **Level 20+**: Fight first tower boss
