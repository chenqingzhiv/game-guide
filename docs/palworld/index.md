## What is Palworld?

Open-world survival crafting where you collect Pals to fight, build, farm, and explore. Pokemon with guns meets factory automation.

## Key Features

- Capture 100+ Pal species
- Build automated bases with Pal labor
- Fight tower bosses and legendary Pals
- Breed Pals for perfect traits
- Explore a massive open world
