## Bionic Upgrades

Steel Teeth (+50% gather), Iron Stomach (+50% carry), Bionic Limb (+30% build), Reinforced Bones (+50% lifespan), Brain Implant (+25% work speed).

Priority: Lifespan > Work > Gather > Build > Carry.

## Mega-Colony Design

District A: Residential + Food (20).
District B: Industrial Wood/Planks (40).
District C: Metal/Science (40).
District D: Power/Water (20).
District E: Mega-Farming (30).

Connect with aqueducts + tunnels + belts.

## Population 200+

15+ lodges, 4-5 breeding pads, diverse crops, 20 water/day consumption.

Reduce breeding in drought. Gravity batteries for power during drought.
