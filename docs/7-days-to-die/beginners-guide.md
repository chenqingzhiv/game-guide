## First Day

1. Find a town, loot houses for food and tools
2. Gather 100 Wood, 30 Stone, 5 Fiber
3. Craft Stone Axe > Shovel > Spear
4. Build 4x4 wood shack with door
5. Place bedroll + campfire + chest

Critical: Place bedroll before night or you respawn randomly!

## Looting Priority

Food > Water > Medicine > Weapons > Ammo > Resources

Best POIs: Houses (low risk), Hardware stores (tools), Hospitals (medicine), Military camps (best loot).

## First Blood Moon (Night 7)

Prep: 5x5 base with 2-block walls, iron doors, barbed wire, spike traps, 200+ arrows, 5 bandages.

During: Stay on roof, pick off zombies from above, repair walls between waves.
