## 🎯 Understanding Loot Stage

Your **Loot Stage** determines the quality and type of loot you find. It increases as you level up and explore harder biomes.

> **Loot Stage = Player Level + (Player Level × Difficulty Bonus) + Biome Bonus**

| Factor | Bonus | Details |
|:-------|:------|:--------|
| Player Level | +1 per level | Every level directly increases loot stage |
| Difficulty Bonus | +0.5×–2.0× | Higher difficulty settings = faster loot scaling |
| Biome Bonus | +0 (Forest) to +40 (Wasteland) | Harder biomes grant higher loot stage |

### Biome Difficulty & Loot Quality

| Biome | Difficulty | Loot Bonus | Best For |
|:------|:-----------|:-----------|:---------|
| Pine Forest | Easy | +0 | Early game, safe looting |
| Desert | Medium | +10 | Mid-game loot, heat hazard |
| Snow | Medium-Hard | +20 | Mid-game loot, cold hazard |
| Wasteland | Hard | +40 | Best loot, extreme danger |

## 📈 Gear Progression Strategy

### Early (Loot Stage 0–15, Days 1–7)

| Priority | Item | Why |
|:---------|:-----|:----|
| 1 | Stone Spear or Bone Knife | Reliable melee, easy to craft |
| 2 | Bow + Stone Arrows | Stealth ranged for early hunting |
| 3 | Cloth Armor Set | Basic protection, craftable from rags |
| 4 | Stone Shovel | Dig buried supplies, gather clay |
| 5 | Pipe Pistol / Pipe Shotgun | Found loot — save 9mm/shells |

**Goal:** Survive first Blood Moon, establish base.

### Mid (Loot Stage 15–40, Days 7–28)

| Priority | Item | Why |
|:---------|:-----|:----|
| 1 | Iron Armor Set | Major defense upgrade |
| 2 | Hunting Rifle or Marksman Rifle | Reliable ranged at medium loot stage |
| 3 | Shotgun or Pump Shotgun | Crowd control for horde nights |
| 4 | Iron Pickaxe | Faster mining |
| 5 | Pistol or SMG | Backup ranged weapon |

**Goal:** Explore Desert/Snow biomes, establish forge + chemistry station.

### Late (Loot Stage 40+, Days 28+)

| Priority | Item | Why |
|:---------|:-----|:----|
| 1 | Steel Armor Set | Best defense in the game |
| 2 | M60 or Sniper Rifle | Endgame firepower |
| 3 | Auto Shotgun | Horde night efficiency |
| 4 | Auger / Chainsaw | Max resource gathering |
| 5 | Compound Bow | Stealth 1-shot kills |

**Goal:** Wasteland exploration, T6 loot grind, endgame challenges.

## 💡 Quick Tips

- **Loot everything early.** Every crate, dresser, and cabinet can drop weapon/armor parts.
- **Salvage operations.** Use a wrench/ratchet on cars, AC units, and appliances for mechanical parts and rare components.
- **Biome bracket.** Hit Desert loot (bonus +10) as soon as you have basic iron gear for a loot stage boost.
- **Trader quests.** Completing quests for traders gives powerful loot rewards and dukes (currency).
- **Quality 6 is the cap.** The difference between Q5 and Q6 is small — don't grind forever if you have a solid Q5 set.
