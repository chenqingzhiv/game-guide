# Sons of the Forest 🌲

**The island holds secrets.** Survive a remote island overrun by cannibals and mutants in this terrifying open-world horror survival game.

[![Sons of the Forest Banner](/assets/images/sons-forest/library_hero.jpg){: .game-hero }](/assets/images/sons-forest/library_hero.jpg)

## What is Sons of the Forest?

Sons of the Forest is an open-world survival horror game. You play as part of a team sent to find a missing billionaire on a remote island. After a helicopter crash, you must survive against cannibalistic tribes, terrifying mutants, and the harsh wilderness. Featuring dynamic building, AI companions, and a story-driven campaign.

## Getting Started Guide

### 1. First Steps

1. **Loot the helicopter crash site** — grab the emergency pack and pistol
2. **Find Kelvin** — your AI companion (he's nearby on the beach)
3. **Build a basic shelter** — use the build book (B key)
4. **Collect water** — use the GPS locator to find ponds
5. **Avoid the cannibals** — stay hidden at night, don't light big fires

### 2. Essential Tools (Priority Crafting)

| Tool | Materials | Use |
|:-----|:----------|:----|
| **Axe** | 2 sticks + 1 rope + 1 rock | Tree cutting, combat |
| **Spear** | 2 sticks + 1 duct tape | Ranged hunting, fishing |
| **Bow** | 1 stick + 1 rope | Silent ranged combat |
| **Modern Axe** | Found in cave | Better tree cutting |
| **Rebreather** | Found in cave | Underwater exploration |
| **Shovel** | Found in cave | Dig up graves and artifacts |

### 3. Food & Water

| Source | Method |
|:-------|:-------|
| **River water** | Drink directly (unsafe) or boil with pot |
| **Rain water** | Place a tarp + pot under open sky |
| **Fish** | Craft a spear, stand in shallow water |
| **Deer/Rabbit** | Trap or hunt with bow / spear |
| **Berries** | Harvest from bushes — edible, low nutrition |
| **Rations** | Loot from crates — emergency food |

## Building Tips

- **Start small** — a 3×3 log cabin with one room
- **Stick structures are weak** — upgrade to logs when possible
- **Build a drying rack** early for meat preservation
- **Stick traps** are cheap and effective against cannibal patrols
- **Defensive walls** can be built from sharpened sticks
- **Solar panels** (found in bunkers) power electric fences

---

## Combat Tips

- **Headshots** kill most enemies in 1-2 hits
- **Fire is your friend** — molotovs and fire arrows are effective against mutants
- **Block before you strike** — holding block absorbs damage
- **Mutants are immune to some weapons** — use the shotgun or explosives
- **Run away** when outnumbered — heal and re-engage
- **Caves** — bring a lighter (for light), food, and a weapon

---

## Story Progression Hint

- **Follow the GPS markers** — they lead to key locations
- **Find the shovel** — it unlocks the main story bunkers
- **The VIP** is in a hidden bunker — look for maintenance hatches
- **Time capsules** give hints about the island's backstory
- **There are multiple endings** — choices matter
