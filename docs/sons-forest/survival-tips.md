## First Day

1. Find water source
2. Collect 10 sticks + 5 rocks
3. Build shelter (10 sticks)
4. Craft bow (5 sticks + 3 cloth + rope)
5. Kill rabbit for food

Dont: Attack cannibals day 1, sleep without fire, enter caves unprepared.

## Essential Crafts

Tools: Stone Axe, Bow, Spear, Crafted Club.
Armor: Leaf (10 leaves + 3 cloth) immediate. Upgrade to Bone (5 bones + 2 cloth) by day 3.

## Base Building

4x4 wood foundation, 1-wall height, single entrance with spikes. Interior: Fire pit + drying rack + storage.

Upgrade to stone walls (fireproof), 2-walls + spikes, deadfall traps, zipline network.
