## Prerequisites

Golden Armor, Shovel, Rebreather, Rope Gun, Glider, 3 Keycards, fully upgraded weapons, 10 Meds, 10 Energy Mix.

## Final Cave

Level 1: The Descent. Armsy, Virginia mutants.
Level 2: The Laboratory. Flooded, use rebreather.
Level 3: The Reactor. 3 power couplings puzzle. Mega-Fingers boss.
Level 4: The Artifact Chamber. Golden Armor required.

## Multiple Endings

Ending A (Escape): Activate in Sky mode. Helicopter arrives.
Ending B (Destroy): Fire mode. Explosion destroys facility.
Ending C (Power): Cube mode. Defense grid activated. You rule the island.
