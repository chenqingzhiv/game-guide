## First 30 Minutes

1. Mine iron + copper by hand
2. Build burner miner on coal + iron
3. Craft furnace > smelt iron plates
4. Build assembling machine for gears
5. Research automation (red science)

Iron gear production is critical for everything.

## Science Setup

Red science: 1 copper plate + 1 gear = 1 pack. 5 assemblers = 45 SPM.
Green science: 1 inserter + 1 belt = 1 pack. Feed from red science line.

Scale up before expanding to next science tier.

## Biter Management

Build radar to spot biter bases. Clear nests near pollution cloud. Wall + turret chokepoints. Don't let evolution get too high before you have red ammo.
