## 🎯 What is Factorio?

Factorio is the **original factory automation game** that defined the genre. You crash-land on an alien planet and must build increasingly complex factories to manufacture a rocket and escape. Everything from a single mining drill to a 10,000-item-per-minute megabase starts with **one iron plate**.

**Why it's the gold standard:**
- **Infinite replayability** — randomized maps, mods, multiplayer
- **Pure mathematics** — ratios are exact, no RNG in production
- **Mature community** — 40M+ mod downloads, thousands of blueprints

## 📋 Quick Tips for New Players

| Phase | Tip |
|:------|:----|
| 🪓 **First 30 min** | Build near iron+copper+stone+coal nodes. Don't box yourself in. |
| ⚡ **Power** | 1 boiler + 2 steam engines per offshore pump. Keep coal feed simple. |
| 🔧 **Early bus** | Build a main bus (4 iron / 4 copper / 2 green circuits / 2 steel). Leave room to expand. |
| 🚂 **Trains** | Two-way tracks work for early game. One-way networks for megabases. |
| 🧊 **Oil** | Start at blue science. 1 pumpjack feeds ~10 refineries. Flare or crack heavy oil. |
| 🏗️ **Modules** | Speed in miners/pumps, Productivity in assemblers/refineries, Efficiency everywhere else. |

## 🎓 Progression Roadmap

```
Burner Phase              → Red/Green Science        (~2 hours)
Steam Power + Basic Oil   → Blue/Gray Science        (~6 hours)
Rail Network + Modules    → Yellow Science           (~12 hours)
Kovarex + Rocket Control  → Rocket Launch            (~20 hours)
Megabase Expansion        → 1,000+ SPM              (~50+ hours)
```

## 🏆 Achievement Goals

| Achievement | Strategy |
|:------------|:---------|
| **There is no spoon** (rocket < 8h) | Rush red→green→gray science. Skip solar. Direct insertion wherever possible. |
| **Lazy bastard** (< 111 crafts) | Let assemblers do everything. Carry burner miners for early power poles. |
| **Steam all the way** | No solar panels. Pumpkins is best uranium-free fuel for boilers. |
| **Golem** (1GJ with no solar) | Nuclear or Spidertron-run rocket fuel in boilers. |
