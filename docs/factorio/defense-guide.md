## Evolution Factor

Time, pollution, and nest destruction increase evolution.

0-25%: Small biters (yellow ammo).
25-50%: Medium (red ammo).
50-75%: Big (piercing + flamethrower).
75-100%: Behemoths (uranium ammo).

## Turret Configs

Early: 4 turrets per 10 tiles, double wall, yellow ammo.
Mid: Triple wall, flamethrowers at 5-tile intervals, red ammo.
Late: Dragon's teeth, uranium turrets, lasers, flamethrowers. Repair packs via logistics.

## Pollution Management

Efficiency Module 1 in miners (-30% pollution). Solar power (zero pollution). Electric furnaces. Tree preservation.
