## Smelting

Early: 48 furnace array (24 per side). 1 red belt ore = 1 belt plates.

Electric: 48 furnaces with Speed 3 + beacons. 4 blue belts output.

## Rail Intersections

4-way: Elevated, chain on entry, path on junction, block on exit. Supports 4 trains.

T-junction: 4x4 chunks. Same signaling.

Train stacker: 6 waiting bays.

## Defense Blueprints

Perimeter segment: 3-layer stone + dragon's teeth + 4 gun + 2 flamethrower + 2 laser.

Artillery outpost: 6 cannons + full defense + train resupply.
