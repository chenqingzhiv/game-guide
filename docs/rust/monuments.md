## Monument Tiers

Tier 1: Gas Station, Supermarket (no rad, low risk)
Tier 2: Harbor, Water Treatment (green rad, medium)
Tier 3: Airfield, Power Plant (blue rad, high)
Tier 4: Military Tunnels, Cargo Ship, Oil Rig (red rad, extreme)

## Puzzle Walkthroughs

Harbor: Green fuse near dock, insert in red switch box.

Water Treatment: Find fuse in main building, insert at control panel.

Launch Site: Find fuse in red hangar. Keycard in office. Elevator to top.

## Radiation Protection

Green (0-10): Wood/burlap
Blue (10-25): Hazmat + rad pills
Red (25-50): Hazmat + 10+ pills
Extreme (50+): Hazmat + 20+ pills
