## Explosives

Satchel: 480 damage. Requires 480 sulfur.
C4: 550 damage. 440 sulfur + 20 cloth + 2 metal.
Rocket: 350 damage (direct). 1400 sulfur + 150 metal.

Cost per stone wall (500 HP): 2 C4 or 3 rockets.

## Raiding Strategies

Online: Door camping, breach with rockets, fight defenders.
Offline: More time, use cheapest explosives, loot and leave.

Always scout first. Check for auto-turrets + traps.

## Defenses Against Raiders

Honeycomb: 6 walls per layer.
Peak-down shooting floor.
Auto-turrets at all entrances.
External TCs prevent raid towers.

Place loot in multiple scattered boxes.
