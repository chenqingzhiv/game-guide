## Resource Nodes

Stone: 500 HP, 25-40 per hit. Metal: 600 HP, 15-25 per hit. Sulfur: 700 HP, 10-20 per hit. HQM: 300 HP, 3-6 per hit.

Best tool: Jackhammer (2.5x rate).

## Gathering Routes

Forest: 5-6 stone nodes, 8-10 trees, 3-4 metal nodes. ~15 min for 3k stone, 5k wood, 1.2k metal.

Mountain: 8 sulfur nodes, 4 HQM nodes, 4 metal nodes. ~20 min.

## Farming Base

2x1 stone base with compound. External gates. 4 furnaces. Quarry base placed near mining quarry. Protect with auto-turret.
