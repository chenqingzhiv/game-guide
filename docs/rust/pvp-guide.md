## Weapon Tiers

Primitive: Bow, Spear, Eoka (30-50 damage)
Tier 1: Revolver, Nailgun, DB (40-70)
Tier 2: Thompson, Custom SMG, SAP (30-50)
Tier 3: AK-47, LR-300 (40-80)

## Spray Patterns

AK-47: First 5 shots slight pull. 6-15 medium pull. 15-30 aggressive. Practice 10 min daily.

Thompson: Very stable, slight upward.

Custom SMG: High fire rate, hard pull.

## Movement

Strafe peeking: peek, shoot, hide. Never peek same spot.

Jump peeking: sprint, jump, aim, shoot, land, retreat.

Crouching: press C mid-spray to throw off aim.

## Gear

Primitive: Wood armor + crossbow + bandages
Roaming: Roadsign + Thompson + 4 med syringes
Endgame: Metal + AK + 6 syringes + HV ammo
