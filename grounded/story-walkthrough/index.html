    Grounded Story Walkthrough

    Grounded's story unfolds through six major labs, each guarded by unique bosses and full of puzzles. The main quest takes approximately 30 hours, with 60+ hours for 100% completion including optional bosses. This guide provides a complete walkthrough from the opening cutscene to the final machine repair.

    Story Overview & Lab Order

    Recommended Lab Order

    Order Lab Location Recommended Gear BURG.L Chips
    1 Hedge Lab Hedge (southeast yard) Tier 1 weapon + acorn armor Super Chip #1
    2 Pond Lab Underwater (central pond) Tier 2 weapon + gill tubes Super Chip #2
    3 Haze Lab Haze (southwest yard, gassy) Gas mask + Tier 2 armor Super Chip #3
    4 Black Anthill Lab Under black anthill Tier 2/3 weapon + poison resist Super Chip #4
    5 Mant Lab Shed's interior (upper yard) Tier 3 weapon + mant-specific build Super Chip #5
    6 Undershed Lab Under the shed (final) Tier 3+ gear, full preparation Final chip + ending

    Important: Labs have level scaling. Attempting Hedge Lab in Tier 1 armor vs Haze Lab in Tier 1 armor are very different difficulty levels. Upgrade between each lab.

    Lab 1: Hedge Lab (Super Chip #1)

    Location: Southeast section of the yard, inside the large hedge bushes. Recommended Level: Tier 1 weapon, Acorn/Red Ant armor.

    Getting There

    1. From the Mysterious Machine, head southeast past the oak tree
    2. Enter the hedge area (large bushes, berry chunks)
    3. Look for a large branch leading upward — follow the spider webs
    4. Navigate the hedge maze (spiders on branches, orb weavers below)

    Lab Navigation

    Area Enemies Objective
    Hedge exterior Orb Weaver Jr., Spiderlings Find the branch path up
    Hedge branch maze Orb Weavers, Bombardier Beetles Navigate webbed branches
    Hedge lab entrance Orb Weaver (boss-tier) Enter the lab through the broken window
    Lab interior Robot turrets (friendly after chip) Navigate laser grid puzzles
    Lab core TAYZ.T (boss fight) Defeat TAYZ.T, claim Super Chip #1

    TAYZ.T Boss Fight

    Phase HP % Behavior Strategy
    1 100–70% Shoots laser beam in line pattern Strafe left/right, attack between beams
    2 70–40% Adds spinning laser attack Jump over spinning beam, attack from flank
    3 40–0% Adds orb-weaver spider spawns + faster lasers Kill spiders first, then focus boss

    TAYZ.T Weakness: Sour damage. If you have a Sour weapon (unlikely at this stage), it does 50% more damage. Otherwise, use the Mint Mallet (find the mint container near the oak tree first).

    Loot: Super Chip #1, BURG.L Science Shop unlock (cooking recipes), Hedge Lab BURG.L chip.

    Reward

    • BURG.L unlocks: Advanced crafting recipes
    • Recipe: Insect Axe (cut weed stems)
    • Recipe: Spider Fang Dagger (venom weapon)
    • 2,500 Raw Science from lab scanning

    Lab 2: Pond Lab (Super Chip #2)

    Location: Underwater, central pond near the oak tree. Recommended Level: Tier 2 weapon, Ladybug armor, Gill Tubes (underwater breathing).

    Required Gear for Pond Exploration

    Item Purpose How to Get
    Gill Tubes Breathe underwater (45s → 90s) Crafted: 2 Silk Rope, 1 Water Flea Mandible
    Flippers Swim faster Crafted: 2 Lilypad Wax, 1 Water Flea Mandible
    Bubble Helmet Oxygen refills from bubbles Crafted: 2 Silk Rope, 2 Clay, 1 Water Flea
    Fin Flops Extra speed (mutation) Unlock from Pond Lab

    Tip: You can surface for air between dives. The pond is only 20m deep. No vehicle needed.

    Lab Navigation

    Area Enemies Objective
    Pond surface Water fleas, diving bell spiders Find the lab entrance tube
    Lab tube (entrance) Diving bell spiders Swim down the vertical tube
    Lab interior (flooded) None (spiders can't enter) Navigate flooded rooms
    Lab core Puzzle: Align laser to unlock door Solve 2 reflector puzzles
    Lab core (unlocked) None Scan all lab data items
    Lab exit Diving bell spider ambush Swim fast back to surface

    Laser Puzzle Solution

    Room 1 (2 reflectors): 1. Place reflector 1 at the corner, angle it 45 degrees toward the center 2. Place reflector 2 at the center, angle it 45 degrees toward the laser receiver 3. Adjust mirrors until laser hits the receiver

    Room 2 (3 reflectors + splitter): 1. Use the beam splitter at the source (splits into 2 beams) 2. Reflector 1 redirects beam A to receiver A 3. Reflector 2 redirects beam B around the corner 4. Reflector 3 redirects beam B to receiver B

    Loot: Super Chip #2, Pond Lab BURG.L chip, Bubble Helmet blueprint.

    Reward

    • BURG.L unlocks: Fishbowl Head (aquatic breathing)
    • Recipe: Koi Scale armor
    • Recipe: Fin Flops mutation
    • 2,000 Raw Science from lab scanning

    Lab 3: Haze Lab (Super Chip #3)

    Location: Southwest section of the yard, inside the gas-filled haze zone. Recommended Level: Tier 2 weapon, Ladybug/Koi armor, Gas Mask.

    Required Gear

    Item Purpose How to Get
    Gas Mask Breathe in haze Crafted: 2 Gnat Fuzz, 2 Clover Leaf, 2 Silk Rope
    Berry Charcoal Filter Fuel for gas mask Crafted: 2 Charcoal Ash, 1 Berry Leather
    Insect Hammer Break Haze Lab entrance plug Crafted: Bombardier Part + Boiling Gland

    Warning: The haze deals 5 HP/s damage without a gas mask. Your gas mask filter lasts 10 minutes. Bring spare filters.

    Lab Navigation

    Area Enemies Objective
    Haze exterior Stinkbugs, infected weevils, ladybugs Navigate through gas clouds
    Lab entrance Rubble + plug Smash plug with Insect Hammer
    Haze lab interior Infected insects (spores), robot turrets Navigate rubble, avoid spores
    Lab core Infected Ladybug (optional boss) Defeat or sneak past
    Lab core (deep) Puzzle: Spore reactor alignment Activate 3 spore vents

    Infected Ladybug Strategy

    Attack Description Counter
    Charge Rush forward 10m Side-step, attack from flank
    Spore Burst AoE spore cloud (confusion) Run 15m away, wait for clear
    Slam Front leg slam Perfect block → 3-hit window
    Explosive Death On death, explodes in spore cloud Run away when HP hits 0

    Loot: Super Chip #3, Haze Lab BURG.L chip, Infected Ladybug head (smoothie ingredient).

    Reward

    • BURG.L unlocks: Mutations slot +2 (total 3)
    • Recipe: Salt Morning Star (salty damage)
    • Recipe: Gas Arrow (stinkbug gas)
    • 3,000 Raw Science from lab scanning

    Lab 4: Black Anthill Lab (Super Chip #4)

    Location: Under the black ant hill in the upper yard, near the sandbox. Recommended Level: Tier 2/3 weapon, Fire Ant armor or Roly Poly.

    Lab Navigation

    Area Enemies Objective
    Ant hill exterior Black worker ants, black soldier ants Enter the anthill through main entrance
    Ant hill tunnels Black soldier ants, ant queens (patrol) Navigate 3-level tunnel network
    Lab entrance (sealed) None Use Black Ant Mandible to open seal
    Lab interior Robot turrets, small spiders Navigate storage rooms
    Lab core Assistant Manager (boss fight) Defeat the robot

    Assistant Manager Boss Fight

    Phase HP % Behavior Strategy
    1 100–66% Laser barrage + turret spawns Destroy turrets first, dodge lasers
    2 66–33% Adds shockwave AoE + electric floor Jump over shockwaves, stay off glowing floor panels
    3 33–0% Enrages (speed doubled), adds spider spawns Pop a healing item, go aggressive on openings

    Weakness: Sour damage. Bring a Sour weapon if possible. Otherwise, use Mint Mace for stun.

    Loot: Super Chip #4, Assistant Manager Keycard, Black Anthill BURG.L chip.

    Reward

    • BURG.L unlocks: New trinkets in shop
    • Recipe: Black Ox Hammer (Tier 3 smashing tool)
    • Recipe: Sour Battle-axe (if you have Sour Glob)
    • 3,000 Raw Science

    Lab 5: Mant Lab (Super Chip #5)

    Location: Inside the shed (northeast upper yard). Recommended Level: Tier 3 weapon, Roly Poly or Mant armor, full mutations.

    Getting to the Shed

    1. Navigate through the upper yard (orchid mantis, fire ants, ox beetles)
    2. Enter the shed through the broken window (north wall)
    3. Inside the shed, navigate through tool shelves, dust piles, and spider webs
    4. Find the Mant's arena (large open area with webs on the ceiling)

    The Mant Boss Fight

    The Mant is the hardest mandatory boss in Grounded. Prepare thoroughly.

    Phase HP % Behavior Strategy
    1 100–75% Melee combos (2–3 hits), web spit (immobilize) Perfect block combo, cut webs with dagger
    2 75–50% Adds mant nymph adds (4 spawns) Kill adds immediately, they heal the Mant
    3 50–25% Adds ground pound (jump to avoid), enrage Stay mobile, jump every ground pound telegraph
    4 25–0% Double speed + all abilities simultaneously Pop all heals, go aggressive, parry everything

    Mant Weakness: Fresh (salty) damage. Spicy damage is 25% reduced. Mint does neutral.

    Recommended Loadout
    Armor Mant (full) or Roly Poly (full)
    Weapon Club of the Mother Demon or Coaltana
    Mutation 1 Parry Master
    Mutation 2 Meat Shield
    Mutation 3 Mithridatism (poison resist) or Coup de Grass
    Healing 10+ Fuzz on the Rocks, 5+ Beefy Slurps

    Loot: Super Chip #5, Mant Claw x2–4, Mant Head, Mant's Keycard (opens Undershed Lab).

    Reward

    • BURG.L unlocks: Mutations slot +2 (total 5)
    • Recipe: Mant armor set
    • Recipe: Coaltana (fire sword, +50% spider damage)
    • 5,000 Raw Science

    Lab 6: Undershed Lab (Final)

    Location: Under the shed, accessible via keycard from the Mant. Recommended Level: Full Tier 3 armor + weapons, 10+ healing items, 3+ meals.

    Lab Navigation

    Area Enemies Objective
    Undershed entrance Spider nest (orb weavers, wolf spiders) Fight through or sneak
    Undershed tunnels Infected spiders, robot guards Navigate 3-level underground complex
    Laser room Laser grid puzzle Align 4 lasers to unlock the main door
    Keycard room Spider ambushes Find all 3 keycards on body bags
    Broodmother arena Broodmother (final boss) Full-strength Broodmother fight
    Machine room None Repair the shrinking machine

    Final Laser Puzzle

    Room layout: Large circular room with 4 laser emitters and 4 receivers.

    Solution: 1. Place reflector at position A (north corner), aim at receiver 1 2. Use beam splitter at source B, sending one beam to receiver 2 and one to position C 3. Reflector at position C sends beam to receiver 3 4. Reflector at position D (final corner) sends beam to receiver 4 5. All 4 lasers must hit simultaneously for 3 seconds

    3 Keycard Locations

    Keycard Location Danger
    Red Body bag near the entrance tunnel 2 orb weavers guarding
    Blue Body bag behind the spider egg nest 4 spiderlings + 1 orb weaver
    Green Body bag hanging from ceiling (needs arrow to cut) Infected wolf spider below

    Broodmother (Final Boss)

    Phase HP % Behavior Strategy
    1 100–66% Standard attacks (bite, web spit, egg spawn) Focus on perfect blocking
    2 66–33% Adds poison cloud, faster attack speed Mithridatism mutation is mandatory
    3 33–0% Enrages, spawns spiderlings constantly, arena floods with poison Use AoE to clear spiderlings, burst boss between spawns

    Broodmother Weakness: Spicy (fire) damage. Coaltana is the best weapon.

    Critical Prep
    Mutation Mithridatism (poison immunity — without it you die in 10 seconds in Phase 3)
    Mutation Parry Master (broodmother combos are 4–5 hits in Phase 3)
    Mutation Meat Shield (extra HP cushion)
    Weapon Coaltana (level 5+, spicy damage)
    Armor Mant set (attack + execution healing)
    Healing 15+ Beefy Slurps, 5+ Fuzz on the Rocks
    Smoothie Boost juice (+20% damage for 10 minutes)

    Loot: Broodmother Head (wall mount), Broodmother Fang x6, Broodmother Venom x4, Club of the Mother Demon blueprint, Broodmother BURG.L chip.

    Ending: Repair the Machine

    After defeating the Broodmother:

    1. Enter the machine room (large chamber with the shrink ray device)
    2. Insert all 5 Super Chips into the console
    3. Solve the final alignment puzzle (match 3 rotating rings)
    4. Activate the machine repair sequence
    5. Watch the end cutscene

    Post-Game Content

    Activity What You Get
    Shrink/grow at will Teleport pads between yard scales
    Buy all BURG.L items Everything unlocked
    Optional: Wasp Queen Dust Wasp Armor, Wasp ranged weapon
    Optional: Orchid Mantis Mantis Armor (stealth), Mantis Spear
    Optional: IBM (Infected Broodmother) IBM trinkets, hardest fight in game
    Achievement hunting 100+ achievements
    NG+ Start over with all gear (scaled enemies)

    Gold Cards (100% Completion)

    Creature Gold Card Effect
    All bug types Unlocks gold statues for base decoration
    All bosses Unlocks boss-themed base items
    Gold Card drop rate 1% per kill (10% with dissection expert)
    Dissection Expert mutation +10% gold card drop rate

    Time to 100%: 80–100 hours. Most of this is farming gold cards which are rare drops.

    Story Walkthrough Tips

    1. Scan every lab item — Each lab has 15–20 scannable items. Scanning gives Raw Science + lore progress.
    2. Read all PDAs — Lab PDAs contain story context (what happened to the scientists, how the shrinking happened).
    3. BURG.L quests — After each Super Chip, BURG.L offers new quests. Do them for Raw Science.
    4. Don't skip optional bosses — The Wasp Queen drops the best ranged weapon. The Orchid Mantis drops the best stealth armor.
    5. Lab shortcuts — Each lab has a shortcut that opens after completing it (connects back to the yard surface).
    6. Boss summoning items — Some bosses (Broodmother, Wasp Queen) require summoned items crafted from specific bug parts.
    7. Build a lab outpost — Place a base with a respawn point near each lab entrance before attempting it.
    8. Bedrolls save progress — Save before each boss fight. Dying sends you back to your last bedroll.
    9. Multiplayer scaling — Bosses scale HP +50% per additional player. Don't bring extra players unless they're geared.
    10. Gliders save time — Between labs, use the web glider from the oak tree to navigate the yard quickly.
