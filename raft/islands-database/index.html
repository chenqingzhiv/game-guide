    🗺️ Island Type Overview

    🟢 Small Islands

    Small islands are randomly generated and respawn as you sail. They are quick to visit and provide basic resources. Marked with a green icon on the Receiver. No story progression. Each has seagull nests with eggs. Some may have a single goat. Underwater reefs around the island contain scrap, metal ore, and copper ore.

    Typical visit: 2–5 minutes. Great for early-game resource grinding.

    🟡 Large Islands

    Large islands are fixed locations and do not despawn once visited. They are marked with a yellow icon on the Receiver. Large islands have: - Tameable animals (goats, llamas, cluckers) - Treasure dig spots (use Shovel) - More abundant and diverse resources - Larger underwater reef systems - Higher-tier ore deposits

    Typical visit: 10–20 minutes. Essential for animal capture and mid-game resource needs.

    🔴 Story Islands

    Story islands are fixed progression locations that advance the narrative. They are marked with red icons on the Receiver and must be visited in order (each gives coordinates to the next). These islands feature: - Unique puzzles and environmental challenges - Hostile enemies (not just Bruce) - Unique blueprints not obtainable elsewhere - Journal entries revealing the backstory - Critical progression items and equipment unlocks

    Typical visit: 30–90 minutes each. Some require multiple trips (especially Balboa and Tangaroa).

    Clucker (Chicken) Large Islands, Balboa ✅ Yes Eggs (food, biofuel) Most efficient biofuel source

    Seagull All islands ❌ No Feathers, Drumsticks Shoot with Bow for feathers

    Explosive Waddler Balboa Island ❌ No Explosive Goo Explodes on death — needed for Net Launcher ammo
    Rat Radio Tower, Vasagatan ❌ No — Slow melee enemies
    Screaming Rat Vasagatan ❌ No — Charging attack, more dangerous
    Hyena Caravan Island ❌ No Raw Hide, Meat Fast melee enemy, found in packs
    Warthog Caravan Island ❌ No Leather Aggressive charging attack
    Lurker Tangaroa, Varuna Point, Utopia ❌ No — Humanoid enemy with ranged and melee attacks
    Poison Pufferfish Tangaroa, Varuna Point, Temperance (water) ❌ No Explosive Goo (variant) Poison spikes in water — dangerous to swim near
    Polar Bear Temperance ❌ No Raw Hide, Meat Very aggressive, high damage. Icy biome only
    Mudthog Utopia ❌ No — Hyena-like creature found on final island
    Mama Bear Balboa Island (cave) ❌ No Raw Hide, Raw Meat, Trophy Boss enemy. Large health pool, high damage
    Rhino Shark Varuna Point, Utopia ❌ No — Boss enemy. Destroys pillars and terrain

    📦 Resource Guide by Island Need

    What You Need Best Island Type Why
    Wood / Planks Small Forest Island or Large Forest Island Dense tree cover, fast chopping
    Scrap Any island (underwater reef) Reefs around every island — Small Sandy for quick dives
    Metal Ore Small Rocky Island or Large Mountain Island Cave systems have rich deposits
    Copper Ore Large Mountain Island Deep caves produce the most copper
    Titanium Ore Caravan Island, Tangaroa, Varuna Point Story islands with deep underwater or cave deposits
    Sand / Clay Small Sandy Island Fast surface gathering
    Animals (tame) Large Tropical Island Guaranteed goat, llama, and clucker spawns
    Food (produce) Large Tropical Island Mangoes, bananas, potatoes, beets, pineapple
    Honey Balboa Island Only reliable source of bees and honey
    Explosive Goo Balboa Island (Explosive Waddlers) Needed for Net Launcher to capture animals
    Leather Caravan Island (Warthogs) Needed for Backpack and armor upgrades
    Wool Balboa Island (Llamas) Needed for Warm Clothes (Temperance) and armor
    Fresh Water Small Island with Pond Emergency water without purifier
