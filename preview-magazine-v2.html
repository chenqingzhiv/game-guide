Valheim

Survive, build, and conquer in a Viking purgatory.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🌍 Procedurally generated open world — Meadows to Ashlands, 7 unique biomes
  • ⚔️ Real-time combat with parry, block, dodge, and 7 legendary Forsaken bosses
  • 🏗️ Full structural physics building — walls, roofs, supports, terraforming
  • 👥 Up to 10-player co-op with shared world progression

💡 Pro Tips

  • Never eat just one food — eat 3 different foods for max HP + stamina
  • Dig a moat around your base — most enemies can't cross deep water
  • Poison resist mead is mandatory before entering the Swamp biome
  • Parry is king — a well-timed block opens enemies for bonus damage

Factorio

The gold standard of factory automation.

🎯 Key Features

  • ⚙️ Infinite replayability — randomized maps, exact production ratios, no RNG
  • 🚂 Deep logistics — conveyor belts, trains, bots, circuits, and nuclear power
  • 🌌 Space Age DLC adds 4 new planets with unique mechanics
  • 📊 40M+ mod downloads, thousands of community blueprints

💡 Pro Tips

  • Build near iron + copper + stone + coal nodes. Don't box yourself in.
  • Use a main bus (4 iron / 4 copper / 2 green circuits / 2 steel)
  • Speed modules in miners/pumps, Productivity in assemblers/refineries
  • Leave room to expand — double the space you think you need

Satisfactory

3D first-person factory builder with exploration.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🌍 30 km² hand-crafted open world — forests, cliffs, caves, deserts
  • 🏭 First-person perspective — walk inside your factories
  • 🔧 9 Tiers of technology with 6 logistics layers (belts to drones)
  • 👫 Up to 4-player co-op with shared progression

💡 Pro Tips

  • Build on foundations — never place machines directly on the ground
  • Rush to Coal Power (Tier 3) — build at least 8 generators
  • Build twice as much space as you think you need
  • Automate everything you consume regularly — build a 'mall'

Dyson Sphere Program

Interstellar factory simulation — build a star-encompassing megastructure.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🌌 True 3D space — build on spherical planets, belts curve with terrain
  • 🚀 Interplanetary logistics — fly between planets, build across star systems
  • ☀️ Visible Dyson sphere — fly right up to it and watch it rotate
  • 🤖 Mecha progression with combat (Dark Fog update)

💡 Pro Tips

  • Don't over-invest in your starter base — you must leave your home planet
  • Use Planetary Logistics Stations early to eliminate belt spaghetti
  • Ship raw ores, not intermediate products — 5× more transport-efficient
  • Overbuild your power grid by 3× — every tower and machine chews power

Shapez 2

Pure logistics puzzle optimization — no combat, no survival.

🎯 Key Features

  • ⬡ Infinite procedurally generated map — never run out of space
  • 🧩 Deep logic puzzles — cut, combine, stack, and color geometric shapes
  • 🔌 Logic wires and binary signals for complex conditional processing
  • 🎨 7+ shape colors with mixing mechanics (primary → secondary colors)

💡 Pro Tips

  • Build modular — copy-paste blueprint groups, don't rebuild from scratch
  • Use stackers early — two simple shapes → one complex shape
  • Color separate from shape — mix dye into clear shapes
  • Upgrade belts often — speed matters more than efficiency in Shapez 2

Timberborn

Beaver-powered colony survival with realistic water physics.

🎯 Key Features

  • 💧 Realistic water physics — rivers flow, evaporate, and flood in real-time
  • 🏗️ Vertical construction — platforms, stilts, and multi-level districts
  • 🐾 Two beaver factions: Folktails (nature) vs Ironteeth (industrial)
  • 🌊 Seasonal cycles — survive multi-day droughts, rebuild in wet seasons

💡 Pro Tips

  • Start with Folktails — their wood economy is more forgiving
  • Build a dam and reservoir before Day 6 — first drought hits Day 10-15
  • Don't overpopulate early — keep 8-12 beavers until stable
  • A deep, narrow reservoir evaporates slower than a wide, shallow one

Grounded

Shrunk to the size of an ant. Survive the backyard.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🐛 Insect combat with perfect blocking and creature weaknesses
  • 🏗️ Full structural building — walls, roofs, ziplines, scaffolding
  • 🕵️ Mystery narrative — discover labs, audio logs, and the truth
  • 👥 Up to 4-player co-op, shared world progression

💡 Pro Tips

  • Craft a Pebblet Axe and Hammer immediately — your first tools
  • Wolf Spiders patrol the oak tree — avoid at all costs early game
  • Grass planks are heavy — build a pallet at your base for storage
  • If it's bigger than you, assume it can kill you in 2-3 hits

Sons of the Forest

Horror survival on a remote island overrun by cannibals.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🌲 Large handcrafted island — forests, caves, snow mountains, beaches
  • 🤖 AI companion Kelvin — follows orders, builds, fetches resources
  • 🏗️ Full structural freedom — log cabins, defensive walls, ziplines
  • 👥 Up to 8-player multiplayer co-op

💡 Pro Tips

  • Loot the helicopter crash site first — grab the emergency pack and pistol
  • Build a basic shelter immediately using the build book (B key)
  • Headshots kill most enemies in 1-2 hits
  • Fire is your friend — molotovs and fire arrows devastate mutants

V Rising

Awaken as a vampire. Build your castle. Feast on blood.

Enshrouded

Rise from the ashes of a fallen kingdom.

🎯 Key Features

  • 🌍 Seamless open world — forests, deserts, mountains, Shroud-covered ruins
  • ⚔️ Action combat with dodge rolls, parries, and skill-based abilities
  • 🏗️ Voxel-based terrain shaping — dig into cliffs, build into hills
  • 👥 Up to 16-player co-op with shared progression

💡 Pro Tips

  • Scavenge scrap materials from ruined structures first
  • Avoid the red Shroud — it damages you over time
  • Stamina management is critical — don't spam attacks
  • Upgrade your Flame Altar to unlock fast travel points
