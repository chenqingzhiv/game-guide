    🔫 Rust PvP Guide

    Data version: Rust 2026 | Last updated: 2026-07

    Rust PvP is unforgiving. The player with better gun control, positioning, and game sense usually wins — even against someone with better gear. This guide covers everything from weapon stats to advanced combat tactics.

    🗡️ Weapon Comparison

    Melee Weapons

    Weapon Damage Swing Rate DPS Special
    Bone knife 18 Fast 30 +25% damage from behind
    Combat knife 25 Fast 35 Silent, fast draw
    Longsword 45 Slow 28 High single hit
    Mace 35 Medium 25 Low-tier
    Salvaged sword 55 Slow 32 Best melee in game

    Primitive Weapons

    Weapon Damage Range Reload Notes
    Bow 50 (headshot: 100) 35m 0.75s Silent, cheap arrows
    Crossbow 70 (headshot: 140) 50m 1.2s Good mid-range
    Spear (thrown) 55 20m Instant One-hit kill headshot
    Eoka pistol 75 15m 2s (unreliable) RNG-based ignition

    Tier 1 Guns

    Weapon Damage Headshot Range Magazine Fire Rate
    Revolver 23 46 25m 6 250 RPM
    Python 42 84 30m 6 200 RPM
    Nailgun 25 50 20m 12 300 RPM
    Waterpipe shotgun 150 (7 pellets × 21) 300 10m 1 Slow

    Tier 2 Guns

    Weapon Damage Headshot Range Magazine Fire Rate DPS
    Thompson 17 34 35m 20 500 RPM 142
    SAR (Semi-Auto Rifle) 35 70 60m 16 200 RPM 116
    Custom SMG 14 28 25m 24 700 RPM 163
    DB Shotgun 210 (10 pellets × 21) 420 12m 2 100 RPM 350 (burst)
    Pump Shotgun 192 (8 pellets × 24) 384 10m 6 60 RPM 192

    Tier 3 Guns

    Weapon Damage Headshot Range Magazine RPM DPS Spray Pattern
    AK-47 🏆 35 70 55m 30 400 233 Moderate (learnable)
    LR-300 31 62 50m 30 450 233 Easiest spray
    MP5 22 44 35m 30 550 202 Very controllable
    Bolt Action 70 140 80m+ 4 35 40 Single shot (sniping)
    M249 20 40 50m 100 500 167 Wild spray
    Spas-12 180 (10 pellets × 18) 360 15m 6 67 200 Close range king

    🏆 Best all-rounder: AK-47. Best recoil after practice, high damage, versatile at all ranges. Best new player gun: Custom SMG or Thompson (cheap, controllable).

    🛡️ Armor Comparison

    Armor Sets

    Set BPs DPS Reduction Speed Penalty Protection Cost
    Naked/Rags 0 0% None None Free
    Hide 5 15% -5% Low 20 cloth
    Wood 10 20% -5% Low 300 wood
    Roadsign 15 30% -15% Medium 6 metal frags
    Metal facemask 20 35% -10% High (head) 8 HQM
    Chest plate 20 40% -20% High (chest) 12 HQM
    Full HQM 28 55% -30% Best 20 HQM + 12 HQM

    Armor Slot Priorities

    Slot Best Option Why
    Head Metal facemask Headshots are deadly — protect your head
    Chest Metal chest plate Body absorbs most bullets
    Legs Roadsign or hide Less important, but protection helps
    Feet Hide boots or roadsign Foot protection against traps

    💡 Pro Tip: Wear Metal Facemask + Metal Chest Plate and roadsign pants for the best protection-to-speed ratio. Full HQM is too slow for open field fights.

    🎯 Recoil & Spray Control

    Recoil Control Basics

    Weapon Pattern Vertical Horizontal Difficulty
    AK-47 Moderate Pull down consistently Slight left-right drift ★★★★★
    LR-300 Easy Minimal pull Almost none ★★☆☆☆
    Thompson Easy Slight pull Random spread ★★★☆☆
    Custom SMG Moderate Up-left drift Wobble ★★★☆☆
    MP5 Very easy Almost none Minimal ★☆☆☆☆

    AK-47 Spray Training

    Bullet 1–5:  Slight pull down (aim at neck)
Bullet 6–10: Medium pull down
Bullet 11–20: Hard pull down + slight left-right adjust
Bullet 21–30: Full pull (crosshair is now below target)

    Training method: Build a wall with colored blocks at 20m. Fire full mags at the blocks. Adjust your mouse pull until all bullets hit a 1-block area.

    🏃 Movement & Positioning

    Movement Rules

    Rule Why
    Always strafe (A/D) while firing Standing still = easy headshot
    ADAD spam Crouch-strafing throws off opponents
    Use cover (rocks, trees, walls) Never fight in the open
    Peek advantage Swing wide → fire → retreat behind cover
    Don't repeak the same angle Enemy will pre-aim it
    Jump peeking Quick jump over cover to spot enemies
    Crouch spam Disrupts headshot aim

    Peeking Strategies

    Peek Type When to Use Risk
    Jiggle peek Check if someone is holding angle Low — fast in/out
    Wide peek Commit to a fight, full damage trade High — enemy can hit you
    Crouch peek Surprise the enemy (they aimed head height) Medium
    Jump peek Spot enemies behind wall Low (but predictable)
    Pre-fire peek Hold fire, round corner, fire immediately Medium

    Positioning Priority

    1. High ground — you see more, harder to hit
    2. Cover — tree, rock, base wall, monument structure
    3. Escape route — never trap yourself in a dead end
    4. Crosshair at head height — always. Walk with ADS at head level.
    5. Third-person advantage — corners with open sides allow you to see without being seen

    ⚕️ Healing & Combat Drugs

    Medical Items

    Item Heal Use Time Crafting
    Bandage 10 HP 2s 5 cloth
    Medical syringe 25 HP 2.5s 1 bandage + 1 cloth
    Large medkit 50 HP + broken limb 5s 4 syringes + 1 tech trash
    Cauterizer 10 HP/s for 10s Instant use Rare loot

    Combat Drugs

    Drug Effect Duration Side Effect
    Pump Jack +50 HP (temporary) 30s -20% accuracy after
    Stim Coffee +25% move speed 45s Heart damage (-10 HP)
    Mace (beer) +50% damage resist 15s Blurred vision
    Green Berrie +5 HP regen per 5s 60s None

    ⚠️ Use drugs before a fight, not during. Pump Jack gives temp HP that absorbs first 2–3 bullets.

    🔫 Advanced Combat Techniques

    Quick Switching

    Technique How When
    Shotgun + SAR swap Fire shotgun → swap to SAR for follow-up Close range
    Bolt + Python swap Fire bolt → swap to Python for finishing shot Mid range
    Bow + crossbow Double-primitive surprise Early game

    Grenade Tactics

    Grenade Damage Radius Effect
    F1 Grenade 55 (direct) 10m Frag damage
    Smoke grenade 0 10m Visual cover
    Flashbang 0 (blind 3s) 8m Blinds enemies
    Molotov 40 + burn 5m Area denial

    Night Fighting

    Tool Effect
    Night vision goggles Green-tinted vision (expensive but OP)
    Weapon flashlight Visible to everyone (gives away position)
    Weapon laser Only visible when ADS (gives away position slightly)
    Campfire/torch Don't use — you become a target

    🗺️ PvP by Biome

    Biome Advantages Dangers Strategy
    Forest Lots of cover, trees Ambushes everywhere Listen for footsteps. Check tree lines.
    Desert Open sightlines No cover Long-range fights. Bring a bolt.
    Snow Reduced visibility Cold damage White hazmat = camo advantage
    Mountains High ground Hard to move Control the peak. Use bolt action.
    Coastline Boat escapes Open beaches Watch the water for approaching boats
    Monuments Best loot Most players Clear before looting. Check corners.

    🧠 Mind Games & Strategy

    Tactic How When
    Door camping Wait at a base door When you know someone's inside
    Bait loot Drop a stack of scrap, ambush grabber Anywhere
    Third-party Let two groups fight, clean up survivors Always when possible
    Fake raiding Explosive noise near enemy base Makes them panic-despawn
    Drop and swap Drop a med kit, bait enemy to pick it up Close quarters
    Silent footstep Walk (don't run) to approach Sneaking up on campers

    ✅ PvP Checklist

    • [ ] Metal facemask + chest plate
    • [ ] 2 med syringes (hotbar slots 3–4)
    • [ ] Weapon(s): AK + bolt / SAR + pump
    • [ ] Ammo: 2+ stacks for primary
    • [ ] Bandages (stack of 6+)
    • [ ] Weapon flashlight/laser (optional)
    • [ ] Grenade (1–2 for distractions)
    • [ ] Escape strategy — know where to run
    • [ ] Callouts for teammates (if grouped)
    • [ ] Confirm enemy position before pushing
