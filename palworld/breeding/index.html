    🧬 Breeding Guide

    Palworld Breeding Mechanics

    Breeding lets you combine two Pals to produce an egg that hatches into a specific offspring. This is the only way to get Pals with perfect passive skill combinations and the highest stats.

    Breeding Farm Setup

    Requirements

    Requirement Details How to Get
    Tech Level 19 Unlock the Breeding Farm Spend 2 Technology Points
    Materials 100 Wood + 20 Stone + 10 Paldium Standard gathering
    1 Male + 1 Female Pal Same species or cross-breed Must be of opposite gender
    1 Cake Required to start breeding See recipe below

    How It Works

    1. Place the Breeding Farm on flat ground
    2. Assign one male and one female Pal to the farm
    3. Place 1 Cake in the farm's inventory box
    4. Wait — breeding takes 5-10 minutes (real time) per egg
    5. Collect the egg from the farm
    6. Incubate the egg in an Egg Incubator (Lv7, 5 Paldium + 10 Stone + 30 Bone)

    Incubation times: Common egg = 2 min, Uncommon = 5 min, Rare = 10 min, Epic = 15 min, Legendary = 20 min. Use Campfires near the incubator to speed up incubation (warm eggs hatch faster).

    Passive Trait Inheritance

    How Traits Pass Down

    Scenario Inheritance Rate Explanation
    Both parents have same trait ~50% Highest chance — breed two Pals with Musclehead + Ferocious
    One parent has the trait ~25% Moderate chance
    Neither parent has trait 0% Will not appear unless randomly mutated
    Random mutation ~5% A new trait can appear on offspring that neither parent had

    Best Passive Traits

    Combat Traits

    Trait Effect Rarity Best For
    Legend +20% ATK, +20% DEF, +15% Speed ★★★★★ (Only from Legendary Pals) All endgame Pals
    Lucky +15% ATK, +15% Work Speed ★★★★ (Rare) Combat + Worker hybrids
    Musclehead +30% ATK, -10% Work Speed ★★★★ Pure DPS Pals
    Ferocious +20% ATK ★★★ Good secondary DPS trait
    Burly Body +20% DEF ★★★ Tank Pals

    Speed Traits (Mounts)

    Trait Effect Best For
    Swift +30% Movement Speed Ground mounts
    Runner +20% Movement Speed Ground mounts (stack with Swift)
    Nimble +10% Movement Speed Budget option
    Eternal Flame +30% Fire ATK (Jormuntide Ignis only) Combat mounts

    Work Traits

    Trait Effect Best For
    Artisan +50% Work Speed Base workers
    Serious +20% Work Speed Good second work trait
    Workaholic +30% Work Speed, +10% SAN consumed Late-game base workers

    Cake Production Chain

    You need cake constantly for breeding. The recipe requires 5 ingredients:

    Ingredient Source Quantity Needed
    Flour Wheat at Mill 5 per cake
    Egg Chikipi Ranch 1 per cake
    Milk Mozzarina Ranch 1 per cake
    Red Berry Farm or wild bushes 2 per cake
    Honey Cinnamoth Ranch 1 per cake

    Automated Cake Farm Setup

    1. Plant wheat: 50 wheat seeds in a plantation (Lv15)
    2. Ranch for eggs: Assign 2 Chikipi to Ranch
    3. Ranch for milk: Assign 2 Mozzarina to Ranch
    4. Ranch for honey: Assign 2 Cinnamoth to Ranch
    5. Build Mill: Lv15 — grind wheat → flour
    6. Build Cooking Pot: Lv9 — combine ingredients into cake
    7. Assign a Kindling Pal (Foxparks or Arsox) to Cooking Pot

    Production rate: With 2 Chikipi + 2 Mozzarina + 2 Cinnamoth + 50 wheat plants, you'll produce ~5 cakes per hour — enough for 5-10 breeding cycles.

    Perfect Breeding Strategy

    Goal: 4-Trait Anubis (Legend + Lucky + Musclehead + Ferocious)

    Step Action
    1 Catch 10+ Relaxaurus with any combat traits
    2 Catch 10+ Celaray with any combat traits
    3 Breed Relaxaurus + Celaray until you get Anubis with 2 good traits
    4 Continue breeding Anubis offspring together until you get 3 traits
    5 Breed the 3-trait Anubis with a Pal that has the missing 4th trait
    6 Keep breeding until all 4 traits appear on one Anubis

    10/10/10 rule: For best results, breed only Pals whose IV stats (HP, ATK, DEF) are at least 10 points above base value. IVs partially inherit.

    Pro Breeding Tips

    1. Gender imbalance fix: If you keep getting only males or females, catch more of the underrepresented gender. Gender ratio is ~50/50 per species.
    2. Cake economy: Never let cake sit in the Breeding Farm once the egg is produced — it gets consumed on start, not per egg. One cake = one breeding attempt.
    3. Egg quality: Common eggs are usually not worth incubating unless the species is rare. Scrap them for Paldium fragments.
    4. Breeding for speed: Swift + Runner + Nimble + Legend on a Fenglope = the fastest ground mount in the game. Use it for exploration and boss running.
    5. Mutation farming: Breed large quantities of the same pair. Even a 5% mutation rate yields results after 20+ eggs. Use this to get Lucky on Pals that don't naturally spawn with it.
    6. Pal condenser: Once you have your perfect 4-trait Pal, feed duplicate Pals of the same species into the Pal Condenser (Lv14) to boost its partner skill level.
