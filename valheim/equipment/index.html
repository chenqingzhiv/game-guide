    Valheim Equipment & Food Progression Guide

    Last updated: June 2026 | Game version: 0.219.14+

    Knowing what to craft and when is half the battle in Valheim. Waste materials on the wrong armor or weapon, and you'll struggle against the next biome's enemies. This guide covers the optimal equipment progression — armor, weapons, food, and meads — for each stage of the game.

    🛡️ Armor Progression

    Valheim has three armor types: light (troll hide), medium (root), and heavy (bronze/iron/silver/padded/carapace). Heavy armor offers more protection but costs more stamina for movement.

    Armor Set Comparison

    Set Biome Total Armor Move Speed Set Bonus Materials Required
    Leather Meadows 8 0% None 6 Leather Scraps, 6 Deer Hide
    Troll Hide Black Forest 16 +5% (sneak) +15% Sneak 29 Troll Hide, 30 Bone Fragments
    Bronze Black Forest 32 –5% None 27 Bronze, 6 Deer Hide
    Root Swamp 20 0% Resist Pierce 20 Root, 4 Ancient Bark, 2 Leather
    Iron Scale Swamp 48 –10% None 60 Iron, 10 Deer Hide
    Silver Mountains 54 –10% Resist Frost 63 Silver, 2 Wolf Pelt, 1 Chain
    Wolf Fur Mountains 26 0% Resist Frost 24 Wolf Pelt, 2 Silver, 4 Chain
    Padded Plains 78 –10% None 105 Iron, 15 Linen Thread
    Carapace Mistlands 96 –10% Resist Pierce 108 Carapace, 20 Scale Hide
    Ashlands Ashlands 108 –10% Resist Fire Flame Metal + Ash materials

    Recommended Path

    1. Leather → Skip for Troll Hide. Leather is fine for Eikthyr but farming Troll Hide from the Black Forest gives better protection and a sneaking bonus — well worth the 29 troll kills.
    2. Troll Hide → Root Set (chest) + Iron Legs/Helm. The Root chest gives pierce resist (critical for Bonemass and deathsquitos), and iron legs/helm give better overall armor. This hybrid is meta for mid-game.
    3. Root Chest + Padded Legs/Helm. Once you reach the Plains, upgrade legs and helm to Padded but keep the Root chest for deathsquito resistance.
    4. Full Carapace or Carapace + Feather Cape. The Feather Cape (late Mistlands) eliminates fall damage — a game-changer for Mistlands exploration.

    Do not craft: Full Bronze armor (too expensive for the stats), Full Iron Scale (iron is better spent on weapons and tools).

    ⚔️ Weapon Progression

    Weapon Trees by Type

    Swords — Best overall. Balanced speed, good special attack.

    Sword Tier Damage Materials Biome
    Bronze Sword 2 35–45 8 Bronze, 2 Wood, 2 Leather Black Forest
    Iron Sword 3 50–65 20 Iron, 2 Wood, 3 Leather Swamp
    Silver Sword 4 75–100 (+ spirit) 40 Silver, 2 Wood, 5 Iron Mountains
    Blackmetal Sword 5 100–120 20 Black Metal, 5 Linen, 4 Iron Plains
    Mistwalker 6 120–140 (+ spirit) 20 Mistlands Ore, 5 Carapace Mistlands

    Maces — Best against skeletons, Bonemass, and swamp enemies.

    Mace Tier Damage Materials Biome
    Bronze Mace 2 30–40 8 Bronze, 5 Wood, 3 Leather Black Forest
    Iron Mace 3 50–65 20 Iron, 5 Wood, 3 Leather Swamp
    Frostner 4 50–75 (+ frost+spirit) 10 Silver, 5 Ancient Bark, 30 Yggdrasil Wood Mountains
    Porcupine 5 65–90 (+ pierce) 20 Black Metal, 5 Linen, 10 Needle Plains
    Demolisher 6 100–140 20 Flame Metal, 10 Carapace Mistlands

    Bows — Essential for every player. Bows are the safest way to deal damage.

    Bow Tier Pierce Draw Speed Materials
    Crude Bow 1 25 2.0s 10 Wood, 8 Leather Scraps
    Finewood Bow 2 42 1.5s 20 Finewood, 10 Core Wood, 2 Deer Hide
    Huntsman Bow 3 68 1.3s 20 Iron, 10 Finewood, 5 Feathers
    Draugr Fang 4 85 (+ poison) 1.2s 10 Silver, 10 Ancient Bark, 20 Guck

    Arrows reference:

    Arrow Damage Crafting Use Case
    Wood 22 8 Wood Early hunting
    Fire 27 + burn 8 Wood, 1 Resin Elder, early combat
    Flinthead 32 8 Wood, 2 Flint Black Forest
    Ironhead 69 8 Wood, 2 Iron Swamp / Mountains
    Obsidian 62 8 Wood, 4 Obsidian Mountains sniper
    Poison 72 + poison 8 Wood, 4 Guck, 2 Ooze Bonemass alternative
    Needle 83 8 Wood, 4 Needle Plains / Mistlands
    Carapace 115 8 Wood, 4 Carapace Ashlands endgame

    Recommended Weapon Path

    1. Crude Bow → Finewood Bow — upgrade immediately after Eikthyr. The range and damage jump is massive.
    2. Finewood Bow → Huntsman Bow — your Swamp and Mountains workhorse.
    3. Huntsman Bow → Draugr Fang — endgame bow. Poison DOT stacks and keeps ticking through boss phases.
    4. Bronze Mace → Iron Mace — for Bonemass. The iron mace is your blunt specialist.
    5. Iron Mace → Frostner — Frostner's slow debuff makes Mountains and Plains exploration much safer.
    6. Frostner → Porcupine — for Yagluth and Plains farming.
    7. Sword branch: Mistwalker for Mistlands, Blackmetal if you can't find Mistlands ore yet.

    🍲 Food Progression

    Food in Valheim determines your max HP (survival) and max Stamina (DPS + movement). Always eat three different foods. The meta is 1 HP + 1 Stamina + 1 Balanced, or 2 HP + 1 Stamina for boss fights.

    Early Game (Meadows)

    Food HP Stamina Duration Source
    Cooked Meat 55 25 25m Boar/Deer — cook on campfire
    Raspberry 15 20 20m Bushes in Meadows
    Mushroom (Yellow) 20 20 20m Meadows ground
    Boar Jerky 48 42 25m 2 Cooked Meat + 1 Honey

    Best early combo: Cooked Meat + Boar Jerky + Raspberry

    Mid Game (Black Forest — Swamp)

    Food HP Stamina Duration Source
    Deer Stew 60 40 30m 1 Cooked Deer + 1 Blueberry + 1 Carrot
    Queen's Jam 30 60 25m 8 Raspberry + 8 Blueberry
    Carrot Soup 20 60 25m 3 Carrot + 1 Mushroom
    Turnip Stew 80 60 30m 1 Turnip + 1 Cooked Meat
    Sausages 70 40 30m 2 Entrails + 1 Thistle + 1 Leather Scraps
    Cooked Fish 70 40 25m Fish caught with fishing rod

    Best swamp combo: Sausages + Turnip Stew + Queen's Jam (220 HP / 160 Stamina)

    Late Game (Mountains — Plains)

    Food HP Stamina Duration Source
    Wolf Skewer 95 70 30m 2 Wolf Meat + 1 Mushroom + 1 Thistle
    Lox Pie 100 70 30m 2 Lox Meat + 2 Barley + 2 Cloudberry
    Blood Pudding 60 120 30m 2 Bloodbag + 2 Barley + 1 Thistle
    Serpent Stew 120 80 30m 1 Serpent Meat + 1 Mushroom + 2 Honey
    Bread 50 75 30m 10 Barley

    Best mountains combo: Wolf Skewer + Lox Pie + Blood Pudding (255 HP / 260 Stamina)

    End Game (Mistlands — Ashlands)

    Food HP Stamina Duration Source
    Misthare Supreme 130 90 30m 1 Hare Meat + 1 Mushroom + 1 Jotun Puff
    Honey Glazed Chicken 120 110 30m 1 Chicken Meat + 2 Honey + 1 Royal Jelly
    Bread 50 75 30m 10 Barley
    Boar Jerky (upgraded) 60 52 25m 2 Cooked Pork + 1 Honey

    Best endgame combo: Misthare Supreme + Honey Glazed Chicken + Bread (300 HP / 275 Stamina)

    🧪 Mead & Potions Reference

    Meads are crafted at the Cauldron with a fermenter. Each mead takes 2–3 in-game days to ferment.

    Mead Recipe Effect Duration Priority
    Minor Healing 10 Honey + 5 Raspberry + 2 Blueberry + 1 Dandelion Heal 75 HP Instant High — craft 10+
    Medium Healing 10 Honey + 5 Bloodbag + 2 Rasp Heal 150 HP Instant High — craft 20+
    Major Healing 10 Honey + 5 Bloodbag + 2 Jotun Puff Heal 250 HP Instant High — Mistlands+
    Tasty 10 Honey + 10 Rasp + 5 Blueberry +300% Stamina regen 20s Medium
    Stamina 10 Honey + 10 Cloudberry + 10 Carrot Heal 150 Stamina Instant High
    Poison Resist 10 Honey + 5 Coal + 1 Thistle + 1 Neck Tail +75% Poison resist 10m Mandatory for Swamp
    Frost Resist 10 Honey + 5 Bloodbag + 2 Greydwarf Eye +75% Frost resist 10m Mandatory for Mountains
    Fire Resist 10 Honey + 5 Coal + 2 Surtling Core +75% Fire resist 10m Mandatory for Ashlands
    Barley Wine 10 Barley + 10 Cloudberry +30% Stamina 10m Low (specialized)

    Mead Usage Tips

    • Always carry 3–5 Health Meads into any biome you haven't cleared yet.
    • Poison Resist is not optional for the Swamp — Bonemass's poison does 90 dmg/tick without it.
    • Fire Resist is not optional for the Ashlands — the base terrain damages you without it.
    • Tasty mead is great for long building sessions, but risky in combat (reduced stamina regen after the buff).

    Quick Crafting Priority

    Eikthyr defeated →
  1. Antler Pickaxe (mine copper/tin)
  2. Finewood Bow
  3. Bronze Buckler (parry tool)
  4. Iron Mace (for Bonemass)
  5. Huntsman Bow or Draugr Fang

Bonemass defeated →
  1. Iron Pickaxe (mine silver)
  2. Frostner (mace)
  3. Silver Sword or Draugr Fang
  4. Wolf Fur Chest (frost resist)

Moder defeated →
  1. Artisan Table
  2. Spinning Wheel (linen)
  3. Padded Armor
  4. Blackmetal Sword / Porcupine

Yagluth defeated →
  1. Carapace Armor
  2. Mistwalker (sword)
  3. Feather Cape
  4. Demolisher (optional)

    Data verified against the Valheim Wiki and in-game testing (v0.219.14). Food values reflect base stats without the Comfort bonus.
