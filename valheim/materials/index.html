    📦 Valheim Materials & Forging Guide

    Last updated: July 2026 | Game version: 0.219.14+

    Knowing what materials are available, when you need them, and how to process them efficiently is the backbone of Valheim progression. This guide covers every material in the game — from flint to flametal — organized by biome, with forging station upgrades, material efficiency formulas, and pre-boss checklists.

    📊 Material Overview by Biome

    🌲 Meadows

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Wood Trees, stumps Everything — buildings, tools, fires ⭐⭐⭐
    Stone Ground pickups Walls, foundations, early tools ⭐⭐⭐
    Flint River banks Stone axe, flint knife, arrows ⭐⭐⭐
    Leather Scraps Boar Workbench upgrades, early armor ⭐⭐⭐
    Deer Hide Deer Leather armor, tanning rack ⭐⭐
    Resin Greydwarfs, trees Torches, fires, arrows ⭐⭐
    Bone Fragments Skeleton kills Bone tower shield, meads ⭐⭐
    Honey Beehives Health regen, mead base ⭐⭐

    🌲 Black Forest

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Tin Tin ore deposits Bronze (with Copper) ⭐⭐⭐
    Copper Copper ore deposits Bronze, Forge, early gear ⭐⭐⭐
    Fine Wood Birch/Oak trees Karve, finewood bow, portals ⭐⭐⭐
    Core Wood Pine trees Log beams, stakewalls ⭐⭐
    Troll Hide Troll kills Troll armor (best early stealth) ⭐⭐⭐
    Surtling Cores Burial Chambers Smelter, Charcoal Kiln ⭐⭐⭐
    Ancient Seeds Graydwarf shaman/brute Summon The Elder ⭐⭐
    Rubies/Amber Chests, burial chambers Sell to Haldor ⭐

    🟢 Swamp

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Iron Scraps Muddy scrap piles in crypts Iron nails, weapons, armor, stonecutter ⭐⭐⭐
    Ancient Bark Ancient Trees Longship, Fermenter, Upgrade stations ⭐⭐⭐
    Chain Wraiths, chests Iron tower shield, some upgrades ⭐⭐⭐
    Withered Bones Bonemass summon area Summon Bonemass ⭐⭐⭐
    Guck Guck spawns (green drips) Draugr fang bow, bile bombs ⭐
    Ooze Blob/Oozer kills Ooze bombs, poison resistance ⭐

    ⛰️ Mountains

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Silver Silver veins (wishbone required) Frostner, Draugr fang, silver armor ⭐⭐⭐
    Obsidian Black rock nodes Obsidian arrows, portal upgrade ⭐⭐⭐
    Wolf Pelt Wolf kills Wolf armor, Wolf cape (frost resist) ⭐⭐⭐
    Freeze Glands Drake kills Frost arrows, mead base ⭐⭐
    Dragon Egg Mountain top nests Summon Moder ⭐⭐⭐
    Crystal Stone Golem kills Crystal weapon, light sources ⭐⭐

    🌾 Plains

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Black Metal Fuling kills Black metal weapons, shield ⭐⭐⭐
    Flax Fuling camps, cultivated Linen thread → padded armor ⭐⭐⭐
    Barley Fuling farms, cultivated Barley flour, food recipes ⭐⭐⭐
    Linen Thread Spinning Wheel (Flax) Padded armor (best heavy armor) ⭐⭐⭐
    Needle Deathsquito kills Needle arrows (highest pierce) ⭐⭐
    Cloudberries Plains forage Medium/high tier food ⭐⭐
    Tar Tar Pits Dark wood building, upgrades ⭐⭐

    🌫️ Mistlands

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Black Marble Dvergr structures Carapace armor, building ⭐⭐⭐
    Soft Tissue Seekers, Gjalls Carapace armor, weapons ⭐⭐⭐
    Sap Sap trees Extractors → refined sap ⭐⭐⭐
    Royal Jelly Seeker brood nests Food, healing meads ⭐⭐⭐
    Mandible Seekers Carapace spear, building ⭐⭐
    Proustite Dust Soft tissue processing Carapace upgrades ⭐⭐
    Eitr Refined sap Magic weapons, staffs ⭐⭐⭐

    🔥 Ashlands

    Material Source Used For Priority
    Flint Metal Flint metal deposits Ashlands weapons, armor, ship ⭐⭐⭐
    Proustite Crystals in lava zones Upgrades, Ashlands gear ⭐⭐⭐
    Ashwood Charred trees Ashlands building, ship ⭐⭐⭐
    Charred Bones Charred enemy kills Bonemass power food, meads ⭐⭐
    Lava Stone Volcanic deposits Fortress building ⭐⭐
    Searing Core Ashlands dungeon Smelter upgrades ⭐⭐⭐

    🔨 Metal Smelting Guide

    Smelting Ratios

    Ore Smelter Fuel per Ingot Time Yield
    Copper Ore Smelter 2 Coal 60s 1 Copper
    Tin Ore Smelter 2 Coal 40s 1 Tin
    Iron Scraps Smelter 3 Coal 45s 1 Iron
    Silver Ore Blast Furnace 3 Coal 50s 1 Silver
    Black Metal Scraps Blast Furnace 2 Coal 30s 1 Black Metal
    Flint Metal Ore Blast Furnace 4 Coal 60s 1 Flint Metal

    Alloy Crafting

    Alloy Component 1 Component 2 Ratio Made In
    Bronze Copper Tin 2:1 Forge
    Black Metal Black Scrap — 1:1 Blast Furnace

    Bronze efficiency tip: The 2:1 ratio means every 6 Copper + 3 Tin = 3 Bronze. Always mine Tin and Copper in a 2:1 ratio to avoid excess.

    Fuel Efficiency

    Fuel Type Burn Time Best For
    Wood 15s each Early game, quick smelting
    Coal 30s each All stages — most efficient
    Core Wood 25s each Decent alternative to coal

    Pro tip: 1 Wood → 1 Coal via Charcoal Kiln. Wood is renewable (plant trees), so turn all spare wood into coal.

    🛠️ Crafting Station Upgrades

    Workbench

    Upgrade Materials Effect
    Chopping Block 10 Wood, 10 Flint Lvl 2: Stone axe, flint tools
    Tanning Rack 10 Wood, 15 Flint, 20 Leather Scraps, 5 Deer Hide Lvl 3: Finewood, deer hide gear
    Adze 10 Fine Wood, 3 Bronze Lvl 4: Iron-age tools
    Tool Shelf 10 Fine Wood, 4 Iron Lvl 5: Black metal tools
    Anvils 10 Black Metal, 5 Linen Thread Lvl 6: Mistlands-tier tools

    Forge

    Upgrade Materials Lvl Effect
    Forge Bellows 5 Deer Hide, 4 Surtling Cores, 10 Wood 2 Copper/bronze gear
    Anvil 5 Bronze, 2 Wood 3 Iron gear
    Forge Toolrack 10 Iron, 4 Wood 4 Silver gear
    Grinding Wheel 25 Wood, 1 Sharpening Stone 5 Black metal gear
    Forge Cooler 10 Fine Wood, 25 Copper, 10 Iron 6 Mistlands gear
    Smith's Anvil 20 Black Marble, 5 Soft Tissue 7 Carapace gear

    Cauldron

    Upgrade Materials Effect
    Spice Rack 10 Dandelion, 5 Mushroom, 5 Carrot, 5 Thistle, 5 Turnip Mid-tier recipes
    Butcher Table 5 Fine Wood, 1 Bronze, 1 Iron Animal drops → better food
    Kitchen Station 10 Iron, 10 Fine Wood Advanced cooking
    Oven 20 Stone, 5 Iron, 10 Fine Wood Baking breads, pies
    Mead Kettle 10 Tin, 5 Copper Mead base crafting

    💎 Rare & Limited Materials

    Material Limited? Best Farm Method
    Dragon Eggs Yes — limited per mountain Explore multiple mountains
    Surtling Cores Semi — respawning fire geysers Portal to swamp fire geysers
    Chain No — wraiths respawn Night swamp farming
    Dvergr parts No — new Infested Mines generate Explore new Mistlands areas
    Proustite Yes per node — new Ashlands zones Explore deeper Ashlands

    📋 Pre-Boss Material Checklist

    Before Eikthyr

    • [ ] 10 Wood, 5 Stone (basic shelter)
    • [ ] Flint axe, hammer, crafting table
    • [ ] Leather tunic (optional)
    • [ ] Bow + 50 arrows

    Before The Elder

    • [ ] 12 Surtling Cores (smelter + kiln)
    • [ ] 6 Tin + 3 Copper → 3 Bronze (weapons)
    • [ ] Finewood bow
    • [ ] Bronze axe (for finewood)

    Before Bonemass

    • [ ] At least 60 Iron (mace + shield + armor)
    • [ ] 30 Ancient Bark (Longship + Fermenter)
    • [ ] Poison Resistance Mead (mandatory)
    • [ ] Stagbreaker (for Bonemass adds)

    Before Moder

    • [ ] 50+ Silver (Frostner + armor)
    • [ ] Wishbone (from Bonemass)
    • [ ] Obsidian arrows (100+)
    • [ ] Health & Stamina meads
    • [ ] Lox cape (cold resist + insulation)

    Before Yagluth

    • [ ] Padded armor set (full set)
    • [ ] Black Metal weapons (sword or mace)
    • [ ] Fire Resistance Barley Wine (mandatory)
    • [ ] Lox pie, fish wraps (high-tier food)

    Before The Queen

    • [ ] Carapace armor set
    • [ ] Krom, Mistwalker, or Demolisher
    • [ ] Wisps (Wisp light required)
    • [ ] Refined Eitr (if using magic)
    • [ ] Major healing + stamina meads

    Before Fader (Ashlands Boss)

    • [ ] Flint Metal gear (weapon + shield)
    • [ ] Ashlands ship
    • [ ] Asksvin saddle (mount)
    • [ ] Lava-resistant food
    • [ ] Best meads (major healing + stamina)

    🔄 Cross-Biome Material Planning

    What to stockpile Why When to start
    Wood + Core Wood Portals, building stations From day 1
    Coal Smelting everything Always have a kiln running
    Iron Used in every tier after Swamp Farm extra during Swamp phase
    Flax Padded armor requires lots Plant from first Plains visit
    Barley Best food base Plant from first Plains visit
    Sap Eitr production Stock up in Mistlands
    Proustite Best late-game upgrades Farm in Ashlands
