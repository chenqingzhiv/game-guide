    ⚔️ Minecraft Combat Guide

    Data version: Minecraft 1.21 | Last updated: 2026-07

    From your first zombie encounter to the Warden in the Deep Dark — this guide covers every combat mechanic, weapon comparison, mob strategy, and raid system in Minecraft.

    🗡️ Weapon Comparison

    Melee Weapons

    Weapon Attack Damage Attack Speed DPS Special
    Netherite Sword 8 (❤️×4) 1.6 12.8 Lava-proof, knockback
    Diamond Sword 7 (❤️×3.5) 1.6 11.2 Best pre-Netherite
    Iron Sword 6 (❤️×3) 1.6 9.6 Reliable mid-game
    Stone Sword 5 (❤️×2.5) 1.6 8.0 Early game standard
    Wooden Sword 4 (❤️×2) 1.6 6.4 Better than fists
    Netherite Axe 10 (❤️×5) 1.0 10.0 Slow but hits hard
    Mace 6 + fall dmg bonus 1.6 Scales 🔥 1.21 new — wind charge synergy
    Trident (melee) 9 (❤️×4.5) 1.1 9.9 Riptide / Loyalty builds

    Ranged Weapons

    Weapon Base Damage Range Draw Time Special
    Bow (fully charged) 6–10 (❤️×3–5) 50+ blocks 1.5s Enchantable (Power, Flame)
    Crossbow 6–11 (❤️×3–5.5) 50+ blocks 1.5s pierce Piercing, multishot
    Trident (thrown) 8 (❤️×4) ~30 blocks Instant Channeling (storm lightning)

    💡 Mace Deal: The 1.21 mace does bonus damage per block fallen before the hit. Paired with a wind charge (knocks you upward), you can deal 20+ damage on a single strike.

    Best Armor Enchantments

    Piece Best Enchant Secondary
    Helmet Protection IV or Aqua Affinity Respiration III, Unbreaking III
    Chestplate Protection IV Unbreaking III, Thorns III

    ⚠️ Protection cap: Each Protection level adds 4% damage reduction (max 80% across all armor). Use Feather Falling on boots separately — it stacks with Protection!

    👾 Mob Encyclopedia & Combat Strategies

    Overworld Hostile Mobs

    Mob HP Damage Strategy
    Zombie 20 3–5 (❤️×1.5–2.5) Simple block-and-hit. Kills villagers → zombies.
    Skeleton 20 3–5 (❤️×1.5–2.5) Zigzag approach. Shield blocks arrows.
    Creeper 20 49–65 (❤️×24–32) Back away! Block with shield reduces blast to 1-3 damage.
    Spider 16 2–4 (❤️×1–2) 2-block gap stops them. Can climb — avoid building under.
    Cave Spider 12 2–3 + poison Poison deals 7 ❤️ over 7 seconds. Drink milk!
    Enderman 40 7 (❤️×3.5) Don't look at its face. 3-block ceiling to trap.
    Witch 26 Splash damage Throws harming + poison + slowness. Ranged kill.
    Creeper (charged) 20 97–145 (❤️×48–72) Lightning strike makes it devastating. Snipe from range.

    Nether Mobs

    Mob HP Damage Strategy
    Zombified Piglin 20 5–8 (❤️×2.5–4) Neutral — don't hit one or the whole horde aggros.
    Blaze 20 6 (❤️×3) Shoot with bow. Snowballs deal 3 damage each!
    Magma Cube 16 (big) 6 (❤️×3) Split on death. Splash potion of healing damages them.
    Ghast 10 6–8 + fire Deflect fireballs with melee hit. Bow from cover.
    Wither Skeleton 20 8 (❤️×4) + wither Stone sword (resists fire). Milk for wither effect.
    Piglin Brute 50 8–13 (❤️×4–6.5) Boss-level. Avoid if possible. Gold armor stops Piglins.
    Hoglins 40 6–10 (❤️×3–5) Knocks you back hard. Fences contain them.

    Boss Mobs

    Boss HP Phases Strategy
    Ender Dragon ❤️ 200 1 (end crystals) Break crystals first. Bed explosion trick. Slow falling potion.
    Wither ⚫ 300 (Bedrock: 450) 2 (half HP = wither effect) Underground tunnel fight. Smite V sword.
    Warden 🃏 500 None (sensor-based) The scariest mob. Avoid at all costs. Sneak + wool silence.

    🌊 Raid System (Village Raid)

    How to Trigger a Raid

    1. Find a village
    2. Kill a raid captain (Illager with Ominous Banner)
    3. Enter the village — Bad Omen effect triggers the raid

    Raid Waves

    Wave Mobs Total
    1 2 pillagers 2
    2 2 vindicators 2
    3 1 ravager + 2 pillagers 3
    4 2 witches + 1 pillager 3
    5 1 ravager + 1 vindicator + 1 witch 3
    6 2 evokers + 2 vindicators + 1 witch 5
    7 Ominous — evoker + ravager + 3 vindicators 5

    Raid Rewards

    Item Source Use
    Hero of the Village effect Survive raid! Discounts from villagers (up to 55%)
    Totem of Undying Evoker drop Saves you from death once
    Emeralds + XP Vindicator + pillager drops Trading + enchanting
    Ravager drops Kill the ravager Saddle + XP

    Raid Farming Setup

    1. Kill tower: Build a 3-block-tall kill chamber with water stream
    2. Villager bunker: Protect 1 villager underground (must be alive!)
    3. Trident killer: A trident-killer machine (see Redstone Guide)
    4. Looting sword: Pick off evokers manually for totems

    ⚙️ Advanced Combat Mechanics

    Critical Hits

    • Deal 150% damage (rounded up)
    • Triggered when you're falling (not sprinting, not on ground)
    • Visual indicator: stars appear around the hit
    • Best combo: Sprint → jump → hit during fall descent

    Shield Mechanics

    Feature Details
    Blocks damage Absorbs up to 100% of melee damage
    Block arrows Deflected projectiles
    Block explosions 100% at full block, partial otherwise
    Axe break Axe attack disables shield for 5s
    Cooldown 0.25s after raising

    Status Effects for Combat

    Effect Source Combat Use
    Strength II Beacon / potion +6 melee damage (massive DPS boost)
    Speed II Beacon / potion Outrun mobs, dodge arrows
    Slow Falling Potion Safe from fall damage, easier mace combos
    Invisibility Potion + armor Sneak past mobs (armor must be off!)
    Regeneration Golden apple 4 HP over 5 seconds
    Absorption Golden apple / beacon +4 temporary hearts

    🎯 Quick Reference: Mob Kill Order (Priority)

    1. Creeper — highest threat. Kill or back away.
    2. Witch — weakens you over time. Bow snipe.
    3. Skeleton — ranged damage. Use shield to approach.
    4. Zombie — slow, predictable. Easy sword practice.
    5. Spider — jumpy but low HP. One or two hits.
    6. Enderman — only aggro if you look. Eye contact = fight.
