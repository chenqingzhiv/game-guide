    ✨ Minecraft Enchanting Guide

    Data version: Minecraft 1.21 | Last updated: 2026-07

    Enchanting turns a good weapon into a legendary one. This guide covers everything from setting up a level 30 enchanting table to building XP farms, trading with librarians, and min-maxing your enchantments.

    🔮 Enchanting Table Setup

    Minimum Level 30 Setup

    To reach enchantment level 30 (the maximum tier), you need 15 bookshelves placed in a specific pattern around the enchanting table.

    Bookshelf Layout (Top-Down View):

     B B B
   B X X X B
   B X E X B
   B X X X B
     B B B

E = Enchanting Table
B = Bookshelf (on any block)
X = Air gap (1 block between shelf and table)

    Bookshelf Placement Rules

    Rule Detail
    Distance Exactly 1 block away (air gap)
    Height Bookshelves can be at same level or 1 block above table
    Blocking Torches, slabs, carpets between table and bookshelf? Blocks connection
    Table level Table must be on level. Bookshelf can be 1 above or same level.

    Cost to Build

    Material Quantity How to Get
    Bookshelves 15 3 books + 6 planks each = 45 books + 90 planks
    Books 45 3 paper + 1 leather each = 135 paper + 45 leather
    Sugar cane ~68 stalks Grows on sand/water edge — farm 3×20 patch
    Leather 45 Kill cows (3 per = 15 cows) or fish

    💡 Faster alternative: Break bookshelves in villages and take them! Most libraries have 5–8 shelves.

    📚 Best Enchantments Per Item

    Swords (Ranked)

    Enchantment Max Level Average DPS Boost Notes
    Sharpness V +20% All-purpose damage
    Smite V +38% (on undead) Better than Sharpness in Nether (wither skeletons, zombies)
    Bane of Arthropods V +38% (on spiders) Niche — only good in spider spawners
    Looting III 2× drops Farming gear, not combat gear
    Fire Aspect II 8 fire damage over time Cooks meat on kill
    Sweeping Edge III +50% sweep damage Clears crowds

    Best all-rounder: Sharpness V + Looting III + Unbreaking III + Sweeping Edge III

    Pickaxes

    Enchantment Max Level Effect
    Efficiency V +2600% mining speed (insta-mine stone)
    Fortune III +4× drops on coal, diamond, emerald, copper, nether gold
    Silk Touch I Mined blocks drop themselves (ores, glass, bookshelves)
    Unbreaking III 3× durability

    Fortune vs. Silk Touch: Fortune gives more materials; Silk Touch preserves rare blocks. Have one of each.

    Axes

    Enchantment Max Level Effect
    Efficiency V Insta-mine wood
    Sharpness V +3 melee damage (good weapon option)
    Silk Touch I Collect leaves, chiseled bookshelves
    Fortune III More apples, sticks from leaves
    Unbreaking III 3× durability

    Bows

    Enchantment Max Level Effect
    Power V +150% damage (one-shot skeletons)
    Flame I Sets mobs on fire
    Punch II Knocks mobs back
    Infinity I 1 arrow = infinite
    Unbreaking III 3× durability

    ⚠️ Infinity cannot be combined with Mending. Choose one!

    Armor

    Piece Best Enchantments
    Helmet Protection IV + Unbreaking III + Aqua Affinity + Respiration III
    Chestplate Protection IV + Unbreaking III + Thorns III (optional)
    Leggings Protection IV + Unbreaking III
    Boots Protection IV + Feather Falling IV + Depth Strider III + Unbreaking III

    🧪 XP Farm Methods (Ranked)

    Farm Type XP/hour Build Time Difficulty Notes
    Enderman XP Farm ~400 levels/hr 2 hours Medium End island, 40-block drop, sweeping edge sword
    Zombie Spawner ~100 levels/hr 30 min Easy Find spawner in cave, water elevator, 20-block drop
    Skelly Spawner ~80 levels/hr 30 min Easy Same as zombie farm
    Gold Farm (portal) ~300 levels/hr 3 hours Hard Nether gold farm, zombie piglin
    Guardian Farm ~500 levels/hr 4 hours Very Hard Ocean monument drain — insane XP
    Bamboo/Smelt XP ~30 levels/hr 15 min Trivial Passive low-volume
    Breeding + trading ~50 levels/hr Passive Easy Villager trading + breeding

    🏆 Best Early-Game XP Farm: Zombie Spawner

    1. Find a zombie spawner in a cave (dark mossy cobblestone room)
    2. Dig a 2×1 tunnel from spawner area
    3. Drop zombies 20 blocks (10 hearts damage — they survive at 1 HP)
    4. Hit them once with a Looting III sword for XP + optional rotten flesh
    5. Light up surrounding caves to prevent mob interference

    🏆 Best Endgame XP Farm: Enderman Farm

    1. Go to The End main island
    2. Build a platform at Y=250 (above endermen spawn area)
    3. Create a chunk-aligned 30×30 platform at Y=0
    4. Water streams funnel endermen into a 40+ block drop
    5. Hit at feet level with sweeping edge sword
    6. Collect pearls + XP

    📖 Librarian Trading: Enchanted Books

    Trading with villagers is often faster than enchanting tables for specific enchantments.

    Tier 1 Librarian Trades

    Trade Cost Items
    Name tag 20 emeralds 1 book
    Enchanted book 5–64 emeralds 1 book
    Bookshelf 3–5 emeralds 1 bookshelf
    Glass 1 emerald 4 glass

    Most Valuable Librarian Books

    Enchantment Max Trade Cost Min Trade Cost Notes
    Mending 38 emeralds 10 emeralds Must-have. Look for cheap before locking
    Silk Touch 32 emeralds 8 emeralds Worth a zombie villager cure
    Efficiency V 64 emeralds 20 emeralds Endgame tool enchant
    Protection IV 48 emeralds 12 emeralds Max armor enchant
    Unbreaking III 36 emeralds 10 emeralds Essential on everything
    Sharpness V 64 emeralds 15 emeralds Sword DPS
    Power V 64 emeralds 20 emeralds Bow damage

    💡 Pro Tip: Break and replace a librarian's workstation to re-roll their book trade until you get a cheap Mending or Silk Touch!

    🔨 Anvil Mechanics

    Anvil Use Costs

    Operation Base Cost Minimum XP
    Rename item 1 level 1
    Apply enchanted book 1–9 levels 1
    Combine items 2–20 levels 2
    Repair (material) 1–4 levels 1

    Anvil Rules

    Rule Detail
    Max anvil uses 6 (item becomes "Too Expensive!" after)
    Conflict table Can't combine Protection + Fire Protection on same item
    Priority Combine lower-level books first, then apply to tool
    Repair cost Prior work penalty doubles each time you use the anvil
    Better: Use Mending + XP instead of anvil repairs for tools you use often

    Combining Enchantments Efficiently

    BAD: Book (Sharpness I) → Apply to sword → Book (Sharpness II) → Apply
     Cost: 1 + 3 + 1 + 5 = 10 levels, 4 anvil uses

GOOD: Book (Sharpness I) + Book (Sharpness I) = Book (Sharpness II)
      Book (Sharpness II) + Book (Sharpness II) = Book (Sharpness IV)
      Apply Book (Sharpness IV) to sword
      Cost: 2 + 4 + 6 = 12 levels, 3 anvil uses

    ⚗️ Grindstone: Removing Enchantments

    Use a Grindstone to remove all enchantments from an item. You get some XP back. Useful when:

    • You accidentally put a bad enchantment on a good tool
    • Enchanted books you found have conflicting enchantments
    • You want to salvage an item before throwing it away

    📊 Enchanting Table vs. Anvil vs. Librarian

    Method Cost Control Best For
    Enchanting Table Lapis + XP Random 1–3 enchants Starting out, exploring options
    Anvil + Books XP levels Full control Min-maxing, specific builds
    Librarian Trading Emeralds Pick the book Mending, Silk Touch, Efficiency V

    🎯 Quick Checklist: Enchanting Setup

    • [ ] Enchanting table with 15 bookshelves in correct pattern
    • [ ] Lapis lazuli (stack of 32+)
    • [ ] XP farm (at least a spawner-based one)
    • [ ] Villager trading hall for max-level books
    • [ ] Anvil + grindstone nearby
    • [ ] Obsidian for bookshelf pattern (optional visual floor)
    • [ ] Nice room — enchanting is style too!
