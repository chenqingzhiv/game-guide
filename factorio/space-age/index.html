    🚀 Factorio Space Age Expansion Guide

    Version: 2.0 Space Age | Last updated: July 2026

    The Space Age expansion transforms Factorio from a single-planet factory game into a multi-planet logistics empire. Four new worlds — each with unique resources, mechanics, and challenges — must be colonized, automated, and connected by interplanetary transport.

    This guide covers everything you need: what to bring, what to build, and what traps to avoid on every planet.

    📋 Overview — The Four Planets

    Planet Theme Science Difficulty Key Mechanic
    🌋 Vulcanus Lava & foundries Metallurgic Science Medium Lava casting, big drills, artillery
    ⚡ Fulgora Lightning & ruins Electromagnetic Science Medium Scrap recycling, lightning harvesting
    🧫 Gleba Fungal swamps Agricultural Science Hard Bio-processing, spoilage, pentapods
    ❄️ Aquilo Frozen ocean Cryogenic Science Very Hard Freezing mechanics, heating, ice platforms
    🛰️ Orbit Space platform Space Science Variable Zero-gravity platform construction

    🌋 Vulcanus — The Forge World

    Vulcanus is a volcanic planet with seas of molten lava, calcite deposits, and aggressive demolishers — giant worm-like enemies that guard resource-rich territories.

    Unlock Requirements

    Requirement Detail
    Tech researched Rocket Silo + Rocket Launch
    Minimum cargo 1 rocket of construction supplies (bots, solar panels, accumulators, assemblers)
    Recommended Personal laser defense, nuclear reactors, cliff explosives

    Core Resources & New Materials

    Resource Found On Used For
    Calcite Lava shoreline deposits Steel smelting (direct iron → steel), sulfuric acid production
    Lava Pumpable from lava seas Molten iron/copper casting (infinite, no mining needed)
    Tungsten Ore Mineable deposits Tungsten carbide, tungsten plates
    Carbon Fiber Assembled Spidertron equipment, rocket parts

    New materials unlocked:

    Material Recipe Purpose
    Tungsten Plate Tungsten ore → smelting Stronger belts, mech armor
    Tungsten Carbide Tungsten + Carbon Mining drill upgrades, foundry
    Metallurgic Science Pack Tungsten plate + Carbon fiber + Steel Core space science progression

    Recommended Landing Prep

    Essential items for first landing:

☐ 50+ Construction robots + 10 roboports
☐ 100+ Solar panels + accumulators
☐ Assemblers (AM2, AM3), chemical plants
☐ 4+ Rocket silos on Nauvis (for resupply)
☐ Personal defense: Shield MK2 + laser defense
☐ Cliff explosives (Vulcanus terrain is rugged)
☐ Nuclear reactor + heat exchangers (or solar)
☐ Belts, undergrounds, splitters (red/blue)

    Unique Buildings

    🏭 Foundry

    The Foundry replaces furnaces on Vulcanus. It accepts molten metals directly from lava and produces plates, gears, pipes, and beams in a single building.

    Feature Detail
    Input Molten iron/copper (from lava) or standard ore
    Output Plates, gears, pipes, steel, copper wire
    Bonuses +50% productivity over standard furnaces
    Module slots 4 (vs 2 on electric furnace)

    💡 Key trick: The Foundry can cast molten iron directly into steel with calcite — no need for the steel furnace step. This is the primary reason to visit Vulcanus.

    ⛏️ Big Mining Drill

    A massive 5×5 mining drill that covers a 5×5 chunk area (80×80 tiles). Produces ore from the entire area with no resource drain — it effectively mines all nodes in its coverage zone simultaneously.

    Feature Detail
    Size 5×5 tiles
    Coverage 5×5 chunk area (configurable)
    Mining speed 2.5× standard drill
    Resource drain None — mines all patches in area

    💡 Key trick: Place Big Drills overlapping multiple ore patches for massive throughput. On Nauvis, a single Big Drill can cover 3-5 iron patches.

    Tech Tree Highlights

    Tech Unlocks Priority
    Metallurgic Science Foundry, lava pumping ⭐⭐⭐ First priority
    Tungsten Alloy Big mining drills ⭐⭐⭐ Essential
    Foundry Advanced casting recipes ⭐⭐⭐ Game-changer
    Artillery Range Artillery shell, remote targeting ⭐⭐ For expansion
    Artillery Shell Range Longer-range artillery ⭐
    Planet Discovery: Vulcanus Actually getting there ⭐⭐⭐

    Strategy & Tips

    1. Kill your first demolisher with poison capsules + personal lasers. They don't regenerate quickly.
    2. Calcite is finite on shoreline deposits but infinite from space platform (crush asteroids). Ship calcite down from orbit for Nauvis steel.
    3. Set up lava pumping immediately — it's infinite ore that never depletes.
    4. Foundry + calcite produces steel at 2× speed with 50% productivity. Ship this recipe back to Nauvis.
    5. Artillery is unlocked on Vulcanus. Use it to clear demolishers from range and claim territory.

    Common Traps

    Trap Why It's Bad Fix
    Underestimating demolishers They 1-shot you early on Bring poison capsules + shields
    Not shipping calcite back Missing infinite steel recipe Set up interplanetary calcite delivery
    Building on lava shores Cliff terrain blocks expansion Bring cliff explosives
    Ignoring artillery Can't clear big demolishers Rush artillery after landing

    ⚡ Fulgora — The Lightning World

    Fulgora is a ruined world covered in scrap metal deposits and constant lightning storms. There is no stone, no coal, and no uranium — but the scrap contains everything you need.

    Unlock Requirements

    Requirement Detail
    Tech researched Rocket Silo + Rocket Launch
    Minimum cargo 1 rocket of solar panels (critical — no coal for power), accumulators, construction bots
    Recommended Lightning rod blueprints, recycler setup, accumulator array

    Core Resources & New Materials

    Fulgora has no mining drills. Instead, you collect scrap from the ruins and recycle it:

    Scrap → Recycling Output % Chance Used For
    Iron ore ~25% Smelting
    Copper ore ~20% Smelting
    Stone ~5% Brick, concrete
    Concrete ~20% Construction
    Batteries ~10% Accumulators, science
    Holmium Ore ~8% Electromagnetic science
    Ice ~5% Water (via heating)
    Steel plate ~4% Various
    Solid fuel ~2% Backup power

    New materials unlocked:

    Material Recipe Purpose
    Holmium Plate Holmium ore → processing → plate Superconductors, EM science
    Superconductor Holmium + copper wire Electromagnetic science pack, advanced modules
    Electromagnetic Science Pack Superconductor + battery + processing unit Core space science

    Recommended Landing Prep

    Essential items for first landing:

☐ 100+ Solar panels (CRITICAL — no coal for steam)
☐ 200+ Accumulators (for lightning-buffered power)
☐ 50+ Construction robots + roboports
☐ Lightning rods (research before departure)
☐ Accumulator blueprints (pre-built)
☐ Recyclers (must be researched on Fulgora)
☐ Belts for scrap sorting (lots of splitters)

    Unique Buildings & Mechanics

    ⚡ Lightning Collection

    Fulgora has constant lightning that damages buildings. But lightning = free power if harvested.

    Building Purpose Range
    Lightning Rod Attracts lightning, protects 9×9 area 9×9 tiles
    Lightning Collector Harvests lightning into accumulators Stores up to 500 MJ

    Design rule: Place a Lightning Collector every 3×3 grid of buildings. Chain collectors to accumulator banks.

    🏚️ Recycler

    Scrap must be processed through the Recycler — a 3×3 building that outputs random components at 25% of original value (infinite resource efficiency).

    Feature Detail
    Input Scrap (from ruins)
    Output Random assortment of base resources
    Module slots 4 (productivity modules increase scrap output)
    Power 1.8 MW

    🔋 Charging Tower

    Wireless power transmission for vehicles. Any vehicle within range (40 tiles) recharges its internal battery. Essential for Fulgora's sprawling scrap fields.

    Tech Tree Highlights

    Tech Unlocks Priority
    Electromagnetic Science Recycling, superconductors ⭐⭐⭐ First priority
    Lightning Collection Lightning rod, collector ⭐⭐⭐ Safety first
    Recycler Scrap processing ⭐⭐⭐ Essential
    Accumulator MK2 Better energy storage ⭐⭐
    Charging Tower Vehicle charging ⭐

    Strategy & Tips

    1. Scrap sorting is the core challenge. You get random outputs — build a sorting system that filters each resource type to dedicated storage/belts.
    2. Use circuit-controlled splitters to manage overflow. Overabundant items (stone, concrete, iron) should be recycled again or voided.
    3. Holmium is the bottleneck. It drops at only ~8% per scrap. Mine lots of scrap fields.
    4. Build lightning collector farms early — they provide nearly infinite free power once storms hit.
    5. Recycle excess batteries into more scrap processing. Batteries are common and not needed in huge quantities.

    Common Traps

    Trap Why It's Bad Fix
    Not building lightning rods first Everything gets destroyed Rod everything before placing machines
    Sorting scrap manually Impossible to manage Build a circuit-controlled sorting bus
    Running out of holmium Stalls EM science production Expand scrap mining aggressively
    No backup power Dust storms reduce solar Build accumulator banks charged by lightning
    Forgetting charging towers Vehicles stranded in scrap fields Place towers at 40-tile intervals

    🧫 Gleba — The Fungal World

    Gleba is a hostile swamp planet where everything spoils. Production must be continuous — you cannot buffer items. Aggressive pentapod enemies replace biters.

    Unlock Requirements

    Requirement Detail
    Tech researched Rocket Silo + Rocket Launch
    Minimum cargo 2+ rockets (Gleba has nothing initially — bring everything)
    Recommended Tesla turrets, rocket turrets, flamethrowers, heating towers, biochamber blueprints

    Core Resources & New Materials

    Gleba has no traditional ore. Instead, you farm bio-flora:

    Resource Source Used For
    Yumako Yumako tree (fruit) Mash → Nutrients, bioflux
    Jellynut Jellynut tree (fruit) Jelly → Nutrients, bioflux
    Bioflux Mash + Jelly → Biochamber Agricultural science, alien research
    Nutrients Mash or Jelly Biochamber fuel
    Spores Decaying flora Pentapod attraction

    The Spoilage System:

    Every Gleba product has a spoilage timer. When it expires, the item turns into spoilage — a waste product that can be composted into nutrients or disposed of.

    Item Spoilage Time Turns Into
    Yumako Fruit 10 minutes Spoilage
    Jellynut 10 minutes Spoilage
    Mash 5 minutes Spoilage
    Jelly 5 minutes Spoilage
    Bioflux 30 minutes Spoilage
    Nutrients 5 minutes Spoilage
    Agricultural Science Pack 60 minutes Spoilage

    New materials unlocked:

    Material Recipe Purpose
    Bioflux Mash + Jelly (Biochamber) Science, biter genetics research
    Nutrients Mash or Jelly Biochamber fueling
    Agricultural Science Pack Bioflux + Nutrients + Egg (Biochamber) Core space science

    Recommended Landing Prep

    Essential items for first landing:

☐ ALL construction materials (no ore on Gleba!)
☐ Solar panels + accumulators + nuclear reactor
☐ Tesla turrets + rocket turrets + ammo (pentapods)
☐ Assemblers, chemical plants (biochamber is fragile)
☐ Heat pipes + heat exchangers (for energy from burning)
☐ Heating towers (burn spoilage for power)
☐ Circuit network components (for spoilage timing)
☐ Belt + inserter blueprints for loop-based production
☐ Personal: Power armor MK2, shields, exoskeleton

    Unique Buildings

    🧪 Biochamber

    A 4×4 building that replaces the assembling machine for all biological recipes. It consumes nutrients as fuel to operate.

    Feature Detail
    Fuel Nutrients (5 sec per operation)
    Productivity +50% base over assemblers
    Module slots 3
    Speed 1.25 (base)
    Crafting categories Bio-processing, basic crafting

    💡 Key trick: Biochambers need constant nutrients. A single Biochamber making nutrients can fuel ~10 other Biochambers. Plan nutrient production as a dedicated sub-factory.

    🔥 Heating Tower

    Burns spoilage, wood, and biofuel for heat — connect to heat exchangers for power. Essential for Gleba's power grid.

    Feature Detail
    Heat output 4 MW
    Fuel Spoilage, wood, solid fuel
    Max temperature 1000°C
    Bonus 250% efficiency when burning spoilage

    💡 Key trick: Your base will generate spoilage. Burn it in Heating Towers for power. This creates a self-sustaining loop: farm → process → spoilage → burn → power → farm.

    Tech Tree Highlights

    Tech Unlocks Priority
    Agricultural Science Biochamber, farming ⭐⭐⭐ First
    Bioflux Processing Bioflux → science ⭐⭐⭐
    Spidertron MK2 All-terrain vehicle ⭐⭐
    Overgrowth Soil Convert land to fertile soil ⭐
    Biolab Advanced bio-processing ⭐⭐

    Strategy & Tips

    1. Build in loops. Because everything spoils, a line that backs up means everything in it rots. Use circuits to monitor freshness and priority-split old items to spoilage disposal.
    2. Nutrients must be fresh. A nutrient that's 50% spoiled only gives half its fuel value. Refresh nutrient production constantly.
    3. Burn spoilage — don't let it pile up. Heating Towers turn waste into free power.
    4. Pentapods attack based on spore production. More production = more spores = more attacks. Defend heavily.
    5. Ship bioflux back to Nauvis. It's used for biter egg production and advanced module research. Set up interplanetary bioflux export.

    Common Traps

    Trap Why It's Bad Fix
    Building a linear production line Spoils when backed up Build loop-based production with priority splitters
    Not enough pentapod defense Constant attacks destroy base Tesla turrets + walls as soon as you land
    Ignoring nutrient freshness Machines stall with old fuel Build a nutrient freshness circuit monitor
    Stockpiling science packs They spoil before you use them Ship science immediately, don't stockpile
    No spoilage disposal Machines jammed with spoilage Heating Tower + circuit-controlled belt loops

    ❄️ Aquilo — The Ice World

    Aquilo is a frozen ocean with no land. Everything must be built on ice platforms. The extreme cold means machines stop working without heating — you need heat pipes everywhere.

    Unlock Requirements

    Requirement Detail
    Tech researched All 3 inner planet science packs (Metallurgic + Electromagnetic + Agricultural)
    Minimum cargo 3+ rockets (Aquilo has nothing — bring everything)
    Recommended Nuclear reactor (mandatory), heat pipes, heat exchangers, ice melting setup, fusion reactor tech

    Core Resources & New Materials

    Aquilo's only natural resource is ammoniacal solution — a liquid that can be extracted from special deposits. Everything else must be imported or made from ice.

    Resource Source Used For
    Ammoniacal Solution Extractors on deposits Liquid for cryogenic science
    Ice Ice platforms (meltable) Water for everything
    Lithium Brine Byproduct of ammoniacal processing Advanced batteries, science

    New materials unlocked:

    Material Recipe Purpose
    Lithium Plate Lithium brine → processing Fusion cells
    Cryogenic Science Pack Superconductor + Lithium + Processing unit Final space science
    Fusion Cell Lithium + fluorine krypton gas Fusion reactor fuel

    Recommended Landing Prep

    Essential items for first landing:

☐ EVERYTHING — no resources except ammoniacal solution
☐ Nuclear reactor + heat exchangers + turbines (CRITICAL)
☐ Heat pipes + heat diffusers (keep machines warm)
☐ Ice platform tiles (the only buildable surface)
☐ Rocket fuel for interplanetary resupply
☐ Superconductors, holmium, processing units
☐ Fusion reactor tech (for endgame power)
☐ Personal: Fusion reactor in armor, shields, legs

    Unique Buildings & Mechanics

    ❄️ Freezing Mechanic

    Every building on Aquilo has a minimum temperature requirement. If the building's temperature drops below threshold, it slows down and eventually stops.

    Building Class Min Temp How to Heat
    Assembler / Furnace -20°C Heat pipe adjacent
    Refinery / Chemical Plant -10°C Heat pipe adjacent
    Pump / Inserter -30°C Proximity to warm machines
    Offshore Pump -50°C Build on heated foundation

    💡 Key trick: Run heat pipes under every machine. A single heat pipe line can warm a row of 20+ machines. Every heat source (nuclear reactor, heating tower) generates heat.

    🧊 Ice Platform

    Aquilo has no ground. Ice platforms are the only buildable surface. Each tile costs ice (melted from the frozen ocean).

    Feature Detail
    Cost 5 ice per tile
    HP 50 (fragile — pentapods can destroy)
    Upgrade Reinforced ice platform (10x HP)
    Heat transmission Yes — heat pipes on platforms work normally

    🛰️ Orbital Platform Trunk

    Aquilo is the only planet where you can directly build ship parts using the orbital platform overhead. This is the key mechanic for building your endgame interplanetary ship.

    Tech Tree Highlights

    Tech Unlocks Priority
    Cryogenic Science Fusion reactor, advanced modules ⭐⭐⭐ Top priority
    Ice Platform Buildable ice surface ⭐⭐⭐ Essential
    Fusion Reactor Endgame power (120 MW per reactor) ⭐⭐⭐ Game-changer
    Railgun Interplanetary weapon system ⭐⭐
    Orbital Platform Expansion Larger space platforms ⭐⭐
    Cryogenic Plant Advanced ice processing ⭐

    Strategy & Tips

    1. Bring a nuclear reactor as your first cargo. Without one, you cannot heat anything, and you cannot build anything.
    2. Build heat pipe grids — run a main heat trunk under your base and branch to every machine.
    3. Ice is water. Melt ice for all fluid needs. You never need to pump water on Aquilo.
    4. Fusion reactor is Aquilo's endgame prize. One reactor provides 120 MW with no fuel consumption (fusion cells are cheap). Build it before leaving.
    5. Ship everything from Nauvis. Aquilo has almost no raw materials. You will run a constant interplanetary supply chain.

    Common Traps

    Trap Why It's Bad Fix
    Not enough heat pipes Machines freeze mid-production Run heat pipes everywhere before connecting machines
    Forgetting ice platforms No buildable surface Research and build ice platforms first
    Running out of power No sun (solar = 10% efficiency) Nuclear → Fusion as fast as possible
    Not importing enough Running out of basic materials Set up automated Nauvis→Aquilo rocket supply
    Ignoring pentapods They destroy ice platforms Turrets on heated foundations

    🚀 Interplanetary Logistics

    Space Age adds a full interplanetary logistics layer. Managing cargo between Nauvis and the four planets is a game in itself.

    🛰️ Space Platforms

    Space platforms are the backbone of interplanetary transport. They orbit Nauvis and can travel between planets.

    Feature Detail
    Size Start at 3×3, expand with platform tiles
    Build Via rocket from Nauvis surface
    Power Solar panels only (in space)
    Storage Cargo bays (upgradeable)
    Defense Turrets against asteroid impacts

    Building a platform:

    1. Launch rocket parts to orbit
    2. Build platform scaffolding on the orbital hub
    3. Add solar panels, cargo bays, and turrets
    4. Add engines for interplanetary travel

    🚀 Rocket Transport System

    Item Purpose
    Rocket Silo Launches cargo to orbit
    Cargo Rocket Ships items between planets
    Orbital Request Automate from Nauvis → planet
    Planetary Landing Items land at designated landing pad

    Automated supply chain:

    Nauvis Silo ← Request (circuit network)
         ↓ Launch
Space Platform ← Sort by destination
         ↓ Interplanetary
Planet Surface ← Landing Pad

    Cross-Planet Resource Shipment Priorities

    Import From Export To What Why
    Vulcanus Nauvis Calcite, Foundry recipes Infinite steel smelting
    Vulcanus All planets Tungsten, Big Drills Mining efficiency
    Fulgora Nauvis Holmium, Superconductors Advanced modules
    Fulgora Aquilo Accumulators Energy storage
    Gleba Nauvis Bioflux Productivity modules, biolab
    Gleba Aquilo Nutrients Biochamber fuel
    Aquilo All planets Fusion cells, Cryogenic science Endgame power

    Orbital Defense — Asteroid Management

    As your platform travels between planets, it passes through asteroid belts. Asteroids damage your platform and must be destroyed or deflected.

    Asteroid Type Threat Defense
    Small asteroids Damage tiles Gun turrets
    Medium asteroids Damage structures Rocket turrets
    Large asteroids Critical damage Railgun (late game)

    💡 Key trick: Collect asteroid chunks with collectors and crush them into iron ore, coal, and water. A well-designed platform is self-sustaining — no resupply needed.
