    💝 Relationships

    Stardew Valley Relationships Guide

    Building friendships with Pelican Town's residents unlocks recipes, cutscenes, items in the mail, and ultimately marriage. This guide covers every NPC's preferences and the most efficient way to max out friendship.

    Friendship Points System

    How Friendship Works

    Action Points Gained Daily Limit
    Talking to NPC +20 Once per day
    Giving Loved Gift +80 Twice per week
    Giving Liked Gift +45 Twice per week
    Giving Neutral Gift +20 Twice per week
    Giving Disliked Gift -20 Twice per week
    Giving Hated Gift -40 Twice per week
    Heart Event completion +50-200 One-time
    Birthday Gift 8x multiplier Once per year
    Winter Feast gift 5x multiplier Once per year

    Hearts required: 2 hearts = 250 points, 8 hearts = 1,000 points, 10 hearts (marriage candidate) = 1,250 points, 14 hearts (after marriage) = 1,750 points.

    Decay mechanic: If you don't talk to an NPC for a day, you lose 2 points (10 points if you haven't spoken in a week). A Bouquet stops decay for marriage candidates at 8+ hearts.

    Universal Gifts

    Universal Loves (+80 points)

    Gift Where to Get Notes
    Prismatic Shard Skull Cavern, Omni Geodes Rare, save for Galaxy Sword first
    Rabbit's Foot Rabbits (mature), Serpents (rare) Easy to produce with a deluxe coop
    Golden Pumpkin Spirit's Eve Festival (Fall 27) One per year
    Pearl Night Market, Blobfish Pond Limited
    Magic Rock Candy Museum (artifact, 90 donations) Extremely rare

    Universal Hates (-40 points)

    Gift Notes
    Clay Loot from artifact spots
    Pufferfish (most NPCs) Only liked by Abigail
    Poppy Only liked by Penny
    Quartz Cheap mineral, most hate it
    Sap Tree tapping byproduct
    Seaweed Fishing byproduct

    Spouse Duties

    Spouses will randomly: - Water crops (all garden plots) - Feed animals (all barn/coop animals) - Repair fences - Fill pet water bowl - Give you a gift in the morning - Cook breakfast (gives random food item, energy boost)

    Best spouse for gameplay: Leah and Penny water crops most frequently. Best spouse for gifts: Harvey gives Complete Breakfast (+200 energy). Best for personality: Abigail and Sebastian have the most interesting dialogue.

    Pro Relationship Tips

    1. Birthday strategy: Give every NPC their loved gift on their birthday (8x multiplier = +640 points = 2-3 hearts at once). Keep a chest with birthday gifts organized by date.
    2. Weekly schedule: Talk to every NPC at least once per week. Rotate through town in a circuit: Farm → Marnie's → Pierre's → River → Beach → Mountain.
    3. Bouquet management: You can date ALL 12 marriage candidates simultaneously with no penalty. The only consequence is a special cutscene if you have a Rabbit's Foot when walking into the Saloon with all candidates above 10 hearts.
    4. Stardrop tea: After reaching 5 hearts with certain NPCs, they mail you recipes. Complete all heart events for the Stardrop at the Flower Dance (spouse dance), and the Stardrop from the Old Master Cannoli (after maxing out friendship).
    5. Talk + gift only: Heart events trigger only between certain hours at specific locations. Check the Stardew Wiki if you're stuck at a heart level.
    6. Seasonal forage gifts: Daffodils (Spring) are Loved by Sandy. Sweet Peas (Summer) are Liked by most. Crocus (Winter) is Loved by Sandy. Use seasonal forage for efficient bulk gifting.
    7. Divorce: You can divorce at Mayor Lewis's house for 50,000g. All memories are erased. If you want to re-marry the same NPC, visit the Witch's Hut for a Dark Shrine of Memory.
    8. Krobus roommate: If you don't want to marry anyone, give Krobus a Void Ghost Pendant. He moves in as a roommate (same benefits as marriage, no divorce option).
